The Eternal Reign of the Lord

1The Lord reigns, He is clothed with majesty;
The Lord is clothed,
He has girded Himself with strength.
Surely the world is established, so that it cannot be [a]moved.
2Your throne is established from of old;
You are from everlasting.

3The floods have [b]lifted up, O Lord,
The floods have lifted up their voice;
The floods lift up their waves.
4The Lord on high is mightier
Than the noise of many waters,
Than the mighty waves of the sea.

5Your testimonies are very sure;
Holiness adorns Your house,
O Lord, [c]forever.

Footnotes:

  1. Psalm 93:1 shaken
  2. Psalm 93:3 raised up
  3. Psalm 93:5 Lit. for length of days
