Strong's Concordance
patralóas: murderer of fathers.
Original Word: πατρολῴας, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: patralóas
Phonetic Spelling: (pat-ral-o'-as)
Definition: murderer of fathers
Usage: a patricide, a murderer of his father.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
variant reading for patrolóas, q.v.
