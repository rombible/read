Strong's Concordance
metalémpsis: taking, receiving, sharing
Original Word: μετάληψις, εως, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: metalémpsis
Phonetic Spelling: (met-al'-ape-sis)
Definition: taking, receiving, sharing
Usage: participation, sharing in, receiving.
HELPS Word-studies

Cognate: 3336 metálēmpsis – an "aggressive" receiving which brings a certain result ("after-effect," used only in 1 Tim 4:3). See 3335 (metalambanō).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3336: μετάληψις

μετάληψις (L T Tr WH μετάλημψις (see Mu)), μεταληψεως, ἡ (μεταλαμβάνω), a taking, participation, (Plato, Plutarch, others): of the use of food, εἰς μετάληψιν, to be taken or received, 1 Timothy 4:3.
