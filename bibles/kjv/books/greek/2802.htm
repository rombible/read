Klauda: Clauda, an island near Crete
Original Word: Κλαῦδη, ης, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: Klauda
Phonetic Spelling: (klow'-day)
Definition: Clauda, an island near Crete
Usage: Cauda, Clauda, an island twenty-three miles south of the western end of Crete.
