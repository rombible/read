Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 688: Ἀραβία

Ἀραβία, Ἀραβίας, ἡ (from Herodotus down), Arabia, a well-known peninsula of Asia, lying toward Africa, and bounded by Egypt, Palestine, Syria, Mesopotamia, Babylonia, the Gulf of Arabia, the Persian Gulf, the Red Sea (and the Ocean): Galatians 1:17; Galatians 4:25.
