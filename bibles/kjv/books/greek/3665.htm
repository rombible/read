homoiotés: likeness, in like manner
Original Word: ὁμοιότης, τητος, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: homoiotés
Phonetic Spelling: (hom-oy-ot'-ace)

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3665: ὁμοιότης

ὁμοιότης, ὁμοιοτητος, ἡ (ὅμοιος), likeness: καθ' ὁμοιότητα, in like manner, Hebrews 4:15 (cf. Winer's Grammar, 143 (136)); κατά τήν ὁμοιότητα (Μελχισέδεκ), after the likeness, Hebrews 7:15. (Genesis 1:11; 4 Macc. 15:4 (3); Plato, Aristotle, Isocr, Polybius, Philo, Plutarch.)
