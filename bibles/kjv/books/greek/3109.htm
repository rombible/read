Makedonia: Macedonia, a region of Greece

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3109: Μακεδονία

Μακεδονία, Μακεδονίας, ἡ (on use of article with cf. Winers Grammar, § 18, 5 a. c.), Macedonia, a country bounded on the south by Thessaly and Epirus, on the east by Thrace and the Aegean Sea, on the west by Illyria, and on the north by Dardania and Moesia (cf. B. D. (especially American edition)): Acts 16:9f, 12; Acts 18:5; Acts 19:21; Acts 20:1, 3; Romans 15:26; 1 Corinthians 16:5; 2 Corinthians 1:16; 2 Corinthians 2:13; 2 Corinthians 7:5; 2 Corinthians 8:1; 2 Corinthians 11:9: Philippians 4:15; 1 Thessalonians 1:7; 1 Thessalonians 4:10; 1 Timothy 1:3.
