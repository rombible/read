Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 870: ἀφόβως

ἀφόβως, adverb (φόβος), without fear, boldly: Luke 1:74; Philippians 1:14; 1 Corinthians 16:10; Jude 1:12. (From Xenophon down.)
