Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3818: Πακατιανη

Πακατιανη, Πακατιανῆς, ἡ, Pacatiana (Phrygia). In the fourth century after Christ, Phrygia was divided into Phrygia Salutaris and Phrygia Pacatiana (later, Capatiana); Laodicea was the metropolis of the latter: (in the spurious subscription). (Cf. Forbiger, Hndbch. d. alt. Geogr. 2te Ausg. ii., 338, 347f; Lightfoot's Commentary on Colossians, Introduction, (especially, pp. 19, 69f).)
