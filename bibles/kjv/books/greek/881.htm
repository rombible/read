Achaz: Ahaz, a king of Judah
Original Word: Ἄχαζ, ὁ

Phonetic Spelling: (akh-adz')

STRONGS NT 881: Ἀχάζ

Ἀχάζ (WH Ἀχας), ὁ (so the Sept. for אָחָז possessing, possessor; in Josephus, Ἀχαζης, Ἀχου, ὁ), Ahaz, king of Judah, (from circa to circa ; cf. B. D. under the word ), (2 Kings 16:1ff; 2 Chronicles 28:16ff; Isaiah 7:1ff): Matthew 1:9.
