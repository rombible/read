Strong's Concordance
Boos: Boaz, an Israelite
Original Word: Βοόζ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Boos
Phonetic Spelling: (bo-oz')
Definition: Boaz, an Israelite
Usage: Boaz, son of Salmon and Rahab, husband of Ruth, father of Obed.
