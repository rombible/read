Strong's Concordance
Chouzas: Chuza
Original Word: Χουζᾶς, ᾶ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Chouzas
Phonetic Spelling: (khood-zas')
Definition: Chuza, an officer of Herod
Usage: Chuza, a steward of Herod Antipas.
Thayer's Greek Lexicon

Χουζᾶς, Χουζᾶ (Tdf. Proleg., p. 104; Buttmann, 20 (18)), ὁ, Chuzas (A. V. (less correctly) Chusa), the steward of Herod Antipas: Luke 8:3.
