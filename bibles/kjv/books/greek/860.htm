Strong's Concordance
haphé: a joint
Original Word: ἁφή, ῆς, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: haphé
Phonetic Spelling: (haf-ay')
Definition: a joint
Usage: a band, fastening (hence, possibly: a ligament), joint.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from haptó
Definition

NASB Translation

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 860: ἁφή

ἁφή, ἁφῆς, ἡ (ἅπτω to fasten together, to fit) (Vu]g.junctura (andnexus)), bond, connection (A. V. joint (see especially Lightfoot on Col. as below)): Ephesians 4:16; Colossians 2:19. (Plutarch, Anton c. 27.)
