Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5447: φυσικῶς

φυσικῶς, adverb, in a natural manner, by nature, under the guidance of nature: by the aid of the bodily senses, Jude 1:10. ((Aristotle, Philo, others.))
