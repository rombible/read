STRONGS NT 535: ἀπαρτισμός

ἀπαρτισμός, ἀπαρτισμου, ὁ (ἀπαρτίζω to finish, complete), completion: Luke 14:28. Found besides only in Dionysius Halicarnassus, de comp. verb. c. 24; (Apollonius Dyscolus, de adv., p. 532, 7, others; cf. Winer's Grammar, p. 24).
