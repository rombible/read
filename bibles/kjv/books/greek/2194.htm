Strong's Concordance
Zaboulón: Zebulun, a son of Jacob and one of the Isr. tribes
Original Word: Ζαβουλών, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Zaboulón
Phonetic Spelling: (dzab-oo-lone')
Definition: Zebulun, a son of Jacob and one of the Israelite tribes
Usage: Zebulon, one of the sons of Jacob, and founder of one of the twelve tribes.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2194: Ζαβουλών

Ζαβουλών, ὁ, indeclinable (זֲבֻלון (but on the Hebrew form see B. D.) habitation, dwelling, Genesis 30:20), Vulg.Zabulon; Zebulun, the tenth son of Jacob; by metonymy, the tribe of Zebulun: Matthew 4:13, 15; Revelation 7:8.
