Strong's Concordance
euthutés: uprightness
Original Word: εὐθύτης, ητος, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: euthutés
Phonetic Spelling: (yoo-thoo'-tace)
Definition: uprightness
Usage: straightness, uprightness.
HELPS Word-studies

Cognate: 2118 euthýtēs – properly, straight (upright), as with complete justice – literally "without deviation" (unnecessary delay). 2118 /euthýtēs ("straight without deviation") is used only in Heb 1:8. See 2117 (euthys).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2118: εὐθύτης

εὐθύτης, εὐθύτητος, ἡ (from the adjective εὐθύς), rectitude, uprightness: tropically, ῤάβδος εὐθύτητος, an impartial and righteous government, Hebrews 1:8 from Psalm 44:7 ().
