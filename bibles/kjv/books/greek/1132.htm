γυμνότης, γυμνότητός, ἡ (γυμνός), nakedness: of the body, Revelation 3:18 (see αἰσχύνη, 3); used of want of clothing, Romans 8:35; 2 Corinthians 11:27. (Deuteronomy 28:48; Antoninus 11, 27.)
