Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1216: Δημήτριος

Δημήτριος, Δημετριου, ὁ, Demetrius;

1. a silversmith of Ephesus, a heathen: Acts 19:24, 38.

2. a certain Christian: 3 John 1:12.
