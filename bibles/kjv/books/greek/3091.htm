STRONGS NT 3091: Λώτ

Λώτ, ὁ (לוט, a covering, a veil) (indeclinable; cf. B. D.), Lot, the son of Haran the brother of Abraham (Genesis 11:27, 31; Genesis 12:4ff; 13:1ff; 14:12ff; 19:1ff): Luke 17:28f, 32: 2 Peter 2:7.
