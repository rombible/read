Strong's Concordance
Bariésous: "son of Joshua," Bar-Jesus, a false prophet
Original Word: Βαρϊησοῦς, οῦ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Bariésous
Phonetic Spelling: (bar-ee-ay-sooce')
Definition: "son of Joshua", Bar-Jesus, a false prophet
Usage: Bar-Jesus, the name of the magician and false prophet at Paphos in Cyprus; he is also called Elymas.
