Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4799: σύγχυσις

σύγχυσις, συγχύσεως, ἡ (συγχέω) (from Euripides, Thucydides, Plato down), confusion, disturbance: of riotous persons, Acts 19:29 (1 Samuel 5:11).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
confusion.

From sugcheo; commixture, i.e. (figuratively) riotous disturbance -- confusion.
