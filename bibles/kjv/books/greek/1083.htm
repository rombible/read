gennésis: birth.
Original Word: γέννησις, εως, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: gennésis
Phonetic Spelling: (ghen'-nay-sis)
Definition: birth
Usage: nativity, birth.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
variant reading for genesis, q.v.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1083: γέννησις

γέννησις, γεννησεως, ἡ (γεννάω), a begetting, engendering (often so in Plato); nativity, birth: Rec. in Matthew 1:18 and Luke 1:14; see γένεσις, 2.
