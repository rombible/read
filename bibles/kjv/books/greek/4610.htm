Strong's Concordance
Silouanos: "sylvan," Silvanus, alt. form of the name Silas
Original Word: Σιλουανός, οῦ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Silouanos
Phonetic Spelling: (sil-oo-an-os')
Definition: "sylvan", Silvanus, alt. form of the name Silas
Usage: Silvanus, a Roman citizen and a helper of Paul.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4610: Σιλουανός

Σιλουανός, Σιλουανοῦ, ὁ, Silvanus, the same man who in Acts is called Σιλᾶς (which see): 2 Corinthians 1:19; 1 Thessalonians 1:1; 2 Thessalonians 1:1; 1 Peter 5:12. (Not infrequent written in the manuscripts Σιλβανος, Silbanus; cf. Tdf. on Iliad chapters)
