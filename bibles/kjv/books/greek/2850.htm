kolakeia: flattery

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2850: κολακεία

κολακεία (T WH κολακια (see Iota)), κολακείας, ἡ (κολεκεύω), flattery: λόγος κολακείας, flattering discourse, 1 Thessalonians 2:5. (Plato, Demosthenes, Theophrastus, Josephus, Herodian, others.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
