STRONGS NT 4562: Σαρούχ

Σαρούχ (Rec.), more correctly (G L T Tr WH) Σερούχ (שְׂרוּג equivalent to שָׂרִג, 'vine-shoot'), ὁ, Serug (so R. V.; but A. V. in the N. T. Saruch), the name of a man (Genesis 11:20f etc.): Luke 3:35.
