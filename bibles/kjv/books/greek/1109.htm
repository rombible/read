γνώστης, γνωστου, ὁ (a knower), an expert; a connoisseur: Acts 26:3. (Plutarch, Flam c. 4; Θεός ὁ τῶν κρύπτων γνώστης, Hist. Susanna, verse 42; of those who divine the future, 1 Samuel 28:3, 9, etc.)
