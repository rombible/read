Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3575: Νῶε

Νῶε (Νωεος, Νοωυ, in Josephus (Antiquities 1, 3, 1ff)), ὁ (נֹחַ, rest), Noah, the second father of the human race: Matthew 24:37; Luke 3:36; Luke 17:26; Hebrews 11:7; 1 Peter 3:20; 2 Peter 2:5.
