aphanismos: a vanishing

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 854: ἀφανισμός

ἀφανισμός, ἀφανισμοῦ, ὁ (ἀφανίζω, which see), disappearance; destruction: Hebrews 8:13. (Theophrastus, Polybius, Diodorus, Plutarch, Lucian, others; often in the Sept., particularly for שַׁמָּה and שְׁמָמָה.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
vanish away.

From aphanizo; disappearance, i.e. (figuratively) abrogation -- vanish away.
