Strong's Concordance
porphuropólis: a seller of purple fabrics
Original Word: πορφυρόπωλις, ιδος, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: porphuropólis
Phonetic Spelling: (por-foo-rop'-o-lis)
Definition: a seller of purple fabrics
Usage: a female seller of purple cloth.
HELPS Word-studies

Cognate: 4211 porphyrópōlis – a woman dealing (selling) purple cloth. See 4209 (porphýra).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4211: πορφυρόπωλις

πορφυρόπωλις, πορφυροπωλιδος, ἡ (πορφύρα and πωλέω), a female seller of purple or of fabrics dyed in purple (Vulg.purpuraria): Acts 16:14. (Photius, Suidas, others.)
