Strong's Concordance
oinopotés: a wine drinker
Original Word: οἰνοπότης, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: oinopotés
Phonetic Spelling: (oy-nop-ot'-ace)
Definition: a wine drinker
Usage: an excessive wine-drinker.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3630: οἰνοπότης

οἰνοπότης, ὀινοποτου, ὁ (οἶνος, and πότης a drinker), a winebibber, given to wine: Matthew 11:19; Luke 7:34. (Proverbs 23:20; Polybius 20, 8, 2; Anacreon () fragment 98; Anthol. 7, 28, 2.)
