STRONGS NT 1113: γογγυστής

γογγυστής, γογγυστου, ὁ, a murmurer (Vulg., Augustine,murmurator), one who discontentedly complains (against God; for μεμψίμοιροι is added): Jude 1:16. (Proverbs 26:21 Theod., Symm.; Graecus Venetus)
