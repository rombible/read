Strong's Concordance
Aminadab: Amminadab, an Israelite ancestor of Christ
Original Word: Ἀμιναδάβ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Aminadab
Phonetic Spelling: (am-ee-nad-ab')
Definition: Amminadab, an Israelite ancestor of Christ
Usage: Amminadab, son of Ram and father of Nahshon, one of the ancestors of Jesus.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 284: Ἀμιναδαβ

Ἀμιναδαβ, ὁ, עַמִּינָדָב; (servant of the prince (others, my people are noble; but cf. B. D. under the word)) (A. V. Aminadab), the proper name of one of the ancestors of Christ (1 Chronicles 2:10 (A. V. Amminadab)): Matthew 1:4; Luke 3:33 (not WH. See B. D. under the word).
