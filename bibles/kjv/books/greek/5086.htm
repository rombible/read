STRONGS NT 5086: Τιβέριος

Τιβέριος, Τιβερίου, ὁ, Tiberius, the Roman emperor (from (Aug. 19) A.D. 14 to (March 16) A.D. 37) in whose reign Christ was crucified: Luke 3:1.
