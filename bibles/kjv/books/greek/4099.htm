Strong's Concordance
Pisidia: Pisidia, a region of Asia Minor
Original Word: Πισιδία, ας, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: Pisidia
Phonetic Spelling: (pis-id-ee'-ah)
Definition: Pisidia, a region of Asia Minor
Usage: Pisidia, a country of Asia Minor, being the south-western part of the Roman province Galatia.
