Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3776: οὐσία

οὐσία, οὐσίας, ἡ (from ὤν, οὖσα, ὄν, the participle of εἰμί), what one has, i. e. property, possessions, estate (A. V. substance): Luke 15:12f. (Tobit 14:13; Herodotus 1, 92; Xenophon, Plato, Attic orators, others.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
goods, property

From the feminine of on; substance, i.e. Property (possessions) -- goods, substance.
