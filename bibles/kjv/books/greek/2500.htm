Strong's Concordance
Iósés: Joses, an Israelite name
Original Word: Ἰωσῆς, ῆτος, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Iósés
Phonetic Spelling: (ee-o-sace')
Definition: Joses, an Israelite name
Usage: (Hebrew), Joses (a) son of Eliezer, (b) son of Mary, half-brother of Jesus, (c) surnamed Barnabas (also called Joseph).
