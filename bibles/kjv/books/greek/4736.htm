Strong's Concordance
Stephanos: "crown," Stephen, the first Christian martyr
Original Word: Στέφανος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Stephanos
Phonetic Spelling: (stef'-an-os)
Definition: "crown", Stephen, the first Christian martyr
Usage: Stephen, one of the seven original deacons at Jerusalem, and the first martyr.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4736: Στέφανος

Στέφανος, Στεφάνου, ὁ, Stephen, one of the seven 'deacons' of the church at Jerusalem who was stoned to death by the Jews: Acts 6:5, 8; Acts 7:59; Acts 8:2; Acts 11:19; Acts 22:20.
