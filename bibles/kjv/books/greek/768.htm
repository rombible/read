STRONGS NT 768: Ασηρ

Ασηρ, ὁ, an indeclinable Hebrew proper name (אָשֵׁר (i. e. happy, Genesis 30:13)) (in Josephus, Ἀσηρος, Ἀσηρου, ὁ), Asher, the eighth son of the patriarch Jacob: Luke 2:36; Revelation 7:6.
