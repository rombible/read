Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 136: αἶνος

αἶνος, , ὁ (often used by the Greek poets);

1. a saying, a proverb.

2. praise, laudatory discourse: Matthew 21:16 (Psalm 8:3); Luke 18:43.
