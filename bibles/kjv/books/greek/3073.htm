Lukia: Lycia, a region of Asia Minor
Original Word: Λυκία, ας, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: Lukia
Phonetic Spelling: (loo-kee'-ah)
Definition: Lycia, a region of Asia Minor
