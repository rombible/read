Strong's Concordance
himatismos: clothing
Original Word: ἱματισμός, οῦ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: himatismos
Phonetic Spelling: (him-at-is-mos')
Definition: clothing
Usage: a collective word: raiment, clothing.

Definition
clothing
