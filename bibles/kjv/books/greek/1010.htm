Strong's Concordance
bouleutés: a councilor
Original Word: βουλευτής, οῦ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: bouleutés
Phonetic Spelling: (bool-yoo-tace')
Definition: a councilor
Usage: a member of a city council, in NT of the Sanhedrin at Jerusalem.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1010: βουλευτής

βουλευτής, βουλευτου, ὁ, a councillor, senator, (buleuta, Pliny, epistles): first in Homer, Iliad 6, 114; of a member of the Sanhedrin, Mark 15:43; Luke 23:50. (Job 3:14; Job 12:17.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
counselor.

From bouleuo; an adviser, i.e. (specially) a councillor or member of the Jewish Sanhedrin -- counsellor.
