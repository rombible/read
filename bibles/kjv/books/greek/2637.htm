Strong's Concordance
katalalos: a defamer
Original Word: κατάλαλος, ον
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: katalalos
Phonetic Spelling: (kat-al'-al-os)
Definition: a defamer
Usage: slanderous, back-biting; subst: a railer, defamer.

Cognate: 2637 katálalos – slanderous. See 2635 (katalaleō).
