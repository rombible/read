Strong's Concordance
kopazó: to grow weary
Original Word: κοπάζω
Part of Speech: Verb
Transliteration: kopazó
Phonetic Spelling: (kop-ad'-zo)
Definition: to grow weary
Usage: I abate, cease raging, am stilled.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from kopos
Definition
to grow weary
NASB Translation
died down (1), stopped (2).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2869: κοπάζω

κοπάζω: 1 aorist ἐκόπασα; (κόπος); properly, to grow weary or tired; hence to cease from violence, cease raging: ὁ ἄνεμος (Herodotus 7, 191), Matthew 14:32; Mark 4:39; Mark 6:51. (Genesis 8:1; Jonah 1:11f; (cf. especially Philo, somn. 2:35).)
