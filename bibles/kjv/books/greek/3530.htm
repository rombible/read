Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3530: Νικόδημος

Νικόδημος (νίκη and δῆμος (i. e. 'conqueror of the people')). Νικοδημου, ὁ, Nicodemus (rabbinical Hebrew נַקְדִימון), a member of the Sanhedrin who took the part of Jesus: John 3:1, 4, 9; John 7:50; John 19:39.
