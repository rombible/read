Strong's Concordance
entaphiasmos: preparation for burial
Original Word: ἐνταφιασμός, οῦ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: entaphiasmos
Phonetic Spelling: (en-taf-ee-as-mos')
Definition: preparation for burial
Usage: embalming, preparation of a body for burial.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from entaphiazó
Definition
preparation for burial
NASB Translation
burial (2).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1780: ἐνταφιασμός

ἐνταφιασμός, ἐνταφιασμοῦ, ὁ (ἐνταφιάζω, which see), preparation of a body for burial: Mark 14:8; John 12:7. (Schol. ad Euripides, Phoen. 1654; (Schol. ad Aristophanes, Plutarch, 1009).)
