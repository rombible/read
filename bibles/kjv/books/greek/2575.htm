Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2575: κάμινος

κάμινος, καμίνου, ὁ, ἡ (Homer, epistle 14, 2 etc., Herodotus on), a furnace (either for smelting, Xenophon, vectig. 4, 49, or for burning earthen ware, or baking bread, Genesis 19:28; Exodus 19:18; Jeremiah 11:4; Daniel 3:6): Matthew 13:42, 50; Revelation 1:15; Revelation 9:2.
