Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 783: ἀσπασμός

ἀσπασμός, ἀσπασμοῦ, ὁ (ἀσπάζομαι), a salutation — either oral: Matthew 23:7; Mark 12:38; Luke 1:29, 41, 44; Luke 11:43; Luke 20:46; or written: 1 Corinthians 16:21; Colossians 4:18; 2 Thessalonians 3:17. (From Theognis down.)
