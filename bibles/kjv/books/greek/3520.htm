Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3520: νῆσος

νῆσος, νήσου, ὁ (νεώ, to swim, properly, 'floating land'), an island: Acts 13:6; Acts 27:26; Acts 28:1, 7, 9, 11; Revelation 1:9; Revelation 6:14; Revelation 16:20. (the Sept. for אִי; (from Homer down).)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance

Probably from the base of naus; an island -- island, isle.
