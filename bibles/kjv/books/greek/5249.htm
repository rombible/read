HELPS Word-studies

5249 hyperperissṓs (from 5228 /hypér, "beyond" and 4057 /perissṓs, "exceeding, abundant") – properly, abounding (literally, "going beyond expected measure)"; exceedingly, "overflowing" (used only in Mk 7:37).
