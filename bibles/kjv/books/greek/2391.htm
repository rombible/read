Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2391: Ιαρεδ

Ιαρεδ (T WH Ἰάρετ, Lachmann Ἰαρεθ; (on the accent in manuscripts see Tdf. Proleg., p. 103)), ὁ (Heb. יֶרֶד descent), Jared, indeclinable proper name (Ἰαραδης (Ἰαρεδες, Bekker edition) in Josephus, Antiquities 1, 2, 2), the father of Enoch (Genesis 5:15, 18; 1 Chronicles 1:2 (here A. V. Jered)): Luke 3:37.
