STRONGS NT 2953: Κύπριος

Κύπριος, Κυπριου, ὁ, a Cyprian or Cypriote, i. e. a native or an inhabitant of Cyprus: Acts 4:36; Acts 11:20; Acts 21:16 (2 Macc. 4:29). ((Herodotus, others.))
