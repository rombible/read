φαντασία, φαντασίας, ἡ, show, showy appearance, display, pomp: Acts 25:23. (Polybius 15, 25, 5, etc.; (Diodorus 12, 83); others.)

From a derivative of phantazo; (properly abstract) a (vain) show ("fantasy") -- pomp.
