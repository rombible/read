Strong's Concordance
Bartholomaios: "son of Tolmai," Bartholomew, one of the twelve apostles
Original Word: Βαρθολομαῖος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Bartholomaios
Phonetic Spelling: (bar-thol-om-ah'-yos)
Definition: "son of Tolmai", Bartholomew, one of the twelve apostles
Usage: Bartholomew, surname of Nathanael, one of the twelve apostles of Jesus.
HELPS Word-studies

918 Bartholomaíos – Bartholomew, probably a surname for Nathanael (literally the "son of Tolmai," cf. Mt 10:3; Jn 1:45ff). That is, Bartholomew's "first name" was apparently "Nathanael."
