Strong's Concordance
Zeus: Zeus, the greatest of the pagan Gk. gods
Original Word: Ζεύς, Διός, Δία (acc.), ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Zeus
Phonetic Spelling: (dzyooce)
Definition: Zeus, the greatest of the pagan Gk. gods
Usage: Zeus, the Greek god of the sky in all its manifestations, corresponding to the Roman Jupiter and to the leading god of the native Lycaonians.
