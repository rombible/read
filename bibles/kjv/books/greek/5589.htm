Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5589: ψιχίον

ψιχίον, ψιχιου, τό (diminutive of ψίξ, ψιχος, ἡ, a morsel), a little morsel, a crumb (of bread or meat): Matthew 15:27; Mark 7:28; Luke 16:21 (T WH omit; L Tr brackets τῶν ψιχίων). (Not found in Greek authors (cf. Winer's Grammar, 24; 96 (91)).)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
crumb.

Diminutive from a derivative of the base of psallo (meaning a crumb); a little bit or morsel -- crumb.
