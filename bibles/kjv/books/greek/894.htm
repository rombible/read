Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 894: ἄψινθος

ἄψινθος, ἀψινθου, ἡ, wormwood, Absinthe: Revelation 8:11; ἄψινθος ibid. is given as a proper name to the star which fell into the waters and made them bitter.
