Zorobabel: Zerubbabel, an Israelite
Original Word: Ζοροβάβελ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Zorobabel
Phonetic Spelling: (dzor-ob-ab'-el)
Definition: Zerubbabel, an Israelite
Usage: Zerubbabel, son of Shealtiel (Salathiel) and father of Abiud and Rhesa.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2216: Ζοροβαβέλ

Ζοροβαβέλ, in Josephus, Ζοροβαβηλος, Ζοροβαβελου, ὁ, (זְרֻבָּבֶל, i. e. either for זְרוּבָבֶל dispersed in Babylonia or for בָּבֶל זְרוּעַ begotten in Babylonia), Zerubbabel, Vulg.Zorobabel, a descendant of David, the leader of the first colony of the Jews on their return from the Babylonian exile: Matthew 1:12; Luke 3:27.
