Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4783: συγκατάθεσις

συγκατάθεσις (T WH συνκαταθεσις (cf. σύν, II. at the end)), συγκαταθεσεως, ἡ (συγκατατίθημι, which see), properly, a putting together or joint deposit (of votes); hence, approval, assent, agreement, (Cicero, acad. 2, 12, 37 adsensio atque adprobatio): 2 Corinthians 6:16. (Polybius, Dionysius Halicarnassus, Plutarch, others.)
