Strong's Concordance
belos: a missile, dart, javelin
Original Word: βέλος, ους, τό
Part of Speech: Noun, Neuter
Transliteration: belos
Phonetic Spelling: (bel'-os)
Definition: a needle
Usage: a missile, dart, javelin, arrow.
