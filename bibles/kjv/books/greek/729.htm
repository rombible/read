Strong's Concordance
arraphos: without seam, of a single piece
Original Word: ἄραφος, ον
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: arraphos
Phonetic Spelling: (ar'-hhraf-os)
Definition: without seam, of a single piece
Usage: not sewed, seamless.
