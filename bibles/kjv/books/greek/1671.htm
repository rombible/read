Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1671: ἑλλάς

ἑλλάς, Ἑλλάδος, ἡ, Greece i. e. Greece proper, as opposed to Macedonia, equivalent to Ἀχαΐα (which see) in the time of the Romans: Acts 20:2 (cf. Wetstein at the passage; Meyer on Acts 18:12).
