Strong's Concordance
agenealogétos: without genealogy
Original Word: ἀγενεαλόγητος, ον
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: agenealogétos
Phonetic Spelling: (ag-en-eh-al-og'-ay-tos)
Definition: without genealogy
Usage: of unrecorded genealogy, whose descent cannot be traced.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 35: ἀγενεαλόγητος

ἀγενεαλόγητος, , ὁ (γενεαλογέω), of whose descent there is no account (in the O. T.) (R. V. without genealogy): Hebrews 7:3 (Hebrews 7:6 μή γενεαλογούμενος). Nowhere found in secular authors.
