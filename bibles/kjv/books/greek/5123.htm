Strong's Concordance
toutesti: that is to say.
Original Word: τουτέστι
Transliteration: toutesti
Phonetic Spelling: (toot-es'-tee)
Definition: that is to say
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
variant reading, comb. of touto and esti, q.v.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5123: τουτεστι

τουτεστι (cf. Winers Grammar, p. 45; Buttmann, 11 (10)) for τουτ' ἐστι, and this for τοῦτο ἐστι, see εἰμί, II. 3. τράγος, τράγου, ὁ, from Homer down, a he-goat: plural, Hebrews 9:12f, 19; Hebrews 10:4.
