apokleió: to shut fast or completely

ἀποκλείω: 1 aorist ἀπεκλεισα; to shut up: τήν θύραν, Luke 13:25. (Genesis 19:10; 2 Samuel 13:17f; often in Herodotus; in Attic prose writings from Thucydides down.)
