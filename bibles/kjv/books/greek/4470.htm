Strong's Concordance
rhakos: a rag
Original Word: ῥάκος, ους, τό
Part of Speech: Noun, Neuter
Transliteration: rhakos
Phonetic Spelling: (hrak'-os)
Definition: a rag
Usage: a piece of cloth, a remnant torn off.
