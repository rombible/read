STRONGS NT 10: Ἀβιούδ

Ἀβιούδ, ὁ, indeclinable proper name, אֲבִיהוּד (father of the Jews (others, of glory)), Abiud, son of Zorobabel or Zerubbabel: Matthew 1:13.
