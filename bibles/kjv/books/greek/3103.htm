Strong's Concordance
Mathousala: Methuselah
Original Word: Μαθουσάλα, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Mathousala
Phonetic Spelling: (math-oo-sal'-ah)
Definition: Matthat, an Israelite
Usage: (Hebrew) Methuselah, son of Enoch and father of Lamech.
Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3103: Μαθουσαλά

Μαθουσαλά, T WH Μαθουσαλά (cf. Tdf. Proleg., p. 103), ὁ (מְתוּשֶׁלַח, man of a dart, from מְתוּ, construct form of the unused מַת, a man, and שֶׁלַח a dart (cf. B. D. under the word)), Methuselah, the son of Enoch and grandfather of Noah (Genesis 5:21): Luke 3:37.
