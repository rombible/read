proschusis: a pouring upon
Original Word: πρόσχυσις, εως, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: proschusis
Phonetic Spelling: (pros'-khoo-sis)
Definition: a pouring upon
Usage: a pouring upon, an effusion, a sprinkling.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4378: πρόσχυσις

πρόσχυσις, προσχυσεως, ἡ (προσχέω to pour on), a pouring or sprinkling upon, affusion: τοῦ αἵματος, Hebrews 11:28. (Ecclesiastical writings (e. g. Justin Martyr, Apology 2, 12, p. 50 d.).)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
sprinkling.

From a comparative of pros and cheo (to pour); a shedding forth, i.e. Affusion -- sprinkling.
