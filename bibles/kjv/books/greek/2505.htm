Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2505: καθά

καθά, adverb for καθ' ἅ, according as, just as: Matthew 27:10. (Xenophon, Polybius, Diodorus, others; O. T. Apocrypha; the Sept. for כַּאֲשֶׁר, Genesis 7:9, 16, etc., and for כְּ, Genesis 19:8; Exodus 12:35, etc.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
as.

From kata and the neuter plural of hos; according to which things, i.e. Just as -- as.
