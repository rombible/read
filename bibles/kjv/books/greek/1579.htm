Strong's Concordance
ekkolumbaó: to swim out of
Original Word: ἐκκολυμβάω
Part of Speech: Verb
Transliteration: ekkolumbaó
Phonetic Spelling: (ek-kol-oom-bah'-o)
Definition: to swim out of
Usage: I swim out (of the water).
