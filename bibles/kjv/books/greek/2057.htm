Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2057: Ἑρμᾶς

Ἑρμᾶς, accusative Ἑρμᾶν (cf. Buttmann, 20 (18)), ὁ (Doric for Ἑρμῆς), Hermas, a certain Christian (whom Origen and others thought to be the author of the book entitled The Shepherd (cf. Salmon in Dict. of Chris. Biog. under the word Hermas 2)): Romans 16:14.
