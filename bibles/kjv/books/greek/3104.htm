Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3104: Μαϊνάν

Μαϊνάν (T Tr WH Μεννά), indeclinable (Lachmann Μεννας, genitive Meyer), ὁ, Menna or Menan (A. V.-1611Menam), the name of one of Christ's ancestors: Luke 3:31 (Lachmann brackets τοῦ Μαϊνάν).

STRONGS NT 3104: ΜεννάΜεννά or Μεννας, see Μαϊνάν.
