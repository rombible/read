Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5336: φάτνη

φάτνη, φάτνης, ἡ ((πατέομαι to eat; Vanicek, p. 445)), a crib, manger: Luke 2:7, 12, 16; Luke 13:15. (From Homer down; the Sept. for אֵבוּס, Job 39:9; Proverbs 14:4; Isaiah 1:3; plural for רְפָתִים, Habakkuk 3:17.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
manager, stall.

From pateomai (to eat); a crib (for fodder) -- manager, stall.
