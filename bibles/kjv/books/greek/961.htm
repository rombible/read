Beroiaios: belonging to Berea, Berean
Original Word: Βεροιαῖος, α, ον
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: Beroiaios
Phonetic Spelling: (ber-oy-ah'-yos)
Definition: Berean, a native of Berea
Usage: belonging to Berea, Berean.
