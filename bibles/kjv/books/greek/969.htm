bérullos: beryl
Original Word: βήρυλλος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: bérullos
Phonetic Spelling: (bay'-rool-los)

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 969: βήρυλλος

βήρυλλος, βηρυλλου, ὁ, ἡ, beryl, a precious stone of a pale green color (Pliny, h. n. 37, 5 (20) (i. e. 37, 79)): Revelation 21:20. (Tobit 13:17; neuter βηρύλλιον, equivalent to שֹׁהַם, Exodus 28:20; Exodus 36:20 ()). Cf. Winers RWB under the word Edelsteine, 11; (especially Riehm, HWB, ibid. 3 and 12).
