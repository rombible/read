kinesis: a moving, stirring
Original Word: κίνησις, εως, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine

Phonetic Spelling: (kin'-ay-sis)

Thayer's Greek Lexicon

κίνησις, κινήσεως, ἡ (κινέω) (from Plato on), a moving, agitation: τοῦ ὕδατος, John 5:3 (R L).
