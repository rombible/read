Zakchaios: Zaccheus, a tax collector
Original Word: Ζακχαῖος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Zakchaios
Phonetic Spelling: (dzak-chah'-ee-yos)
Definition: Zaccheus, a tax collector
Usage: Zacchaeus, a Jewish tax-gatherer.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2195: Ζακχαῖος

Ζακχαῖος, Ζακχαιου, ὁ (זַכַּי pure, innocent; cf. 2 Esdr. 2:9; Nehemiah 7:14), Zacchaeus, a chief tax-collector: Luke 19:2, 5, 8. (B. D., under the word.)
