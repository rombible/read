Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1158: Δανιήλ

Δανιήλ, ὁ (דָּנִיאֵל and דָּנִאֵל i. e. judge of God (or God is my judge)), Daniel, proper name of a Jewish prophet, conspicuous for his wisdom, to whom are ascribed the well-known prophecies composed between ; (but cf. BB. DD.): Matthew 24:15; Mark 13:14 Rec.
