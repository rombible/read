Strong's Concordance
chóros: the northwest wind
Original Word: χῶρος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: chóros
Phonetic Spelling: (kho'-ros)
Definition: the northwest wind
Usage: the north-west wind, and the quarter of the sky from which it comes.

χῶρος, χωρου, ὁ, the northwest wind (LatinCorus orCaurus): for the quarter of the heavens from which this wind blows, Acts 27:12 (on which see λίψ, 2).
