Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1965: ἐπίορκος

ἐπίορκος, ἐπιορκον (from ἐπί (which see D. 7) against, and ὅρκος); (masculine as a substantive) a false swearer, a perjurer: 1 Timothy 1:10. (From Homer down.)
