Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1708: ἐμπλοκή

ἐμπλοκή (see ἐν, III. 3), ἐμπλοκῆς, ἡ, (ἐμπλέκω), an interweaving, braiding, a knot: τριχῶν (Lachmann omits), an elaborate gathering of the hair into knots, Vulg.capillatura, (A. V. plaiting), 1 Peter 3:3 (κόμης, Strabo 17, p. 828).
