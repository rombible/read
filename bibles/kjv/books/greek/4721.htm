stegé: a roof

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4721: στέγη

στέγη, στεγης, ἡ (στέγω to cover), from Aeschylus and Herodotus down, a roof: of a house, Mark 2:4; ἐισέρχεσθαι ὑπό τήν στέγην τίνος (see εἰσέρχομαι, 1, p. 187{b} bottom), Matthew 8:8; Luke 7:6.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
roof.

Strengthened from a primary tegos (a "thatch" or "deck" of a building); a roof -- roof.
