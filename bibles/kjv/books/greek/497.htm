Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 497: ἀντιστρατεύομαι

ἀντιστρατεύομαι;

1. to make a military expedition, or take the field, against anyone: Xenophon, Cyril 8, 8, 26.

2. to oppose, war against: τίνι, Romans 7:23. (Aristaenet. 2, 1, 13.)
