Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2490: Ἰωαννας

Ἰωαννας, Ἰωάννα, and (according to L T Tr WH) Ἰωανάν, indeclinable (see Ἰωάννης), ὁ, Joannas (or Joanan), one of the ancestors of Christ: Luke 3:27.
