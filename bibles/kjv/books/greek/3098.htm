Strong's Concordance
Magóg: Magog, a foreign nation
Original Word: Μαγώγ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Magóg
Phonetic Spelling: (mag-ogue')
Definition: Magog, a foreign nation
Usage: (Hebrew), Magog, sometimes as name of a people, sometimes as name of a country in the Old Testament, probably the Scythians; hence: used in apocalyptic literature.
