Strong's Concordance
Barachias: Barachiah, the father of a man killed in the temple
Original Word: Βαραχίας, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Barachias
Phonetic Spelling: (bar-akh-ee'-as)
Definition: Barachiah, the father of a man killed in the temple
Usage: Barachiah; his identity is uncertain, perhaps father of the Zechariah killed by the Zealots in the last Jewish War.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 914: Βαραχίας

Βαραχίας, Βαραχιου, ὁ (בֶּרֶכְיָה, Jehovah blesses), Barachiah in Matthew 23:35 said to have been the father of the Zachariah slain in the temple; cf. Ζαχαρίας.
