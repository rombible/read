Strong's Concordance
epipothésis: longing
Original Word: ἐπιπόθησις, εως, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: epipothésis
Phonetic Spelling: (ep-ee-poth'-ay-sis)
Definition: longing
Usage: eager longing (desire), strong affection.
