Strong's Concordance
Kornélios: Cornelius, a Roman centurion
Original Word: Κορνήλιος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Kornélios
Phonetic Spelling: (kor-nay'-lee-os)
Definition: Cornelius, a Roman centurion
Usage: Cornelius, a centurion of the Roman army, stationed at Caesarea.

Κορνήλιος, Κορνηλίου, ὁ, a Latin name, Cornelius, a Roman centurion living at Caesarea, converted to Christianity by Peter: Acts 10:1ff
