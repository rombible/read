Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2140: εὐποιΐα

εὐποιΐα (εὐποιΐα WH (cf. Iota, at the end)), εὐποιΐας, ἡ (ἐυποιος), a doing good, beneficence: Hebrews 13:16; Arrian exp. Alex. 7, 28, 8; Alciphron 1, 10; Lucian, imag. 21; a benefit, kindness, Josephus, Antiquities 2, 11, 2; (plural, ibid. 19, 9, 1).
