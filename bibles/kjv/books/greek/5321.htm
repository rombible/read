Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5321: φανέρωσις

φανέρωσις, φανερωσεως, ἡ (φανερόω), manifestation: with a genitive of the object, 1 Corinthians 12:7; 2 Corinthians 4:2. ((Aristotle, de plantis 2, 1 and 9; also for אוּרִים (the Sept. δελωσις) Leviticus 8:8 manuscript Venet.) Ecclesiastical writings, Hesychius) (Synonym: see ἀποκαλύπτω, at the end.)
