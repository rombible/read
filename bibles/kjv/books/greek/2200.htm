Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2200: ζεστός

ζεστός, ζεστη, ζεστον (ζέω), boiling hot, hot, (Strabo, Appian, (Diogenes Laërtius, others); metaphorically, of fervor of mind and zeal: Revelation 3:15f.
