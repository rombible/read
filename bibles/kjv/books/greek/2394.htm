Strong's Concordance
Iasón: Jason, the name of one, perhaps two, Christian
Original Word: Ἰάσων, ονος, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Iasón
Phonetic Spelling: (ee-as'-oan)
Definition: Jason, the name of one, perhaps two, Christian
Usage: Jason, a Christian of Thessalonica, perhaps the same as the relative of Paul.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2394: Ἰάσων

Ἰάσων, Ἰάσονος, ὁ, Jason, a Thessalonian, Paul's host: Acts 17:5-7, 9; whether he is the same who is mentioned in Romans 16:21 as a kinsman of Paul is uncertain.
