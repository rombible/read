STRONGS NT 3959: Πάταρα

Πάταρα, Παταρων, τά (cf. Winers Grammar, 176 (166)), Patara, a maritime city of Lycia, celebrated for an oracle of Apollo: Acts 21:1. (B. D. under the word ; Lewin, St. Paul, ii. 99f.)
