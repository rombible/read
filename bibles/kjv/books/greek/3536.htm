Strong's Concordance
Nineuités: a Ninevite, an inhabitant of Ninevah
Original Word: Νινευίτης, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Nineuités
Phonetic Spelling: (nin-yoo-ee'-tace)
Definition: a Ninevite, an inhabitant of Ninevah
Usage: a Ninevite, an inhabitant of Nineveh, a city on the Tigris in Assyria.
