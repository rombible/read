Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 274: ἀμέμπτως

ἀμέμπτως, adverb, blamelessly, so that there is no cause for censure: 1 Thessalonians 2:10; ( WH marginal reading); . (From Aeschylus down. Cf. Trench, § ciii.)
