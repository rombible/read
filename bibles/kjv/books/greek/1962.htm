Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1962: ἐπινεύω

ἐπινεύω: 1 aorist ἐπένευσα; from Homer down; to nod to; tropically, (by a nod) to express approval, to assent: Acts 18:20, as often in Greek writings.
