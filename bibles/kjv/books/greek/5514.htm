Strong's Concordance
Chloé a prim. word: Chloe.
Original Word: Χλόη, ης, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: Chloé a prim. word
Phonetic Spelling: (khlo'-ay)
Definition: Chloe
Usage: Chloe, probably with business connections either in Corinth or in Ephesus or in both.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
young foliage (Verdant, epithet of the pagan goddess Demeter), Chloe, a Chr. woman
NASB Translation
Chloe's (1).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5514: Χλόη

Χλόη ((i. e. 'tender verdure'; an appellation of Demeter, 'the Verdant')), Χλόης, ἡ, Chloe, a Christian woman of Corinth: 1 Corinthians 1:11. (Cf. B. D., under the word.)
