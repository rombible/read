apseudés: free from falsehood
Original Word: ἀψευδής, ές
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: apseudés
Phonetic Spelling: (aps-yoo-dace')
Definition: free from falsehood
Usage: not guilty of falsehood, truthful.
