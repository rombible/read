Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5017: τάραχος

τάραχος, ταραχου, ὁ (παράσσω), commotion, stir (of mind): Acts 12:18; tumult (A. V. stir), Acts 19:23. (The Sept.; Xenophon, Plutarch, Lucian).
