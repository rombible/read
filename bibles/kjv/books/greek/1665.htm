Original Word: Ἐλισάβετ, ἡ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable

Phonetic Spelling: (el-ee-sab'-et)

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1665: Ἐλισάβετ

Ἐλισάβετ (WH Ἐλεισαβετ, see WH's Appendix, p. 155, and under the word εἰ, ἰ) (אֱלִישֶׁבַע my God is my oath, i. e. a worshipper of God), Elisabeth, wife of Zacharias the priest and mother of John the Baptist: Luke 1:5ff
