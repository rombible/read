próinos: at early morning
Original Word: πρωϊνός, ή, όν
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: próinos
Phonetic Spelling: (pro-ee-nos')
Definition: at early morning
Usage: belonging to the morning, early.

Word Origin
from prói
Definition

NASB Translation

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4407: πρωϊνός

πρωϊνός (WH πρωϊνός (see their Appendix, p. 152), Tdf. edition 7 προϊνός (cf. Iota)) (for the older πρώιος, see ὀρθρινός; the same term. in the Latinserotinus, diutinus), πρωινή, πρωινόν (πρωι<), pertaining to the morning: ὁ ἀστήρ ὁ πρωϊνός, Revelation 2:28 (on which see ἀστήρ); (where Rec. ὀρθρινός). (The Sept.; Babrius, Plutarch, Ath., others.)
