STRONGS NT 1027: βροντή

βροντή, βροντῆς, ἡ, thunder: Mark 3:17 (on which see Βοανεργές); John 12:29; Revelation 4:5; Revelation 6:1; Revelation 8:5; Revelation 10:3; Revelation 11:19; Revelation 14:2; Revelation 16:18; Revelation 19:6. (From Homer down.)

Akin to bremo (to roar); thunder -- thunder(-ing).
