Strong's Concordance
goneus: a parent
Original Word: γονεύς, έως, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: goneus
Phonetic Spelling: (gon-yooce')
Definition: a parent
Usage: a begetter, father; plur: parents.

Word Origin
from ginomai
Definition
a parent
NASB Translation

STRONGS NT 1118: γονεύς

γονεύς, γονεως, ὁ (ΓΑΝΩ, γέγονα) (Homer h. Cer., Hesiod, others); a begetter, parent; plural οἱ γονεῖς the parents: Luke 2:41, 43 L text T Tr WH; (); ; John 9:2, 3, 20, 22, 23; 2 Corinthians 12:14; Romans 1:30; Ephesians 6:1; Colossians 3:20; 2 Timothy 3:2; accusative plural γονεῖς: Matthew 10:21; ( Lachmann marginal reading); Luke 2:27; (); Mark 13:12; (John 9:18); on this form cf. Winers Grammar, § 9, 2; (Buttmann, 14 (13)).
