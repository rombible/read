Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4858: συμφωνία

συμφωνία, συμφωνίας, ἡ (σύμφωνος) (from Plato down), music: Luke 15:25. (Polybius 28, 10, 5; (plural of 'the music of the spheres,' Aristotle, de caelo 2, 9, p. 290b, 22; others.))
