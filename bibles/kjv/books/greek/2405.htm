Strong's Concordance
hierateia: priesthood
Original Word: ἱερατεία, ας, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: hierateia
Phonetic Spelling: (hee-er-at-i'-ah)
Definition: priesthood
Usage: the duty (office) of a priest, priesthood.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from hierateuó
Definition
priesthood
NASB Translation
priest's office (1), priestly office (1).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2405: ἱερατεία

ἱερατεία (WH ἱερατια; cf. Iota), ἱερατείας, ἡ (ἱερατεύω), the priesthood, the office of priest: Luke 1:9; Hebrews 7:5. (the Sept. for כְּהֻנָּה; Aristotle, pol. 7, 8; Dionysius Halicarnassus; Boeckh, Inscriptions ii., pp. 127, 23; 363, 27.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
office of the priesthood, priest's office.

From hierateuo; priestliness, i.e. The sacerdotal function -- office of the priesthood, priest's office.
