Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5617: ὡσεη

ὡσεη (G T Tr, but R L Ὠσεη; see WH. Introduction § 408; Tdf. Proleg., p. 107) (הושֵׁעַ 'deliverance'), ὁ, Hosea, a well-known Hebrew prophet, son of Beeri and contemporary of Isaiah (Hosea 1:1f): Romans 9:25.
