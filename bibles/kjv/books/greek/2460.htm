Strong's Concordance
hippeus: a horseman
Original Word: ἱππεύς, έως, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: hippeus
Phonetic Spelling: (hip-yooce')
Definition: a horseman
Usage: a horse-soldier, a mounted soldier, a cavalryman.
