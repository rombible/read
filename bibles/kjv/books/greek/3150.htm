Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3150: ματαιολογία

ματαιολογία, ματαιολογιας, ἡ (ματαιολόγος), vain talking, empty talk (Vulg.vaniloquium): 1 Timothy 1:6. (Plutarch, mor., p. 6 f.; Porphyry, de abstin. 4, 16.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
vain jangling.

From mataiologos; random talk, i.e. Babble -- vain jangling.
