Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5402: Φοίβη

Φοίβη, Φοιβης, ἡ (literally, 'bright', 'radiant'), Phoebe or Phebe, a deaconess of the church at Cenchreae, near Corinth Romans 16:1 ((see διάκονος, 2 at the end)).
