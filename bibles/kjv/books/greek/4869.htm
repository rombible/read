Strong's Concordance
sunaichmalótos: a fellow prisoner
Original Word: συναιχμάλωτος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: sunaichmalótos
Phonetic Spelling: (soon-aheekh-mal'-o-tos)
Definition: a fellow prisoner
Usage: a fellow captive or prisoner.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from sun and aichmalótos
Definition
a fellow prisoner
NASB Translation
fellow prisoner (2), fellow prisoners (1).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4869: συναιχμάλωτος

συναιχμάλωτος, συναιχμαλωτου, ὁ, a fellow-prisoner (Vulg.concaptivus): Romans 16:7; Colossians 4:10; Philemon 1:23 (Lucian, asin. 27). (Cf. Lightfoot on Colossians, the passage cited; Fritzsche, Commentary on Romans, vol. i., p. 21 note.)
