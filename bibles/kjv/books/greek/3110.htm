Strong's Concordance
Makedón: a Macedonian, an inhabitant of Macedonia
Original Word: Μακεδών, όνος, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Makedón
Phonetic Spelling: (mak-ed'-ohn)
Definition: a Macedonian, an inhabitant of Macedonia
Usage: a Macedonian, an inhabitant of the Roman province Macedonia.
