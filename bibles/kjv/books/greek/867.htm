Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 867: ἄφιξις

ἄφιξις, ἀφιξεως, ἡ (ἀφικνέομαι), in Greek writings generally arrival; more rarely departure, as Herodotus 9, 17; Demosthenes 1463, 7; (1484, 8); Josephus, Antiquities 4, 8, 47; 3Macc. 7:18; and so in Acts 20:29.
