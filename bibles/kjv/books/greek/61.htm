Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 61: ἄγρα

ἄγρα, (ας, ἡ (ἄγω);

1. a catching, hunting: Luke 5:4.

2. the thing caught: ἡ ἄγρα τῶν ἰχθύων ' the catch or haul of fish' i. e. the fishes taken (A. V. draught), Luke 5:9.
