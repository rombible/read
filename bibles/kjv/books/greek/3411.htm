Definition

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3411: μισθωτός

μισθωτός, μισθωτοῦ, ὁ (μισθόω), one hired, a hireling: Mark 1:20; John 10:12f (Aristophanes, Plato, Demosthenes, others; the Sept. for שָׂכִיר.)
