Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 148: αἰσχρολογία

αἰσχρολογία, (ας, ἡ (from αἰσχρολόγος, and this from αἰσχρός and λέγω), foul speaking (Tertullianturpiloquium), low and obscene speech, (R. V. shameful speaking): Colossians 3:8: (Xenophon, Aristotle, Polybius) (Cf. Lightfoot at the passage; Trench, § xxxiv.)
