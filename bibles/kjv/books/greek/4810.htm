Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4810: σῦκον

σῦκον, σύκου, τό, from Homer down, Hebrew תְּאֵנָה, a fig, the ripe fruit of ἡ συκῆ (which see): Matthew 7:16; Mark 11:13; Luke 6:44; James 3:12.
