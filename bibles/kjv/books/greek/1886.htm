Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1886: ἔπαυλις

ἔπαυλις, ἐπαυλισεως, ἡ (ἐπί and αὖλις tent, place to pass the night in; hence, a country-house, cottage, cabin, fold), a farm; a dwelling (A. V. habitation): Acts 1:20 from Psalm 68:26 (). (Diodorus, Plutarch, others; also a camp, military quarters, Plato, Polybius)
