Strong's Concordance
Ioel: Joel
Original Word: Ἰωήλ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Ioel
Phonetic Spelling: (ee-o-ale')
Definition: Joda, an Israelite
Usage: Joel, the Hebrew prophet.
Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2493: Ιωηλ

Ιωηλ, ὁ, indeclinable (יואֵל whose God is Jehovah, equivalent to a worshipper of God (others, 'Jehovah is God')), Joel, the eminent prophet who according to the opinion of very many recent writers prophesied in the reign of Uzziah (cf. B. D. under the word , 3): Acts 2:16.
