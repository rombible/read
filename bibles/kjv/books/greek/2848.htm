kokkos: a grain
Original Word: κόκκος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: kokkos
Phonetic Spelling: (kok'-kos)
Definition: a grain

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2848: κόκκος

κόκκος, κοκκου, ὁ (cf. Vanicek, Fremdwörter etc., p. 26), a grain: Matthew 13:31; Matthew 17:20; Mark 4:31; Luke 13:19; Luke 17:6; John 12:24; 1 Corinthians 15:37. (Homer h. Cer., Herodotus down.)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
kernel of corn, grain.

Apparently a primary word; a kernel of seed -- corn, grain.
