Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2496: Ιωραμ

Ιωραμ, ὁ, indeclinable (יְחורָם i. e. whom Jehovah exalted), Joram, the son and successor of Jehoshaphat on the throne of Judah, from (circa) to 884 (2 Kings 8:16ff; 2 Chronicles 21:2ff): Matthew 1:8.
