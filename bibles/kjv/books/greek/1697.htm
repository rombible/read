Hemmór: Emmor, a Canaanite
Original Word: Ἐμμόρ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Hemmór
Phonetic Spelling: (em-mor')

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1697: Ἑμμόρ

Ἑμμόρ (Αμμωρ L T Tr (but WH Ἑμμώρ, see their Introductory § 408)), ὁ (חֲמור i. e. ass), Emmor (or Hamor, according to the Hebrew), proper name of a man: Acts 7:16; see concerning him, Genesis 33:19; Genesis 34:2f
