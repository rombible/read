ἐντροπή, ἐντροπῆς, ἡ (ἐντρέπω, which see), shame: πρός ἐντροπήν ὑμῖν λέγω (or λαλῶ), to arouse your shame, 1 Corinthians 6:5; 1 Corinthians 15:34. (Psalm 34:26 (); Psalm 68:8, 20 (); respect, reverence, Sophocles, Polybius, Josephus, others.)
