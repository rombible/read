Strong's Concordance
krinon: a lily
Original Word: κρίνον, ου, τό
Part of Speech: Noun, Neuter
Transliteration: krinon
Phonetic Spelling: (kree'-non)
Definition: a lily
Usage: a lily growing wild, variously identified with the red anemone, the whole lily, the sword lily.
