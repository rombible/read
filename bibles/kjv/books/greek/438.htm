Strong's Concordance
anthos: a flower
Original Word: ἄνθος, ους, τό
Part of Speech: Noun, Neuter
Transliteration: anthos
Phonetic Spelling: (anth'-os)
Definition: a flower
Usage: bloom, possibly a reference to the bright flowers, such as poppies (among the grass).
