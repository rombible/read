Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5222: ὑπάντησις

ὑπάντησις, ὑπαντησεως, ἡ (ὑπαντάω), a going to meet: John 12:13, and L T Tr WH in Matthew 8:34 (Buttmann, § 146, 3) and Matthew 25:1 (cf. Buttmann, the passage cited). (Judges 11:34; Josephus, Antiquities 11, 8, 4; Appendix,

b. c. 4, 6.)
