Bariónas: "son of Jonah," Bar-Jonah, a surname of Peter
Original Word: Βαριωνᾶς, ᾶ, ὁ
Part of Speech: Proper Noun, Indeclinable
Transliteration: Bariónas
Phonetic Spelling: (bar-ee-oo-nas')
Definition: "son of Jonah", Bar-Jonah, a surname of Peter
Usage: Bar-Jonas, son of Jonas, the surname of Simon Peter.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 920: Βαριωνας

Βαριωνας, Βαριωνᾶ (cf. Buttmann, 20 (17f)), ὁ (from בַּר son, and יונָה Jonah (others יוהָנָן i. e. Johanan, Jona, John; cf. Meyer on John 1:42(43) and Lightfoot as below)), Bar-Jonah (or Bar-Jonas), the surname of the apostle Peter: Matthew 16:17 (L T WH; in John 1:42 (); ff son of John; see Lightfoot Fresh Revision, etc., p. 159 note (American edition, p. 137 note)); see in Βαρ and Ἰοωνας, 2.
