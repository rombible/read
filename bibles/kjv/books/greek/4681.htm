Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4681: Σπανία

Σπανία, Σπανίας, ἡ, Spain, in the apostolic age the whole peninsula south of the Pyrenees: Romans 15:24, 28. ((Winer's Grammar, 25); the more common Greek form is Ἰσπανια, 1 Macc. 8:3 (apparently the Phoenician or Latin name for Ἰβηρια; cf. Pape, Eigennamen, under the words).)
