Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4497: Ροβοαμ

Ροβοαμ (רְחַבְעָם, i. e. 'enlarging the people', equivalent to Ἐυρυδημος in Grk, from רָחַב and עָם), ὁ, Roboam, Rehoboam, the son and successor of king Solomon: Matthew 1:7.
