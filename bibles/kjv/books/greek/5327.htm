Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5327: φάραγξ

φάραγξ, φάραγγος, ἡ, a valley shut in by cliffs and precipices; a ravine: Luke 3:5. (Alcman, Euripides, Thucydides, Demosthenes, Polybius, others; the Sept..)
