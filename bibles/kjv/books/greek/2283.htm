Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2283: Θαμάρ

Θαμάρ (Treg. Θαμάρ), ἡ (טָמָר (i. e. palm-tree)), Tamar, proper name of a woman, the daughter-in-law of Judah, son of the patriarch Jacob (Genesis 38:6): Matthew 1:3.
