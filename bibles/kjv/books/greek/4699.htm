Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4699: σπόγγος

σπόγγος, σπογγου, ὁ (perhaps akin is fungus; Curtius, § 575), from Homer down, sponge: Matthew 27:48; Mark 15:36; John 19:29.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
