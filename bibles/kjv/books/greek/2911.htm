krémnos: a steep bank

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2911: κρημνός

κρημνός, κρημνοῦ, ὁ (from κρεμάννυμι), a steep (place), a precipice: Matthew 8:32; Mark 5:13; Luke 8:33. (2 Chronicles 25:12; Greek writings from Homer down.)
