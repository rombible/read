kataloipos: remaining
Original Word: κατάλοιπος, ον
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: kataloipos
Phonetic Spelling: (kat-al'-oy-pos)
Definition: remaining
Usage: left behind; the rest, the remainder.
