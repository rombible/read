Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 1028: βροχή

βροχή, βροχης, ἡ (βρέχω, which see), a later Greek word (cf. Lob. ad Phryn., p. 291), a besprinkling, watering, rain: used of a heavy shower or violent rainstorm, Matthew 7:25, 27; Psalm 67:10 (); Psalm 104:32 (, for גֶּשֶׁם.
