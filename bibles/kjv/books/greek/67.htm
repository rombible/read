Strong's Concordance
Agrippas: Agrippa, the name of two descendant of Herod the Great
Original Word: Ἀγρίππας, α, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Agrippas
Phonetic Spelling: (ag-rip'-pas)
Definition: Agrippa, the name of two descendant of Herod the Great
Usage: Agrippa, i.e. Herod Agrippa II.
