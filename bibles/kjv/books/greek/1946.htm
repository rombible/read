Strong's Concordance
Epikoureios: an Epicurean
Original Word: Ἐπικούρειος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Epikoureios
Phonetic Spelling: (ep-ee-koo'-ri-os)
Definition: to run ashore
Usage: an Epicurean, one who holds the tenets of Epicurus.

Ἐπικούρειος (Ἐπικουριος T WH; see Iota), Ἐπικουρειου, ὁ, Epicurean, belonging to the sect of Epicurus, the philosopher: Acts 17:18.
