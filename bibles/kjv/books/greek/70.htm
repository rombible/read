Strong's Concordance
agrupnia: sleeplessness, watching
Original Word: ἀγρυπνία, ας, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: agrupnia
Phonetic Spelling: (ag-roop-nee'-ah)
Definition: sleeplessness, watching
Usage: the state of being awake (at night), sleeplessness, watching.
HELPS Word-studies

Cognate: 70 agrypnía – properly, without sleep; sleeplessness. See 69 (agrypnéō).
