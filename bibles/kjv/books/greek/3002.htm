◄ 3002. Lebbaios ►
Strong's Concordance
Lebbaios: Lebbaeus.
Original Word: Λεββαῖος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: Lebbaios
Phonetic Spelling: (leb-bah'-yos)
Definition: Lebbaeus
Usage: Lebbaeus, a pet-name for Thaddaeus, one of the twelve apostles of Jesus; the full form of the name is not known.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
variant reading for Thaddaios, q.v.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3002: Λεββαῖος

Λεββαῖος, see Θαδδαῖος.
