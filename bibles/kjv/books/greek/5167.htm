Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5167: τρυγών

τρυγών, τρυγόνος, ἡ (from τρύζω to murmur, sigh, coo, of doves; cf. γογγύζω), a turtle-dove: Luke 2:24. (Aristophanes, Theocritus, others; Aeh v. h. 1, 15; the Sept. for תֹּר.)
