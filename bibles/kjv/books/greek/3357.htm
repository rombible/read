Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 3357: μετρίως

μετρίως (μέτριος), adverb (from Herodotus down);

a. in due measure.

b. moderately: οὐ μετρίως (A. V. not a little), exceedingly (Plutarch, Flam. 9, et al.), Acts 20:12.
