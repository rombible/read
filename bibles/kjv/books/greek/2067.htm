Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2067: ἔσθησις

ἔσθησις (Rec.elz ἔσθησις), ἐσθησεως, ἡ (from ἐσθέω, and this from ἐσθής, which see), clothing, apparel: plural, Luke 24:4 R G; Acts 1:10 L T Tr WH; (cf. Philo, vit. Moys. iii. § 18; Eusebius, h. e. 2, 6, 7 and Heinichen's note). (Rare in secular writings (Aristotle, rhet. 2, 8, 14 variant); cf. Winer's Grammar, § 2, 1 c.)
