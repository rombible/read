pseudapostolos: a false apostle
Original Word: ψευδαπόστολος, ου, ὁ
Part of Speech: Noun, Masculine
Transliteration: pseudapostolos
Phonetic Spelling: (psyoo-dap-os'-tol-os)

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 5570: ψευδαπόστολος

ψευδαπόστολος, ψευδαποστολου, ὁ (ψευδής and ἀπόστολος), a false apostle, one who falsely claims to be an ambassador of Christ: 2 Corinthians 11:13.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
false teacher.

From pseudes and apostolos; a spurious apostle, i.e. Pretended pracher -- false teacher.
