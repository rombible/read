sunantésis: meet.
Original Word: συνάντησις, εως, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: sunantésis
Phonetic Spelling: (soon-an'-tay-sis)
Definition: meet
Usage: a meeting with.
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
late or variant reading for hupantésis, q.v.

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 4877: συνάντησις

συνάντησις, συναντησεως, ἡ, a meeting with (Euripides, Ion 535; Dionysius Halicarnassus, Antiquities 4, 66): εἰς συνάντησιν τίνι, to meet one (Buttmann, § 146, 3), Matthew 8:34 R G (for לִקְרַאת, Genesis 14:17; Genesis 30:16; Exodus 4:27; Exodus 18:7).
