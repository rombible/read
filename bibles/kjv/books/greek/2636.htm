Strong's Concordance
katalalia: evil-speaking
Original Word: καταλαλιά, ας, ἡ
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine
Transliteration: katalalia
Phonetic Spelling: (kat-al-al-ee'-ah)
Definition: evil-speaking
Usage: evil-speaking, backbiting, detraction, slander.
HELPS Word-studies

Cognate: 2636 katalalía – evil speech, slander (railing, defaming talk). See 2635 (katalaleō).

Thayer's Greek Lexicon
STRONGS NT 2636: καταλαλιά

καταλαλιά, καταλαλιάς, ἡ (κατάλαλος, which see), defamation, evil-speaking: 2 Corinthians 12:20; 1 Peter 2:1 (on the plural cf. Winers Grammar, 176 (166); Buttmann, 77 (67)). (Wis. 1:11; Clement of Rome, 1 Cor. 30, 1 [ET]; 35, 5 [ET], and ecclesiastical writings; not found in classical Greek.)
