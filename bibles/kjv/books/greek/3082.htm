Strong's Concordance
Lustra: Lystra, a city of Lycaonia
Original Word: Λύστρα, ας, ἡ, ων, τά
Part of Speech: Noun, Feminine; Noun, Neuter
Transliteration: Lustra
Phonetic Spelling: (loos'-trah)
Definition: Lystra, a city of Lycaonia
Usage: Lystra, a Lycaonian city in the southern part of the Roman province Galatia.
