Definition

Gudgodah (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
גֻּדְגֹּ֫דָה with article הַגֻּדְגֹּ֫דָה proper name, of a location (meaning dubious) station of Israel in wilderness Deuteronomy 10:7 (twice in verse) (in "" Numbers 33:32 חֹר הַגִּדְגָּ֑ד see below חֹר).
