◄ 3996. mabo ►
Strong's Concordance
mabo: entrance, a coming in, entering
Original Word: מָבוֹא
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: mabo
Phonetic Spelling: (maw-bo')
Definition: entrance, a coming in, entering
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from bo
Definition
entrance, a coming in, entering
NASB Translation
come (1), enter (1), entrance (13), entry (1), place of its setting (1), setting (5), sunset* (1), west* (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מָבוֺא noun masculineJeremiah 38:14 entrance, a coming in, entering; — ׳מ absolute Jeremiah 38:14 3t. + Ezekiel 42:9 Kt (Qr מֵבִיא wrong, compare Co); construct מְבוֺא Deuteronomy 11:30 12t.; suffix מְבוֺאוֺ Psalm 104:19 2t.; מְבֹאוֺ Psalm 50:1; מבואך 2 Samuel 3:25 Kt (Qr מוֺבָאֶ֑ךָ inexplicable compare Dr); plural construct מְבוֺאֵי Ezekiel 26:10, מְבוֺאֹת Ezekiel 27:3; —

1 entrance, i.e. place or way of entrance, into a city Judges 1:24,25; 1 Chronicles 4:39; Proverbs 8:3 (מְּתָחִים ׳מ); into buildings 2 Kings 11:16; 2 Kings 16:18; 1 Chronicles 9:19 (שֹׁמְרֵי הַמָּבוֺא), 2 Chronicles 23:13,15; Jeremiah 38:14; Ezekiel 42:9; Ezekiel 46:19; see also מְבוֺאֹת יָם entrance of (the) sea, gate of the sea, of situation of Tyre Ezekiel 27:3.

2 entering, act of entrance, by violence, in storm of city Ezekiel 26:10; coming in or together, making a crowd, כִּמְבוֺא עָ֑ם Ezekiel 33:31 simile of eagerness to hear Yahweh's word; in phrase אֶתמֿוֺצָאֲךָ וְאֶתמֿבואך 2 Samuel 3:25 thy going out and thy coming in, mode of entering temple, or the people who enter Ezekiel 44:5; particularly of sunset מְבוֺא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ Psalm 104:19; = west Deuteronomy 11:30; Joshua 1:4; Joshua 23:4 הַיָּם הַגָּדוֺל מְבוֺא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ = Mediterranean, Zechariah 8:7 אֶרֶץ מְבוֺא הַשֶּׁמֶשׁ ("" אֶרֶץ מִזְרָח), Malachi 1:11; Psalm 50:1; Psalm 113:3 sunset = west, opposed to sunrise = east; in phrase from east to west, i.e. over the whole earth, everywhere on earth.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
by which came, as cometh, in coming, as men enter into, entering, entrance into

From bow'; an entrance (the place or the act); specifically (with or without shemesh) sunset or the west; also (adverb with preposition) towards -- by which came, as cometh, in coming, as men enter into, entering, entrance into, entry, where goeth, going down, + westward. Compare mowba'.
