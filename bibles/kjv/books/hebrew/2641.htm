חַסְרָה proper name, masculine grandfather of Shallum who was husband of Huldah the prophetess (ᵐ5 A Εσσερη, ᵐ5L Ασερ) = חַרְחַם 2 Kings 22:14 (ᵐ5 Αραας, ᵐ5L Αδρα).
