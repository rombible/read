[מַגְזֵרָה] noun feminine cutting instrument, axe — 2 Samuel 12:31 of David's treatment of captives, וַיָּשֶׂם בַּמְּגֵרָה ֗֗֗ וּבְמַגְזְרוֺת הַבַּרְזֶל.
