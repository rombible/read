Definition
crooked
NASB Translation
crooked ways (1), roundabout (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[עֲקַלְקָל] adjective intensive crooked; — feminine plural אֳרָחוֺת עֲקַלְקַלּוֺת Judges 5:6 i.e. roundabout paths (GFM); suffix עֲקַלִקַלּוֺתָם Psalm 125:5 their crooked-nesses.
