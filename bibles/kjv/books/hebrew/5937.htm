Word Origin
a prim. root
Definition
to exult
NASB Translation
become jubilant (1), exult (11), exults (1), jubilant (1), rejoice (2).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
be joyful, rejoice, triumph

A primitive root; to jump for joy, i.e. Exult -- be joyful, rejoice, triumph.
