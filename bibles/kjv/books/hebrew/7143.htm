Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
קָרֵחַ14 proper name, masculine in Judah (bald one); — 2 Kings 25:23, Καρηθ, ᵐ5L Καρηε; Jeremiah 40:8 12t. Jeremiah, Καρηε.
