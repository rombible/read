NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from sheba
Definition
sevenfold, seven times
NASB Translation
seven times (2), sevenfold (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
שִׁבְעָתַ֫יִם noun feminine dual seven-fold, seven times (compare Ges§§ 97h, 134r); —

1 seven-fold, seven times as much, Isaiah 30:26; Proverbs 6:31; as adverb Genesis 4:15,24; Psalm 79:12.

2 seven times, adverb Psalm 12:7. — 2 Samuel 21:9 read Qr שְׁבַעְתָּם see I. שֶׁבַע.
