1 as simple number:

a. absolute singular, (I) following by singular of noun enumerated: מֵאָה קְשִׂיטָה Genesis 33:19; Joshua 24:32 (both E); אִישׁ ׳מ Judges 7:19; 1 Kings 18:13; 2 Kings 4:43; אַמָּה ׳מ 1 Kings 7:2; Ezekiel 40:19 +; כִּכָּר ׳מ 2 Kings 23:33; 2Chronicles 25:6; 27:5; 36:3; Ezra 8:26; אֶלֶף ׳מ = 100,000 1 Kings 20:29 6t. hundredfold

Or metyah {may-yaw'}; properly, a primitive numeral; a hundred; also as a multiplicative and a fraction -- hundred((-fold), -th), + sixscore.
