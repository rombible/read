[מְרֵרָה] noun feminine gall; only suffix יִשְׁמֹּךְ לָאָרֶץ מְרֵרָתִי Job 16:13 he poureth on the ground my gall ("" יְפַלַּח כִּלְיוֺתַי), figurative of God's cruel treatment (compare מְרֹרָה Job 20:25).
