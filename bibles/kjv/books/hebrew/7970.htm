Brown-Driver-Briggs
שְׁלשִׁים (very seldom שְׁלשִׁפ, שְׁלוֺשִׁים):174

noun indeclinable thirty; —

1 before singular noun: אַמָּה Genesis 6:15 3t., +, with בָּאַמָּה Exodus 26:8 3t.; שָׁנָה 2 Samuel 5:4 12t. (age of paternity, of Levitical or royal duty, etc.), אִישׁ Judges 14:19 3t., etc.; before plural noun בָּנִים Judges 10:4; Judges 12:9, אֲנְשִׁים Jeremiah 38:10 (read here probably שְׁלשָׁהּ Ew Hi Gf and others), less common words Ezekiel 40:17; Ezra 1:9,10, etc.; after plural noun Genesis 32:16, מְּעָמִים ׳שׁ Ezekiel 41:6; אֶלֶף ׳שׁ 1 Chronicles 23:3 +, אֲלָפִים ׳שׁ Ezra 2:35; Nehemiah 7:38; ׳שׁ following units Genesis 11:12 14t., precedes 1 Kings 22:42 29t.; ׳שׁ following 100 (1000, etc.) Genesis 5:5 15t., precedes Genesis 5:3 21t.

2 as ordinal ׳וְאַחַת שָׁנְה לְ שָׁנָה לְ (בִּ) שְׁנַת שׁ 1 Kings 16:23, compare 1 Kings 16:29 4t.; שָׁנְה omitted Nehemiah 5:14 4t.; שְׁנַת omitted 2 Kings 25:27 = Jeremiah 52:31; שָׁנָךְ ׳בִּשׁ alone Ezekiel 1:1. — 1 Samuel 13:5 read שְׁלשֶׁת ᵐ5L We Dr and others; 2 Samuel 23:13 read שְׁלשָׁה Qr Vrss Th and modern; 1 Chronicles 11:11 read הַשְּׁלשֶׁת, הַשְּׁלשָׁה ᵐ5L Kit Benz and others (so "" 2 Samuel 23:8 for הַשָּׁלִשִׁי).
