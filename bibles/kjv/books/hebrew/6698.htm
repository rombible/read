Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. צוּר proper name, masculine Σουρ (Rock); —

1 a prince of Midian Numbers 25:15; Numbers 31:8; Joshua 13:21.

2 Gibeon-ite 1 Chronicles 8:30; 1 Chronicles 9:36.
