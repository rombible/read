Definition

NASB Translation
hornet (2), hornets (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
צִרְעָה noun feminine collective hornets (? as wounding, prostrating; Late Hebrew = Biblical Hebrew); — allies of Israel; — ׳הַצּ Exodus 23:28; Joshua 24:12 (both E), Deuteronomy 7:20.
