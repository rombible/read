NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin

Definition
Moses' father-in-law
NASB Translation
Jethro (9).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
יִתְרוֺ proper name, masculine father of Moses' wife Exodus 3:1; Exodus 4:18; b Exodus 18:1,2,5,6,9,10,12 (all E), = יֶתֶר Exodus 4:18; in all ᵐ5 Ιοθορ.
