Eliezer: "God is help," the name of several Israelites, also of a Damascene
Original Word: אֱלִיעֶזֶר
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Eliezer
Phonetic Spelling: (el-ee-eh'-zer)
Definition: "God is help", the name of several Israelites, also of a Damascene
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from el and ezer
Definition
"God is help," the name of several Isr., also of a Damascene
NASB Translation
Eliezer (14).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֱלִיעֶ֫זֶר (עֶֿ֑זרֶ Genesis 15:2 +) proper name, masculine (God is help, compare Exodus 18:4; see also אֶלְעָזָר below, עֲזַרְאֵל, Phoenician אשמנעזר, בעלעזר, עזרבעל)

a. Abraham's steward (אֱלִיעֶ֑זֶר), a Damascene Genesis 15:2.

b. a son of Moses Exodus 18:4; 1 Chronicles 23:15,17 (twice in verse).

c. Benjamite 1 Chronicles 7:8.

d. several priests 1 Chronicles 15:24; 1 Chronicles 26:25; Ezekiel 10:18.

e. Reubenite 1 Chronicles 27:16.

f. prophet in time of Jehoshaphat2Chronicles 20:37.

g. Levite chief Ezekiel 8:16; Ezekiel 10:23.

h. son of Harim Ezra 10:31.
