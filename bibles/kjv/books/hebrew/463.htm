Eliam: "God is kinsman," an Israelite name

Phonetic Spelling: (el-ee-awm')

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from el and am
Definition
"God is kinsman," an Isr. name
NASB Translation
Eliam (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֱלִיעָם proper name, masculine (God is kinsman; < = El is kinsman (see GrayEncy. Bib. AMMI, NAMES WITH Nöib. NAMES, § 46) Phoenician אלעם)

a. father of Bathsheba 2 Samuel 11:3; compare עַמִּיאֵל 1 Chronicles 3:5.

b. one of David's heroes 2 Samuel 23:34 (according to some = a).
