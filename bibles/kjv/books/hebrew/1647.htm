Strong's Concordance
Gereshom: three Israelites
Original Word: גֵּרְשׁם
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Gereshom
Phonetic Spelling: (gay-resh-ome')
Definition: three Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from garash
Definition
three Isr.
NASB Translation
Gershom (14).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
גֵּרְשֹׁם proper name, masculine

1 son of Moses & Sipporah Exodus 2:22 (explanation as if from גור) = Exodus 18:3; גֵּרְשׁוֺם 1 Chronicles 23:15,16; 1 Chronicles 26:24; so also גֵּרְשֹׁם Judges 18:30 ᵑ0 בֶּןֿ מְנַשֶּׁה compare VB.

2 a son of Levi גֵּרְשֹׁם 1 Chronicles 6:1; 1 Chronicles 6:28; 1 Chronicles 15:7 (compare גֵּרְשׁוֺן); גֵּרְשׁוֺם 1 Chronicles 6:2; 1 Chronicles 6:5; 1 Chronicles 6:47; 1 Chronicles 6:56.

3 a son of Phinehas Ezra 8:2.
