Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Eshtemoa, Eshtemoh

Or beshtmowam {esh-tem-o'-ah}; or seshtmoh {esh-tem-o'}; from shama' (in the sense of obedience); Eshtemoa or Eshtemoh, a place in Palestine -- Eshtemoa, Eshtemoh.

see HEBREW shama'
