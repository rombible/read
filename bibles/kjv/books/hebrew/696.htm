Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[אֹ֫רֶב] noun [masculine] ambuscade only figurative —

a. יָשִׂים אָרְבּוֺ Jeremiah 9:7 i.e. he planneth treachery.

b. אָרְבָּם their intrigue Hosea 7:6 compare Nowp.126.
