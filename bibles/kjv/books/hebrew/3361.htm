Word Origin

Definition
"let the people be established," a city in N. Isr.
NASB Translation
Jokmeam (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
יָקְמְעָם proper name, of a location (perhaps = יְקָמְעָם i.e. יָ֫קָם עָם Ol§ 277k, 3; = let the people be established ? compare GrayProp. N. 218); — in Northern Israel 1 Kings 4:12, Λουκαμ, ᵐ5L Ουκαμ; possibly = modern Tel Kaimûn, north of Megiddo, BuhlGeogr. 210; city of refuge in Ephraim 1 Chronicles 6:53 ( = קִבְצַיִם [q. v.] Joshua 21:22), Ικααμ, Ιεκμααν, etc.

קוֺנֵן see קין.
