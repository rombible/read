◄ 107. iggereth ►
Strong's Concordance
iggereth: a letter
Original Word: אִגְרֶת
Part of Speech: Noun Feminine
Transliteration: iggereth
Phonetic Spelling: (ig-eh'-reth)
Definition: a letter
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from the same as Agur
Definition
a letter
NASB Translation
letter (4), letters (6).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אִגֶּ֫רֶת noun feminine letter, letter-missive (late, probably loan-word, Assyrian egirtu DlW) — absolute Nehemiah 2:8 2t.; construct Esther 9:29; plural אִגְּרוֺת absolute 2 Chronicles 30:1 3t.; construct Nehemiah 2:9; אִגְּרֹתֵיהֶם Nehemiah 6:17 — letter, especially royal letter 2 Chronicles 30:1,6; Nehemiah 2:7,8,9; but also others Nehemiah 6:5,17,19; Esther 9:26,29 ("" סֵפֶר Esther 9:20; Esther 9:30; other synonym מִכְתָּב, נִשְׁתְּוָן q. v.)

אֲגַרְטָל see גרטל.

אֶגְרֹף see גרף.

אֵד, אוֺדוֺת, אֹדוֺת see אוד.
