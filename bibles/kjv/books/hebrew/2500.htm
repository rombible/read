Brown-Driver-Briggs
I. חֵ֫לֶף noun [masculine] exchange, only construct, as preposition in return for (so הֲלַף, , common in Aramaic e.g. Genesis 4:23 ᵑ7 ᵑ6 for Hebrew תַּחַת) — עֲבֹדָתָם ׳ח (עֲבֹדַתְכֶם) Numbers 18:21,31 (both P).
