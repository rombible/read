Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
diadem, hood, mitre

Or tsanowph {tsaw-nofe'}; or (feminine) tsaniyphah {tsaw-nee-faw'}; from tsanaph; a head-dress (i.e. Piece of cloth wrapped around) -- diadem, hood, mitre.
