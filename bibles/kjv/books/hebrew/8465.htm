Brown-Driver-Briggs
תַּ֫חַן proper name, masculine (perhaps abbreviated from תַּחֲנֶה) — an Ephraimite Numbers 26:35 ᵐ5 Ταναχ, 1 Chronicles 7:25 ᵐ5 Θαεν, ᵐ5L Θααν.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
night hawk

Probably from chanah; station; Tachan, the name of two Israelites -- Tahan.
