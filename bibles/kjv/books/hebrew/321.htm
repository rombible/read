NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
(Aramaic) from achar
Definition
another
NASB Translation
another (6), one else (1), other (2), someone else (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אָחֳרָן adjective masculine another (Egyptian Aramaic אחרן S-CD 11 +; Nabataean, Palmyrene id. (Lzb212); ᵑ7; compare Syriac ); — Daniel 2:11,44; Daniel 3:29; Daniel 4:5; Kt Daniel 5:17; Daniel 7:24. The feminine in use is אָחֳרַי (Egyptian Aramaic אחרה, RÉS246; ᵑ7 Talmud אוֺחְרִי (D§ 20. 5); compare K§ 61, 5 M§ 87 d NöBeitr.51), Daniel 2:39 ׳מַלְכוּ א, Daniel 2:39; Daniel 7:5,6,8,20.
