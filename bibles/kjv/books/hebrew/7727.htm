Shobab: two Israelites

Definition

NASB Translation
Shobab (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. שׁוֺבָב proper name, masculine Σωβαβ, etc.:

1 son of David 2 Samuel 5:14 = 1 Chronicles 14:4; 1 Chronicles 3:5.

2 son of Caleb 1 Chronicles 2:18.
