Tahanites

Or Tchaphnchec (Ezek. 30:18) {tekh- af-nekh-ace'}; or Tachpnec (Jeremiah 2:16) {takh-pen-ace'}; of Egyptian derivation; Tachpanches, Techaphneches or Tachpenes, a place in Egypt -- Tahapanes, Tahpanhes, Tehaphnehes.
