Strong's Concordance
ashte: one
Original Word: עַשְׁתֵּי
Part of Speech: Noun
Transliteration: ashte
Phonetic Spelling: (ash-tay')
Definition: one
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from ashath
Definition
one
NASB Translation
eleven* (6), eleventh* (13).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
עַשְׁתֵּי19 noun number one (Assyrian išten, one; išten ešrit, eleven; Dl§ 75, Tel Amarna [iš-]tit, first time; see Gie ZAW i (1881), 226 Ges§ 97e Köii. l, 212 and references); — only + עָשָׂר, עֶשְׂרֵה (q. v.) = eleven, eleventh, Jeremiah 1:3; Jeremiah 39:2 = Jeremiah 52:5 = 2 Kings 25:2; Ezekiel 26:1 Ezekiel 40:49; Zechariah 1:7; Exodus 26:7,8; Exodus 36:14,15; Numbers 7:72; Numbers 29:20 (all P), Deuteronomy 1:3; 1 Chronicles 12:13; 1 Chronicles 24:12; 1 Chronicles 25:18; 1 Chronicles 27:14.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
eleventh

Apparently masculine plural construction of esheth in the sense of an afterthought (used only in connection with asar in lieu of 'echad) eleven or (ordinal) eleventh -- + eleven(-th).
