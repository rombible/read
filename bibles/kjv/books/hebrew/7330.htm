[רָזֶה] adjective lean (on formation compare LagBN 49); — feminine singular רָזָה, Ezekiel 24:20 (of שֶׂה; opposed to בִּרְיָה), of land, = barren, Numbers 13:20 (J E; opposed to שְׁמֵנָה).
