Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
empire, kingdom, realm, reign, royal

Or malkuth {mal-kooth'}; or (in plural) malkuyah {mal-koo-yah'}; from malak; a rule; concretely, a dominion -- empire, kingdom, realm, reign, royal.
