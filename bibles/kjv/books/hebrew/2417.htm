Definition

NASB Translation
life (2), living (3), living man (1), who lives (1).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
life, that lives, living

(Aramaic) from chaya'; alive; also (as noun in plural) life -- life, that liveth, living.

see HEBREW chaya'
