adjective ordinal number seventh; — שְׁבִיעִי Genesis 2:2 +, שְׁבִעִי Exodus 12:15 +; feminine שְׁבִיעִית Joshua 6:16 +, Exodus 23:11 +, שְׁבִעִת Exodus 21:2 עִית- 1 Kings 18:44; 2Chronicles 23:1; — fifth, especially P; always with article:

1. masculine 7th day Genesis 2:3 47t.; month (חֹדֶשׁ) Genesis 8:4 23t. + (noun omitted) Ezekiel 45:25 3t.; lot (הַגּוֺרָל) Joshua 19:40 #NAME? 1 Chronicles 24:10; 1 Chronicles 25:14; noun omitted also 1 Chronicles 2:15; 1 Chronicles 26:3,5 (son), 1 Chronicles 12:11 (man), 1 Chronicles 27:10 (captain).

2. feminine 7th year, 2 Kings 11:4 7t. + (noun omitted) Exodus 21:2; Exodus 23:11, also שְׁנַת הַשְּׁבִיעִית (Ges§ 134p) Ezra 7:8 sabbath Leviticus 23:16; time (מַּעַם) Joshua 6:16 #NAME? 1 Kings 18:44.
