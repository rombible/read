Brown-Driver-Briggs
חַשְׁבַּדָּנָה proper name, masculine (etymology dubious) — one of those who stood with Ezra at the reading of the law Nehemiah 8:4; B omits; A Ασαβααμα, ᵐ5L Αβαανας.
