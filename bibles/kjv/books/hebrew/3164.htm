Yachdiy'el: Jahdiel

Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine

יַחְדִּיאֵל proper name, masculine (God giveth joy) a chief man in Manasseh 1 Chronicles 5:24, ᵐ5 Ιελιηλ, A ᵐ5L Ιεδιηλ.
