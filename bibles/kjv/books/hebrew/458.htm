NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from el and melek
Definition
"God is king," the husband of Naomi
NASB Translation
Elimelech (6).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֱלִימֶ֫לֶךְ (& מֶֿ֑לֶח Ruth 2:1) proper name, masculine (God is king, compare מַלְכִּיאֵל) husband of Naomi Ruth 1:2,3; Ruth 2:3; Ruth 4:3,9.
