Definition

NASB Translation
Hoshaiah (3).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
הוֺשַׁעֲיָה proper name, masculine (Yah has saved) —

1 a prince of Judah Nehemiah 12:32, ᵐ5 Ωσαια.

2 father of Azariah or Jezaniah, a chief in time of Jeremiah Jeremiah 42:1; Jeremiah 43:2, ᵐ5 Μαας(ς)αιος, etc.
