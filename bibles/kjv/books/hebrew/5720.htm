II. עָדִין proper name, masculine 1. in ׳בְּנֵי ע, returned exiles Ezra 2:15 = Nehemiah 7:20; Ezra 8:6, ᵐ5. Αδ(δ)ιν, Ηδ(ε)ί, etc.

2 a chief Nehemiah 10:17, ᵐ5. Ηδ(ε)ιν, Αδειν.
