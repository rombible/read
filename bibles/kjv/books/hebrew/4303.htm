Strong's Concordance
matam or matammah: tasty or savory food, dainties
Original Word: מַטְעַם
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: matam or matammah
Phonetic Spelling: (mat-am')
Definition: tasty or savory food, dainties
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from taam
Definition
tasty or savory food, dainties
NASB Translation
delicacies (2), savory dish (3), savory food (3).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[מַטְעָם] noun masculine only plural tasty or savoury food, dainties — מַטְעַמִּים Genesis 27:4,7,9,14,17,31 (all J); suffix מַטְעַמּוֺתָיו Proverbs 23:3; מַטְעַמֹּתָיו Proverbs 23:6.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
dainty meat, savoury meat

Or (feminine) matiammah {mat-am-maw'}; from ta'am; a delicacy -- dainty (meat), savoury meat.

see HEBREW ta'am
