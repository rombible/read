Brown-Driver-Briggs
גֹּ֫מָר proper name

1.masculine a. son of Japhet Genesis 10:2,3= 1 Chronicles 1:5,6; Ezekiel 38:6; LagOnom. Sacr. 2, 95, 2nd ed. 367, see BN 77 proposes גמרים for גמדים Ezekiel 27:11 q. v.; he identification גֹּמֶר with Cappadocia (compare ᵑ7 Ezekiel 27:11, KiepLb. d. alt. Geogr. 91) = Armenian Gamir -χ (with plural ending) see also LagArm. Stud. 32 § 448; compare further Assyrian Gimirrai, SchrKG 157 ff. DlPa 245 f. also LenOr.ii. 1, 332 ff.

2. feminine wife of Hosea, Hosea 1:3.
