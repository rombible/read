NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[חָשֹׁךְ] adjective obscure, low, only masculine plural as substantive; בַּליִֿתְיַצֵּב לִפְנֵי חֲשֻׁכִּים Proverbs 22:29 he shall not stand before obscure men (opposed to לפנימֿלכים).
