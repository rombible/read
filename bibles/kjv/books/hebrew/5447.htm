Brown-Driver-Briggs
סֵ֫בֶל noun [masculine] load, burden; — absolute Nehemiah 4:11; Psalm 81:7 (enforced burden); construct id., = burdensome labour (of corvée) 1 Kings 11:28. — See also סַבָּל.
