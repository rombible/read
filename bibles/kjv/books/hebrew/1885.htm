Definition

NASB Translation
Dathan (10).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
דָּתָן proper name, masculine (compare Assyrian Datana ShlmObelisk, 1. 161) a Reubenite, son of Eliab Numbers 16:1,12,24,25,27 (twice in verse); Numbers 26:9 (twice in verse); Deuteronomy 11:6; Psalm 106:17.
