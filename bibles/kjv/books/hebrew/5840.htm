Brown-Driver-Briggs
עַזְרִיקָם proper name, masculine Εζρεικαν, Εζρικαμ etc.,

1 descendant of David 1 Chronicles 3:23.

4 Levite 1 Chronicles 9:14; Nehemiah 11:15.
