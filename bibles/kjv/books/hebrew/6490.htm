Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
commandment, precept, statute

Or piqqud {pik-kood'}; from paqad; properly, appointed, i.e. A mandate (of God; plural only, collectively, for the Law) -- commandment, precept, statute.

see HEBREW paqad
