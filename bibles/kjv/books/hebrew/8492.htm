Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
new, sweet wine

Or tiyrosh {tee-roshe'}; from yarash in the sense of expulsion; must or fresh grape-juice (as just squeezed out); by implication (rarely) fermented wine -- (new, sweet) wine.

see HEBREW yarash
