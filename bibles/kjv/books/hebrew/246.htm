Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[אֲזֵק] noun [masculine] id., specifically

manacle, only plural manacles אָסוּר בָּאזִקִּים Jeremiah 40:1, and מִןהָֿאזִקִּים אֲשֶׁר עליֿדך Jeremiah 40:4.

זָר see I. זור, זָרָא see below II. זור.

זֵר see III. זוּר.
