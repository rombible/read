Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[מְלִילָה] noun feminine ear of wheat, etc. (perhaps as rubbed or scraped, compare Late Hebrew הַמּוֺלֵל מְלִילוֺת); — only plural absolute מְלִילוֺת Deuteronomy 23:26 (compare Di).
