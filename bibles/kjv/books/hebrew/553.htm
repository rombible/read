◄ 553. amets ►
Strong's Concordance
amets: to be stout, strong, bold, alert
Original Word: אָמַץ
Part of Speech: Verb
Transliteration: amets
Phonetic Spelling: (aw-mats')
Definition: to be stout, strong, bold, alert
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
a prim. root
Definition
to be stout, strong, bold, alert
NASB Translation
conquered (1), courageous (11), determined (1), harden (1), hardened (1), increases (1), made his obstinate (1), made firm (1), made haste (2), made strong (1), makes her strong (1), mighty (1), proved...strong (1), raises (1), strengthen (6), strengthened (3), strong (2), stronger (1), summon (1), supported (1), take courage (2).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
confirm, be courageous of good courage, steadfastly minded, strong, stronger, establish, fortify, harden,

A primitive root; to be alert, physically (on foot) or mentally (in courage) -- confirm, be courageous (of good courage, stedfastly minded, strong, stronger), establish, fortify, harden, increase, prevail, strengthen (self), make strong (obstinate, speed).
