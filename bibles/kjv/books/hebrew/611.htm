Brown-Driver-Briggs
אָסוֺן noun masculine mischief, evil, harm; always absolute without article Genesis 42:4,38 (as subject of קרא), Genesis 44:29 (קרה), Exodus 21:22,23 (היה).

אָסוּךָ see סוך.

אסם (gather, store, Aramaic ).
