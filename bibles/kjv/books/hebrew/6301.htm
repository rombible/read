Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מְּדָהצוּר proper name, masculine (the Rock hath ransomed) Manassite, Numbers 1:10; Numbers 2:20; Numbers 7:54,59; Numbers 10:23 Φαδασσουρ.
