Definition

Hillel (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
הִלֵּל proper name, masculine (he hath praised; compare Late Hebrew proper name הִלֵּל, Hillel) father of a judge of Israel Judges 12:13,15, called הַמִּרְעָתוֺנִי see מִּרְעָתוֺן.
