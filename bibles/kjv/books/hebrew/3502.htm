יִתְרָה noun feminine abundance, riches; — יִתְרָה עָשָׂה Isaiah 15:7 = יִתְרַת עָשָׂה Jeremiah 48:36 (on construct compare DaSynt. § 25) the abundance (which) he hath gotten.
