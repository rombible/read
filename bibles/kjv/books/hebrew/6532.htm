Strong's Concordance
paroketh: a curtain
Original Word: פֹרֶכֶת
Part of Speech: Noun Feminine
Transliteration: paroketh
Phonetic Spelling: (po-reh'-keth)
Definition: a curtain
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from an unused word
Definition
a curtain
NASB Translation
curtain (1), veil (24).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מָּרֹ֫כֶת25 noun feminine curtain, before Most Holy Place, in tabernacle (P) (properly that which habitually shuts off, i.e. *parrâku (LagBN 88) Köii. 1, 201); — ׳פ absolute Exodus 26:31 17t. P; construct הַמָּסָ֑ךְ ׳פ Exodus 35:12; Exodus 39:34; Exodus 40:21; Numbers 4:5, הַקֹּדֶשׁ ׳פ Leviticus 4:6, הָעֵדוּת ׳פ Leviticus 24:3; in temple 2Chronicles 3:14.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
vail

Feminine active participle of the same as perek; a separatrix, i.e. (the sacred) screen -- vail.

see HEBREW perek
