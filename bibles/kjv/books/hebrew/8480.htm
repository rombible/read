II. תַּ֫חַת proper name

1. masculine a. Levite 1 Chronicles 6:9; 1 Chronicles 6:22.

b. Ephraimite 1 Chronicles 7:20 (twice in verse). — Θααθ, Κααθ, etc.

2. location station in wilderness Numbers 33:26,27; Κατααθ.
