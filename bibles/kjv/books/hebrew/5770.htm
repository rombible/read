avan: eye
Original Word: עָוַן
Part of Speech: Verb
Transliteration: avan
Phonetic Spelling: (aw-van')
Definition: eye
Brown-Driver-Briggs
[עִין] verb denominative eye (enviously), look (askance) at; —

Qal Participle (or

Pô`el מ omitted, see Dr), with accusative עוֺיֵן 1 Samuel 18:9 Qr (Kt עון).
