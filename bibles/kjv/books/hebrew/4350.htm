◄ 4350. mekonah ►
Strong's Concordance
mekonah: a fixed resting place, base
Original Word: מְכוֹנָה
Part of Speech: Noun Feminine
Transliteration: mekonah
Phonetic Spelling: (mek-o-naw')
Definition: a fixed resting place, base
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
fem. of makon
Definition
a fixed resting place, base
NASB Translation
foundation (1), pedestal (1), stand (7), stands (15).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מְכוֺנָה, מְכֹנָה25

noun feminine fixed resting-place, base; — absolute ׳מ 1 Kings 7:27 5t.; מְכֹנָה 1 Kings 7:34 (twice in verse); 1 Kings 7:35; suffix מְכֻנָתָתּ Zechariah 5:11 (Ges§ 27, 3. R. 1); plural מְכוֺנוֺת Jeremiah 27:19, מְכֹנֹת 1 Kings 7:27 12t.; suffix מְכוֺנֹתָ֯ו Ezra 3:3; — base or stand of laver כִּיּוֺד 1 Kings 7:27 (twice in verse) + 13 t. 1 Kings 7; 2 Kings 16:17; 2 Kings 25:13,16 = Jeremiah 52:17,20; Jeremiah 27:19; 2Chronicles 4:14 (twice in verse); of altar Ezra 3:3; of the symbolic ephah Zechariah 5:11.
