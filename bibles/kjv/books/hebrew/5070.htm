Nadab: an Israelite name
Original Word: נָדָב

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from nadab
Definition
an Isr. name
NASB Translation
Nadab (20).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
נָדָב proper name, masculine (generous, noble) —

1 eldest son of Aaron Exodus 6:23; Exodus 24:1,9; Exodus 28:1; Leviticus 10:1; Numbers 3:2,4; Numbers 26:60,61; 1 Chronicles 5:29; 1 Chronicles 24:1. ᵐ5 Ναδαβ (so 3, 4).

2 son of Jeroboam I 1 Kings 14:20; 1 Kings 15:25,27,31. ᵐ5 Ναβατ, Ναβαθ, ᵐ5L Ναδαβ.

3 a Jerahmeelite 1 Chronicles 2:28,30.

4 a Gibeonite 1 Chronicles 8:30; 1 Chronicles 9:36.
