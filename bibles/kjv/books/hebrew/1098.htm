בְּלִיל noun masculineIsaiah 30:24 fodder (Aramaic ; compare Arabic moisture of fresh pasture) — בְּלִיל Isaiah 30:24; suffix בְּלִילוֺ Job 6:5; Job 24:6 — fodder (strictly, mixed fodder, farrago) Job 6:5; as growing in field Job 24:6 compare Isaiah 30:24.
