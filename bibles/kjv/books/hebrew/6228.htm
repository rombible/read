Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. עָשָׁן proper name, of a location in Shephelah of Judah Joshua 15:42, in Simeon according to Joshua 19:7; 1 Chronicles 4:32, Levitical city 1 Chronicles 6:44; Ασαν, etc.; = בּוֺר עָשָׁן, q. v. p. 92 above
