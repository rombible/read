Brown-Driver-Briggs
אִבְצָן proper name, masculine judge of Isr. Judges 12:8,10 (LagGN 1891, 19 (אֲ)בֶן צָן, ᵐ5 Ἀβεσσαν, ᵑ6 ).

אבק (Arabic run away (compare LagBN 51)).
