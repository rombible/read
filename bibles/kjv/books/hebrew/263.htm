[אַחֲוָיָה] noun feminine a declaring (properly Infinitive Aph`el, K§ 60. 1)); — construct אַחֲוָיַת אֲחִידָן Daniel 5:12 the declaring of riddles (compare לְהַגִּיד הַחִרדָה Judges 14:14f., Bev).
