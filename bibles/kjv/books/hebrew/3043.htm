Strong's Concordance
Yediael: "knowing God," three Israelites
Original Word: יְדִיעֲאֵל
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Yediael
Phonetic Spelling: (yed-ee-ah-ale')
Definition: "knowing God", three Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from yada and el
Definition
"knowing God," three Isr.
NASB Translation
Jediael (6).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
יְדִיעֲאֵל proper name, masculine (Palmyrene ידיבעל DHM in MV10, 983 compare Sabean proper name with ידע DHMZMG 1875, 612) —

1 a Benjamite 1 Chronicles 7:6,10,11, ᵐ5 Αδειηλ, Ιεδιηλ, etc.

2 one of David's heroes according to 1 Chronicles 11:45 apparently = the Manassite captain of 1 Chronicles 12:21 (Baer; van d. H v.1 Chronicles 12:20), ᵐ5 Ελθειηλ, Ιεδιηλ.

3 a Korahite porter 1 Chronicles 26:2 ᵐ5 Ιδερηλ, Ιεδιηλ.
