Definition

NASB Translation
Palti (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. מַּלְטִי proper name, masculine Φαλτει (perhaps abbreviated from מַּלְטִיאֵל; —

1 Benjamite Numbers 13:9.

2 second husband of Michal 1 Samuel 25:44 (= מַּלְטִיאֵל 2 Samuel 3:15).
