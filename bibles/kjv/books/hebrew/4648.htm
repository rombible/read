Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Mephibosheth

Or Mphibosheth {mef-ee-bo'-sheth}; probably from pa'ah and bosheth; dispeller of shame (i.e. Of Baal); Mephibosheth, the name of two Israelites -- Mephibosheth.
