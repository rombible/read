Original Word: מָאֵן

NAS Exhaustive Concordance

Definition

NASB Translation
refuse (3).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מָאֵן verbal adjective refusing, followed by Infinitive, and always of disobeying ׳י; Exodus 7:27; Exodus 9:2; Exodus 10:4 (all J), Jeremiah 38:21.
