NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from bayith and emeq
Definition
"valley house," a place on the border of Asher
NASB Translation
Beth-emek (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
בֵּית הָעֵ֫מֶק proper name, of a location on border of Asher (valley-house) Joshua 19:27. Surveyi. 145 compare `Amka, 7 miles northeast of Akko (but see RobBR iii. 103, 108).
