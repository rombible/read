Definition

NASB Translation
majesty (3).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[הֲדַר] noun [masculine] honour, majesty, of king; — emphatic הַדְרָא Daniel 5:18; suffix הַדְרִי Daniel 4:27; Daniel 4:33 (where Behrm plausibly (after Theod.) הַדְרֵת [ᵑ7 הֲדַר return]).
