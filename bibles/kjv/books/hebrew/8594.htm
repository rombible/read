Brown-Driver-Briggs
[תַּעַרֻבָה] noun feminine pledge; — only plural in phrase בְּנֵי הַתַּעַרֻבוֺת 2 Kings 14:14 i.e. hostages = 2 Chronicles 25:24.

From arab; suretyship, i.e. (concretely) a pledge -- + hostage.
