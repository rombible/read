bewray self, that are bidden, call for, forth, self, upon, cry unto, be famous,

A primitive root (rather identical with qara' through the idea of accosting a person met); to call out to (i.e. Properly, address by name, but used in a wide variety of applications) -- bewray (self), that are bidden, call (for, forth, self, upon), cry (unto), (be) famous, guest, invite, mention, (give) name, preach, (make) proclaim(- ation), pronounce, publish, read, renowned, say.
