מַכְנַדְּבַי proper name, masculine one of those who had taken strange wives Ezra 10:40 (GrayExpos. Times, Feb. 1889, p. 233 proposes מכרנבו = possession of Nebo); ᵐ5 Μαξαδναβου, A Μαχναδααβου; ᵐ5L Ναδαβου.
