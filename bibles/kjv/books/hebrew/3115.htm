יוֺכֶ֫בֶד proper name, feminine ᵐ5 Ἰωχαβεδ (׳י is glory; compare Phoenician כבדמלקרת) daughter of Levi, wife of Amram and mother of Moses Exodus 6:20; Numbers 26:59 (both P).
