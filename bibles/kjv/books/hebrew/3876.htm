Lot: Abraham's nephew
Original Word: לוֹט
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Lot
Phonetic Spelling: (lote)
Definition: Abraham's nephew
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from lut
Definition
Abraham's nephew
NASB Translation
lot (32), Lot's (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. לוֺט33 proper name, masculine Lot son of Haran, and nephew of Abram; ᵐ5 Λωτ; — Genesis 11:27,31; Genesis 12:4,5; Genesis 13:1,5,7,8,10,11 (twice in verse); Genesis 13:12; Genesis 13:14; Genesis 14:12; Genesis 14:16 15t. Genesis 19; בְּנֵי לוֺט = Moabites Deuteronomy 2:9; = Ammonites Deuteronomy 2:19; = both, Psalm 83:9.
