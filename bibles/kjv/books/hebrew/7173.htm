Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. קַרְקַע proper name, of a location (floor); — with article + ה locative, הַקַּרְקָ֑עָה Joshua 15:13, on extreme southern border of Judah; τὴν κατὰ δυσμὰς Καδης.
