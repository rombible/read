[יַקִּיר] adjective honourable, difficult; —

1 honourable, emphatic יַקִּירָא Ezra 4:10.

2 difficult (compare Biblical Hebrew כָּבֵד adjective 1 d), feminine singular יַקִּירָה Daniel 2:11.
