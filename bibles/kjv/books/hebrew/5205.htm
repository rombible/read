נִיד noun masculine:(see below) quivering motion of lips; — only construct שְׂפָתַי ׳נ Job 16:5 the motion of my lips (in expressing sympathy). — ᵑ0 makes subject of יחשׂך, but read probably אחשׂך, see חשׂך.
