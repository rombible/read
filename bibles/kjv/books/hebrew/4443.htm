Brown-Driver-Briggs
מַלְכִּירָם proper name, masculine son of Jeconiah (= Jehoiakin) (my king (= Yahweh) is high, compare GrayProp. N. 146 f.; Phoenician proper name מלכרם); — 1 Chronicles 3:18. ᵐ5 Μελχ(ε)ιραμ.
