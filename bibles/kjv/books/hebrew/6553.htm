Brown-Driver-Briggs
מִּרְעָתוֺנִי adjective, of a people of foregoing, Judges 12:13,15; 1 Chronicles 27:14 compare מִּרְעָחֹנִי 2 Samuel 23:30 = 1 Chronicles 11:31.

II. פרע (√ of following; compare Syriac sprout; Assyrian pir'u, sprout, progeny, pirtu, hair of head; late Arabic sprout (Schroeter in MeiArchiv. i. 176 Dozyii.256), long hair of woman; — on this and following √ see SchulthHom. Wurx. 56 NöZMG iiv (1900), 154).
