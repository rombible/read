Strong's Concordance
Maakathi: inhab. of Maacah
Original Word: מַעֲכָתִי
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: Maakathi
Phonetic Spelling: (mah-ak-aw-thee')
Definition: inhab. of Maacah
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from Maakah
Definition
inhab. of Maacah
NASB Translation
Maacathite (4), Maacathites (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מַעֲכָתִי adjective, of a people ׳הַמּ 1 Chronicles 4:19; so הַמַּעֲכָתִי בֶּןֿ 2 Samuel 23:34 of one of David's heroes, We deletes בן; read probably ׳בֵּיח הַמּ Klo HPS; see also Dr; of associate of Ishmael the Judaean 2 Kings 25:23 = Jeremiah 40:8; collective = the Maachathites Deuteronomy 3:14; Joshua 12:5; Joshua 13:11,13 (all + הַגְּשׁוּרִי; compare מַעֲכָה 3). — ᵐ5 Μαχ(ε)ι, Μαχατ(ε)ι, Μααχαθι, etc.
