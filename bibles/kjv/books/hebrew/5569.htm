Brown-Driver-Briggs
סָמָר adjective bristling, rough; — ׳יֶלֶק ס Jeremiah 51:27 bristling locust, perhaps with allusion to horn-like sheaths enclosing wings of the pupa, see DrJoel and Amos, 58 (on formation compare LagBN 50).
