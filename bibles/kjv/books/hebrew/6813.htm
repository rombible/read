[צָעַן] verb wander, travel (Arabic journey, go away; probably connected with Assyrian ƒênu, Ethiopic , Aramaic טְעַן, Palmyrene טען, all load (beast, etc.) = Hebrew טָעַן Genesis 45:17; Aramaic טְעַן, also bear, carry); —
