Strong's Concordance
Zakkur: the name of several Israelites
Original Word: זַכּוּר
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Zakkur
Phonetic Spelling: (zaw-koor')
Definition: the name of several Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from zakar
Definition
the name of several Isr.
NASB Translation
Zaccur (9).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
זַכּוּר proper name, masculine ᵐ5 Ζακχυρ, Ζακχουρ, etc.: —

1 a Reubenite Numbers 13:4 (P).

2 a Simeonite 1 Chronicles 4:26.

3 Levites:

a. 1 Chronicles 24:27.

b. an Asaphite 1 Chronicles 25:2 (Σακχους), 1 Chronicles 25:10 (Ζαχχουθ), Nehemiah 12:35 (Ζακχουρ), compare זִכְרִי above 3c. c. Nehemiah 10:13; compare Nehemiah 13:13

4 companion of Ezra, Ezra 8:14 Qr (Kt זבוד, compare above).

5 contemporary of Nehemiah, Nehemiah 3:2.
