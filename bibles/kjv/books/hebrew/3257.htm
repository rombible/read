[יָע] noun [masculine] shovel (ᵑ7Jer יָעֵי Exodus 27:3) — plural הַיָּעִים Exodus 38:3 7t.; יָעָיו Exodus 27:3; — utensils for cleaning altar, shovels Exodus 27:3; Exodus 38:3; Numbers 4:14 (all P), 1 Kings 7:40,45; 2 Kings 25:14; 2Chronicles 4:11,16; Jeremiah 52:18 (only in lists of utensils).
