Phonetic Spelling: (yeh-ho-naw-dawb')

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from Yhvh and nadab
Definition
"the LORD is noble," a Rechabite, also a nephew of David
NASB Translation
Jehonadab (3), Jonadab (12).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
יְהוֺנָדָב, יוֺנָדָב proper name, masculine (׳י is noble, or is liberal, or hath impelled, compare נְדַבְיָה) —

1 ׳יְהוֺ son of Rechab & chief of the Rechabites (ᵐ5 Ἰωαναδαβ) 2 Kings 10:15 (twice in verse); 2 Kings 10:23; Jeremiah 35:8,14,16,18; = ׳יוֺ Jeremiah 35:6,10,19.

2 ׳יְהוֺ nephew of David (ᵐ5 Ἰωναδαβ, Ἰωναδαμ; ᵐ5L Ἰωαναθαν) 2 Samuel 13:5; = ׳יוֺ 2 Samuel 13:3 (twice in verse); 2 Samuel 13:32,35; (apparently called Jonathan 2 Samuel 21:21; 1 Chronicles 20:7, compare יְהוֺנָתָן

3)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Jehonadab, Jonadab

From Yhovah and nadab; Jehovah-largessed; Jehonadab, the name of an Israelite and of an Arab -- Jehonadab, Jonadab. Compare Yawnadab.
