Abiram: "exalted father," an Israelite name
Original Word: אֲבִירָם
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Abiram
Phonetic Spelling: (ab-ee-rawm')
Definition: "exalted father", an Israelite name
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from ab and rum
Definition
"exalted father," an Isr. name
NASB Translation
Abiram (11).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֲבִירָם proper name, masculine ((the) Exalted One is (my) father (see BaeRel 156) compare Assyrian Aburamu (?) KAT2479 compare DlL2, p. 91, 1. 225).

1 a Reubenite, son of Eliab Numbers 16:1,12,24,25,27 (twice in verse); Numbers 26:9; Deuteronomy 11:6; Psalm 106:17.

2 son of Hiel the Bethelite 1 Kings 16:34. compare also following.
