Strong's Concordance
Amnon: "faithful," two Israelites
Original Word: אַמְנוֹן
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: Amnon
Phonetic Spelling: (am-nohn')
Definition: "faithful", two Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from aman
Definition
"faithful," two Isr.
NASB Translation
Amnon (25), Amnon's (3).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אַמְנוֺן proper name, masculine (faithful)

1 eldest son of David 2 Samuel 3:2; 2 Samuel 13; = אֲמִינוֺן 2 Samuel 13:20; = אַמְנֹן 1 Chronicles 3:1.

2 a son of Shimon 1 Chronicles 4:20.

אֲמִינוֺן diminutive, so Ew§ 167 a compare Wi. § 269, or text error; compare Dr 2 Samuel 13:20, see אַמְנוֺן.
