Brown-Driver-Briggs
יָרִיב proper name, masculine (he contendeth or taketh (our) part, conducteth (our) case; compare Sabean ירב Hal615); —

1 son of Simeon 1 Chronicles 2:24, Ιαρειν[μ, β], = יָכִין

1, see below כון.
