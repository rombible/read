Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[נָחֵת] adjective descending; — masculine plural נְחִתִּים (on form compare Köii. 81), 2 Kings 6:9 (to battle); but read probably with Th Klo Benz after ᵐ5 נֶחְבִּים hidden.
