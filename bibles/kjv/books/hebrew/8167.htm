Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. [שְׂעִירָה] proper name, of a location, probably in southeast Ephraim (= goat, Thes); — with ה locative הַשְּׂעִי֫רָתָה Judges 3:26; Σε(Τ)ειρωθα, ᵐ5L Σηρωθα.
