Definition

NASB Translation
abundant (1), glutted (1), satisfied (2), satisfied* (1), sufficient (1), what does not satisfy (1).

to have enough, till

Feminine of soba'; satiety -- (to have) enough, X till...be full, (un-)satiable, satisfy, X sufficiently.
