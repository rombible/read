Strong's Concordance
Chushay: two Israelites
Original Word: חיּשַׁי
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Chushay
Phonetic Spelling: (khoo-shah'-ee)
Definition: two Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from chush
Definition
two Isr.
NASB Translation
Hushai (14).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
חוּשַׁי proper name, masculine 1. הָאַרְכִּי ׳ח 2 Samuel 15:32; so 2 Samuel 17:5,14, called רֵעֶה דוד 2 Samuel 15:37 friend of David, so 2 Samuel 16:16a; compare 2 Samuel 16:16b; 2 Samuel 16:17,18; 1 Chronicles 27:33; also 1 Samuel 17:6,7,8,15.

2 1 Kings 4:16 father of one of Solomon's officers.
