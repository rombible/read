קוֺל (sometimes, with prefix and suffix, קֹל)506

noun masculineGenesis 39:14 sound, voice; — absolute קוֺל Genesis 4:10 + (הַקֹּל Genesis 27:22; Genesis 45:16); construct קוֺל Genesis 3:8 + (וְקֹל Exodus 19:16, לְקֹל Exodus 4:8 (twice in verse)); suffix קוֺלִי 1 Samuel 26:17 +, קוֺלְךָ Judges 18:25 +, etc. (קֹלִי Genesis 22:18, קֹלֶ֑ךָ Exodus 3:18, etc.); plural (usually of thunder) קֹלֹת Exodus 9:23 4t., קוֺלֹת Exodus 20:18 (Gi Exodus 20:15), קֹלוֺת 1 Samuel 2:17 5t.; —

1 sound: especially

a. of human voice Joshua 6:10; 2 Kings 7:10; in speech Genesis 27:22 (twice in verse); 1 Samuel 1:13; 1 Samuel 24:17; 1 Samuel 26:17 (twice in verse); recognized (הִכִּיר) 1 Samuel 26:17; Jeremiah 18:3; ׳יְפֵה ק Ezekiel 33:32 pleasant of voice, compare Songs 2:14 (twice in verse); Songs 8:13; in singing Exodus 32:18; 2 Samuel 19:36; Isaiah 52:8, calling Judges 9:7, shouting Exodus 32:17; 1 Samuel 4:6 (twice in verse); 1 Kings 1:40; Ezra 3:13, rejoicing Jeremiah 7:34 (4 t. in verse) = Jeremiah 16:9; Jeremiah 25:10; Jeremiah 33:11, praise Psalm 66:8; Psalm 26:7; Joel 2:10, laughter Jeremiah 30:19, weeping Genesis 21:16; Judges 2:4; 1 Samuel 11:4; Ruth 1:9,14; Ezra 3:13, outcry Genesis 39:15,18; 1 Samuel 4:14; Jeremiah 8:19, distress, lament Jeremiah 9:18; Ezekiel 27:30; Zechariah 11:3; Psalm 102:6, supplication Psalm 28:2; Psalm 28:6; Psalm 31:23; Psalm 86:6; adverb אֶחָד ׳ק with one voice Exodus 24:3; 2Chronicles 5:13, גָּדוֺל ׳ק loud voice 2 Samuel 15:23 + (compare ׳ג ׳בְּק Genesis 39:14; 1 Samuel 28:12 +), רָם ׳ק Deuteronomy 27:14; ׳נָשָׂא ק Genesis 21:16 + (see נָשָׂא 1b (5)), ׳הֵרִים ק Genesis 39:15 + (see דום Hiph`il); on ׳ק as independent noun, followed by subject + verb of calling Psalm 3:5; Psalm 27:7; Psalm 142:2 see Ges§ 144m

b. of ׳י Genesis 3:8,10; Isaiah 6:8; Exodus 19:19; 1 Kings 19:3; Micah 6:9; Ezekiel 10:5 (compare also 2b) +; adverb גָּדוֺל ׳ק Deuteronomy 5:19; Ezekiel 9:1; דְּמָמָה דַקָּה ׳ק 1 Kings 19:12.

c. of seraph Isaiah 6:4.

d. of angel Daniel 8:16 (אָדָם ׳ק i.e. in human speech, compare Bev); דְּבָרָיו ׳ק Daniel 10:6,9 (twice in verse).

e. of animals: bleating of sheep 1 Samuel 15:14, lowing of cattle 1 Samuel 15:14; Jeremiah 9:9, neighing of horses Jeremiah 8:16, roaring of lion Amos 3:4; Jeremiah 2:15 +, hissing of serpent Jeremiah 46:22 (in simile), singing of birds Nahum 2:8; Zephaniah 2:14; Psalm 104:12; Songs 2:12; Ecclesiastes 12:4.

f. exclamation, at beginning of clause, a sound of...! = hark! Genesis 4:10; Isaiah 13:4; Isaiah 40:3; Isaiah 52:8; Jeremiah 4:15; Jeremiah 10:22; Jeremiah 50:28; Songs 2:8; Songs 5:2 (Ges§ 146b).

2 sound:

a. of instrument, especially (הַ)שֹּׁפָר ׳ק Exodus 9:16; Exodus 20:18 (Gi Exodus 20:15) Amos 2:2; 1 Kings 1:41 +, ׳חֲצֹצְרוֺת וגו2Chronicles 5:13, כִּנּוֺר Ezekiel 26:13, עוּנָב Job 21:12; of מַּעֲמֹנִים on high priest's robe Exodus 28:35.

b. thunder-clap or peal (compare Dr 1 Samuel 12:17), ׳קוֺל י (or קוֺלוֺ) Amos 1:2; Isaiah 30:30,31; Jeremiah 10:13 = Jeremiah 51:16, Joel 2:11; Joel 4:16; Psalm 18:14 = 2 Samuel 22:14; Psalm 29:3; Psalm 29:7; Psalm 46:7; Job 37:4 (compare 1 Samuel 7:10; Psalm 77:18; Psalm 77:19; Psalm 104:7), so especially plural absolute (see above) Exodus 9:23,28,29,33,34; Exodus 19:16; Exodus 20:18 (Gi Exodus 20:15), 1 Samuel 7:10; 1 Samuel 12:17,18; Job 28:26 = Job 38:25.

c. קוֺל רַגְלֶיהָ 1 Kings 14:6 compare 2 Kings 6:32, and (probably) Genesis 3:8; also (הַ)צְּעָדָה ׳ק 2 Samuel 5:24 = 1 Chronicles 14:15; of runners 2 Kings 11:13; 2Chronicles 23:12.

d. stamping of hoofs Jeremiah 47:3; Ezekiel 26:10 ( +chariots, etc.), compare 1 Kings 1:41.

e. of chariots Nahum 3:2; Joel 2:5; wheels Ezekiel 3:13; whip Nahum 3:2.

f. of sea, and great waters Habakkuk 3:10; Ezekiel 1:24; Ezekiel 43:2; Psalm 93:3; Psalm 93:4, compare Jeremiah 50:42 (in simile); צִנּוֺרֶיךָ ׳ק Psalm 42:8.

g. earthquake Ezekiel 37:7.

h. a fall Jeremiah 49:21; Ezekiel 26:15; Ezekiel 31:16.

i. of a multitude 1 Samuel 4:14; Isaiah 13:4; Ezekiel 23:42.

j. din of war Exodus 32:17; Jeremiah 50:22,46; Jeremiah 51:55; Lamentations 2:7.

k. of wings Ezekiel 1:24; Ezekiel 3:13; Ezekiel 10:5.

l. flame Joel 2:5; crackling of thorns Ecclesiastes 7:6; rustling of leaves Leviticus 26:36.

m. millstones Jeremiah 25:10; Ecclesiastes 12:4.

3 of articulate speech, thing said:

a. (1) often of human advice, command, entreaty Genesis 3:17; Genesis 4:23; Exodus 3:18; Exodus 4:1,9; Deuteronomy 1:45; Deuteronomy 21:18 (twice in verse); Deuteronomy 21:20; 1 Samuel 2:25; 1 Samuel 8:7,9; 2 Samuel 12:18; דִּבְרֵיכֶם ׳ק Deuteronomy 1:34; Deuteronomy 5:25; even of written words 2 Kings 10:6; so also הָאֹת ׳ק Exodus 4:8 (twice in verse) voice (teaching) of the sign. (2) of a report Genesis 45:16; Ecclesiastes 10:20. (3) of a proclamation Exodus 36:6 (P), 2 Chronicles 24:9; 30:5; 36:22 = Ezra 1:1; Ezra 10:7; Nehemiah 8:15. (4) אָלָה ׳ק Leviticus 5:1 utterance of adjuration.

b. often (especially Deuteronomy, Jeremiah) of words of ׳י Genesis 22:18; Genesis 26:5; Exodus 5:2; Exodus 15:26; Exodus 19:5; Deuteronomy 8:20; Deuteronomy 9:23; Deuteronomy 13:15; Deuteronomy 13:19 +, Judges 2:2,20; Judges 6:10; 1 Samuel 12:14,15; 1 Samuel 15:1,19,20,22; Jeremiah 3:13,25; Jeremiah 7:23 +; of ׳מַלְאַךְ י Exodus 23:21,22. — קֹל Jeremiah 3:9 see below קלל.

קֹל noun [masculine] lightness, frivolity (so Vrss Ki Gie; Gf thinks = קָלוֺן; > = voice); — מִקֹּל זְנוּנָהּ Jeremiah 3:9. — קֹל voice, see קוֺל.

קול (√ of following; Biblical Hebrew קול, קוֺל).



Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
aloud, bleating, crackling, cry out, fame, lightness, lowing, noise,

Or qol {kole}; from an unused root meaning to call aloud; a voice or sound -- + aloud, bleating, crackling, cry (+ out), fame, lightness, lowing, noise, + hold peace, (pro-)claim, proclamation, + sing, sound, + spark, thunder(-ing), voice, + yell.
