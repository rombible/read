NASB Translation
Abronah (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
עַבְרֹנָה proper name, of a location a station of Israelites in wilderness, one march from Ezion-geber, on Gulf of Akaba Numbers 33:34,35 (P); ᵐ5 (Σ)εβρωνα.
