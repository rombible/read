Strong's Concordance
ochori: another
Original Word: אָחֲרִי
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: ochori
Phonetic Spelling: (okh-or-ee')
Definition: another
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
fem. of ochoran, q.v.

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אָחֳרִי adjective feminine another: see אָחֳרָן. below
