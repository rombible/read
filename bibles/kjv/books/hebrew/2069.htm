Zebadyah or Zebadyahu: "Yah has bestowed," the name of several Israelites

from zabad and Yah

זְבַדְיָהוּ, זְבַדְיָה proper name, masculine (׳י hath bestowed, compare יְהוֺזָבָד, & references, also זַבְדִּיאֵל above) — זְבַדְיָהוּ

1 Levites,

a. 1 Chronicles 8:15;

b. 1 Chronicles 8:17.

4 one of David's men 1 Chronicles 12:8.

5 an officer of David 1 Chronicles 27:7.

6 a returning exile Ezra 8:8.

7 one of priestly house Ezra 10:20.
