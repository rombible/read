אֶדְרֶ֫עִי proper name, of a location a chief city of Bashan אֶדְרֶ֖עִי Joshua 19:37; אֶדְרֶ֑עִי Numbers 21:33 = Deuteronomy 3:1,10; בְּעַשְׁתָּרֹת בְּאֶדְרֶ֑עִי Deuteronomy 1:4; בְּעַשְׁתָּרֹת וּבְאֶדְרֶ֑עִי Joshua 12:4; Joshua 13:12; compare Joshua 13:31; — on identity of all these compare Di Deuteronomy 3:10; modern Der`ât, 7 2-Janhours west-northwest of Bosra; Euseb.
