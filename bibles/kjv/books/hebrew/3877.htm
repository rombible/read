Lotan: a son of Seir
Original Word: לוֹטָן
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Lotan
Phonetic Spelling: (lo-tawn')

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from lut
Definition
a son of Seir
NASB Translation
Lotan (5), Lotan's (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
לוֺטָן proper name, masculine ᵐ5 Λωταν; a son of שֵׂעִיר Genesis 36:20,22 = 1 Chronicles 1:38,39, and father of חֹרִי etc. Genesis 36:22 = 1 Chronicles 1:39; called a chief (אַלוּף) of הַחֹרִי Genesis 36:29.
