Brown-Driver-Briggs
צָלִי adjective et

noun masculine roasted, roast; — construct (as adjective) צְלִיאֵֿשׁ (P) of flesh (בָּשָׂר) Exodus 12:8,9 (opposed to מְבֻשָׁל בַּסָּ֑יִם); absolute as substantive cognate Isaiah 44:16 see [ צָלָה].
