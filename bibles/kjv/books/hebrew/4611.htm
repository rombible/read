◄ 4611. maalal ►
Strong's Concordance
maalal: a deed, practice
Original Word: מַעֲלָל
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: maalal
Phonetic Spelling: (mah-al-awl')
Definition: a deed, practice
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from alal
Definition
a deed, practice
NASB Translation
actions (2), dealings (1), deeds (34), doings (1), practices (2), works (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[מַעֲלָל]41 noun masculineNehemiah 9:35 deed, practice; — only plural מַעֲלָלִים 1 Samuel 25:3; construct מַעַלְלֵי Psalm 77:12; Psalm 78:1; suffix מַעֲלָלֶיךָ Deuteronomy 28:20, מַעֲלָלָיו Hosea 4:9 +, מַעַלְלֵיכֶם Isaiah 1:16 +, מַעַלְלֵיהֶם Isaiah 3:10 +, etc.; —

1 usually (especially Jeremiah 17 t.) bad practices of men, רַע מַעֲלָלִים 1 Samuel 25:3 he was evil in his practices; Judges 2:19 ("" דֶּרֶךְ), Hosea 5:5; Hosea 7:2; Isaiah 3:8; Jeremiah 11:18; Psalm 106:29; Psalm 106:39 ("" מַעֲשֵׂיהֶם); הָרָעִים ׳מ Nehemiah 9:35; ׳רֹעַ מ the evil of your (their, etc.) practices Hosea 9:15; Isaiah 1:16; Jeremiah 4:4; Jeremiah 21:12 (suffix 3 masculine plural Kt, 2 masculine plural Qr), +; הָרָעִים ׳מ Zechariah 1:4 Qr (+ הָרָעִים ׳ד; Kt מעליליהם), אֲשֶׁר לֹא טוֺבִים ׳מ Ezekiel 36:31 (+ id.); ׳הֵרֵעוּ מ Micah 3:4; ׳מְּרִי מ, i.e. their consequences, Isaiah 3:10; Jeremiah 17:10; Jeremiah 21:14; Jeremiah 32:19 (+ דֶּרֶךְ).

2 deeds of ׳י Psalm 77:12, of אֵל Psalm 78:7; compare Micah 2:7.

3 acts, in General, of youth Proverbs 20:11 ("" מָּעֳלוֺ).

[מעליל], מעליליהם Zechariah 1:4 Kt, see foregoing 1
