Strong's Concordance
Uzza or Uzzah: four Israelites
Original Word: עֻזָּא
Part of Speech: proper name, masculine; proper name, masculine
Transliteration: Uzza or Uzzah
Phonetic Spelling: (ooz-zaw')
Definition: four Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from azaz
Definition
four Isr.
NASB Translation
Uzza (9), Uzzah (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
עֻזָּא proper name, masculine Οζα, Αζα :

1 driver of the ark 2 Samuel 6:3 = עֻזָּה 2 Samuel 6:6; 2 Samuel 6:7; 2 Samuel 6:8 = עֻזָּא 1 Chronicles 13:7,9,10,11.

2 in גַּןעֻֿזָּא burial-place of Manasseh and Amon 2 Kings 21:18,26 (׳ע perhaps = עֻזִּיָּה, compare StaG i. 569, ii. 679 Kit).

3 Benjamite name 1 Chronicles 8:7.

4 head of family of returned exiles Ezra 2:49 = Nehemiah 7:51.

עֻזָּה proper name, masculine 1. Levite name 1 Chronicles 6:14, Οζ(ι)α, Αζα.

2 see עֻזָּא.
