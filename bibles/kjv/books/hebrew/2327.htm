NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from chub
Definition
a place N. of Damascus
NASB Translation
Hobah (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
חוֺבָה proper name, of a location Genesis 14:15, north of Damascus; modern –oba, 20 hours north of Dam., according to WetzstDel Gn. ed. 4, 561 ff. so Di.
