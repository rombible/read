Brown-Driver-Briggs
[דָּגַר] verb gather together as a brood (compare Aramaic דְּגַר heap together; so Nasar.) —

Qal Perfect3masculine singular דָּ֨גָר Jeremiah 17:11; 3feminine singular דָּֽגְרָה Isaiah 34:15 — gather together into its shadow Isaiah 34:15 (of קִמֹּז, q. v.), דָּגָר וְלֹא יָלָד֑ Jeremiah 17:11 (of partridge; simile of one getting riches unjustly).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
gather, sit

A primitive root, to brood over eggs or young -- gather, sit.
