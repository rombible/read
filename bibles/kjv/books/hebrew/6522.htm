Strong's Concordance
Perizzi: a people in the land of Canaan
Original Word: פְרִזִי
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: Perizzi
Phonetic Spelling: (per-iz-zee')
Definition: a people in the land of Canaan
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
perhaps from the same as perazah
Definition
a people in the land of Canaan
NASB Translation
Perizzite (13), Perizzites (10).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מְּרִזִּי23 adjective, of a people Perizzite (possibly, but not certainly, connected with above √); — only ׳הַמּ as proper name collective ὁ (οἱ) Φερεζαῖος (#NAME?), of ancient inhabitants of Canaan, + הַכְּנַעֲנִי [q. v.] Genesis 13:7; Genesis 34:30 (J), Judges 1:4,5; also in list of peoples dispossessed by Israel Genesis 15:20; Exodus 3:8 17t.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Perizzite

For praziy; inhabitant of the open country; a Perizzite, one of the Canaanitish tribes -- Perizzite.

see HEBREW praziy
