Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
חָסֹן adjective strong, of the Amorite Amos 2:9 (כָּאַלּוֺנִים ׳ח, strong as the oaks); הֶחָסוֺן as substantive the strong one Isaiah 1:31.
