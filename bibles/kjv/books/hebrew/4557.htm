Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
abundance, account, all, infinite, tale, telling, time

From caphar; a number, definite (arithmetical) or indefinite (large, innumerable; small, a few); also (abstractly) narration -- + abundance, account, X all, X few, (in-)finite, (certain) number(-ed), tale, telling, + time.
