Assir: an Israelite name

Definition

NASB Translation
Assir (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אַסִּיר proper name, masculine son of Korah Exodus 6:24; 1 Chronicles 6:7; called son of Ebiasaph v.1 Chronicles 6:8; v.1 Chronicles 6:22.
