Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
שָׂפָם noun [masculine] moustache (compare N Syriac rete; on meaning and form see Thes Ol408 Köii. 73); — absolute ׳שׂ Micah 3:7 +, suffix שְׂפָמוֺ 2 Samuel 19:25; — moustache: ׳עָשָׂה שׂ 2 Samuel 19:25 (ᵐ5 μύστάξ) i.e. trim it; ׳עָטָה עַלשֿׂ Micah 3:7; Ezekiel 24:17,22; Leviticus 13:45 (P; see I. עטה 1).

שִׂמַּח see III. ספח. מִשְׂמָּח see II. ספח.
