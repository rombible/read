Definition

NASB Translation
Besai (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
בֵּסַי proper name, masculine head of a family of Nethinim, בְּנֵיבֵֿסַי Nehemiah 7:52 = בֵֿסָ֑י׳ב Ezra 2:49.

בסר (be too early, Arabic , compare Aramaic בְּסִירָה half-ripe).
