NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
(Aramaic) corresponding to illi
Definition
"highest," a name of God
NASB Translation
most high (10).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[עִלָּי K§ 59 l) e)] adjective highest; — always emphatic עליא Kt, עִלָּאָה Qr, the Most High God: ׳אֱלָהָא ע Daniel 3:26; Daniel 3:32; Daniel 5:18,21; ׳ע alone, the Most High, Daniel 4:14; Daniel 4:21; Daniel 4:22; Daniel 4:29; Daniel 4:31; Daniel 7:25 (compare Nabataean proper name עליאל Lzb341 SAC91).
