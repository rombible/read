Definition

NASB Translation
Hachmoni (1), Hachmonite (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
חַכְמוֺנִי proper name, masculine (wise) —

1 father of one of David's warriors 1 Chronicles 11:11.

2 father of the tutor of David's sons 1 Chronicles 27:32.
