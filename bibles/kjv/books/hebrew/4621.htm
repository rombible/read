Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מַעֲצָד noun [masculine] axe; — Jeremiah 10:3; in Isaiah 44:12 probably strike out (so Du CheHpt Skinner).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
ax, tongs

From an usused root meaning to hew; an axe -- ax, tongs.
