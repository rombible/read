Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
יוֺעָשׁ proper name, masculine ᵐ5 Ἰωας (׳י hath aided (?; compare Arabic ); compare Sabean אלעֿותֿ DHM in MV) —

1 a Benjamite, son of Becher 1 Chronicles 7:8.

2 an officer under David 1 Chronicles 27:28.
