יְדִדוּת noun feminine love = object of love; — only construct יְדִדוּת נַפְשִׁי Jeremiah 12:7 (׳י speaks) love (i.e. beloved) of my soul.

dearly beloved

From ydiyd; properly, affection; concretely, a darling object -- dearly beloved.
