רְנָנִים noun [masculine] plural bird of piercing cries, i.e. ostrich, according to ᵑ9 Bo Di and most, ׳כְּנַף ר Job 39:13, but read probably יְעֵנִים Hoffm Bu Du (as Lamentations 4:3 Qr), see [ יָעֵן].
