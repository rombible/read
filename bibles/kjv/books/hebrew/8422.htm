NASB Translation
Tubal (8).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
תּוּבַל proper name, of a territory and people in east Asia Minor, perhaps nearly = Cappadocia ( = Assyrian Tabalu DlPa 250 f. COTGenesis 10. 2, cf DiGenesis 10:2); — ׳ת Isaiah 66:19; Ezekiel 27:13, תֻּבַל Ezekiel 32:26, תֻּבָ֫ל Genesis 10:2 = 1 Chronicles 1:5; Ezekiel 38:2,3; Ezekiel 39:1; Θοβελ.

Or Tubal {too-bal'}; probably of foreign derivation; Tubal, a postdiluvian patriarch and his posterity -- Tubal.
