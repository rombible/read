Word Origin

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
equity, right thing, uprightness

Feminine of nakoach; properly, straightforwardness, i.e. (figuratively) integrity, or (concretely) a truth -- equity, right (thing), uprightness.

see HEBREW nakoach
