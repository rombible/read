I. [יַעְרָה] noun feminine honeycomb, only construct הַדְּבָשׁ יַעְרַת 1 Samuel 14:27 (see II. יער). — יְעָרוֺת see I. יַעַר.

Feminine of ya'ar, and meaning the same -- (honey-) comb, forest.
