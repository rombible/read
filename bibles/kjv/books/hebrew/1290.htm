Strong's Concordance
berek: the knee
Original Word: בֶּרֶךְ
Part of Speech: Noun Feminine
Transliteration: berek
Phonetic Spelling: (beh'-rek)
Definition: the knee
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from barak
Definition
the knee
NASB Translation
feeble* (1), knee (1), kneeled* (1), kneels* (1), knees (20), lap (1).
