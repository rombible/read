Yamin: three Israelites
Original Word: יָמִין

Definition

NASB Translation
Jamin (6).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. יָמִין proper name, masculine 1. son of Simeon Genesis 46:10; Exodus 6:15; Numbers 26:12; 1 Chronicles 4:24.

2 man of Judah 1 Chronicles 2:27.

3 Levite (?) name, time of Ezra Nehemiah 8:7.
