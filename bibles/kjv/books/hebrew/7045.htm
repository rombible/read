Strong's Concordance
qelalah: a curse
Original Word: קלָלָה
Part of Speech: Noun Feminine
Transliteration: qelalah
Phonetic Spelling: (kel-aw-law')
Definition: a curse
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from qalal
Definition
a curse
NASB Translation
accursed (1), curse (27), curses (2), cursing (3), imprecation (2).
