Yachaziel: "God sees," the name of several Israelites
Original Word: יַחֲזִיאֵל
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Yachaziel
Phonetic Spelling: (yakh-az-ee-ale')
Definition: "God sees", the name of several Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from chazah and el
Definition
"God sees," the name of several Isr.
NASB Translation
Jahaziel (6).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
יַחֲזִיאֵל proper name, masculine (El sees) —

1 Benjamite warrior of David 1 Chronicles 12:4.

2 priest of David 1 Chronicles 16:6.

3 a Levite 1 Chronicles 23:19; 1 Chronicles 24:23.

4 a Levite of the sons of Asaph2Chronicles 20:14.

5 ancestor of one of the families of the restoration Ezra 8:5.
