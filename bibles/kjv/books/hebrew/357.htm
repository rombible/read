Strong's Concordance
Ayyalon: "deer," the name of several cities in Palestine
Original Word: אַיָּלוֹן
Part of Speech: Proper Name Location
Transliteration: Ayyalon
Phonetic Spelling: (ah-yaw-lone')
Definition: "deer", the name of several cities in Palestine
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from ayyal
Definition
"deer," the name of several cities in Pal.
NASB Translation
Aijalon (10).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אַיָּלוֺן proper name, of a location (Deer-field) Aijalon — ׳א Joshua 10:12 8t.; Joshua 19:42; Joshua 21:24 (Levitical city) Judges 1:35; 1 Samuel 14:31 doubtless same; so 1 Chronicles 6:54 (where Dan omitted); apparently later in Benjamin 1 Chronicles 8:13; 2Chronicles 11:10; 28:18 (see Be); עֵמֶק אַיָּלוֺן Joshua 10:12 almost certainly named from same; = Epiph. Ἰαλω modern Jâlo RobBR ii. 253, Surveyiii. 19.

2 city in Zebulun Judges 12:12.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Aijalon, Ajalon

From 'ayal; deer-field; Ajalon, the name of five places in Palestine -- Aijalon, Ajalon.

see HEBREW 'ayal
