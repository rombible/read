Brown-Driver-Briggs
[עָמֵק] adjective deep, unfathomable; — plural construct עִמְקֵי שָׂפָה (Ges§ 93ii) i.e. unintelligible of speech Isaiah 33:19; Ezekiel 3:5,6. — Proverbs 9:18 see עֹמֶק.
