NASB Translation
Thummim (5).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Thummim

Plural of tom; perfections, i.e. (techn.) One of the epithets of the objects in the high-priest's breastplate as an emblem of complete Truth -- Thummim.

see HEBREW tom
