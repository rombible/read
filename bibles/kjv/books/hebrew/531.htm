NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin

Definition

NASB Translation
Amoz (13).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אָמוֺץ proper name, masculine father of Isaiah ( = follow-ing) Isaiah 1:1; Isaiah 2:1; Isaiah 13:1; Isaiah 20:2; Isaiah 37:2,21; Isaiah 38:1 = 2 Kings 19:2,20; 2 Kings 20:1; 2Chronicles 26:22; 32:20,32.
