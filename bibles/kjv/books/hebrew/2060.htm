Definition

NASB Translation
Vashti (10).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
וַשְׁתִּי proper name, feminine (= Persian (Zend) vahista, best, compare JustiHdb. d. Zendsprache, 272) queen of Ahasuerus (Xerxes) king of Persia, Esther 1:9,11,12,15,16,17,19; Esther 2:1,4,17.
