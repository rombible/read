Strong's Concordance
tebunah: an understanding
Original Word: תָּבוּן
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: tebunah
Phonetic Spelling: (taw-boon')
Definition: an understanding
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from bin
Definition
an understanding
NASB Translation
discernment (1), reasonings (1), skill (1), skillful (1), skillfully (1), understanding (37).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
fruit, gain, increase, revenue

And (feminine) tbuwnah {teb-oo-naw'}; or towbunah {to-boo-naw'}; from biyn; intelligence; by implication, an argument; by extension, caprice -- discretion, reason, skilfulness, understanding, wisdom.

see HEBREW biyn
