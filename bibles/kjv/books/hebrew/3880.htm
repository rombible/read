Definition
a wreath
NASB Translation
garland (1), wreath (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[לִוְיָה] noun feminine wreath; — construct לִוְיַת חֵן only figurative Proverbs 1:9 ("" עֲנָקִים) of instruction of parents, Proverbs 4:9 ("" עֲטֶרֶת תִּפְאֶרֶת) of work of Wisdom.
