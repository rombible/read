Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
שְׁכוֺל noun [masculine] bereavement, loss of children; — ׳לֹא אֵדַע שׁ Isaiah 47:8 ("" אַלְמָנָה), compare Isaiah 47:9; figurative Psalm 35:12.

Infinitive of shakol; bereavement -- loss of children, spoiling.
