Strong's Concordance
Azarel: "God has helped," the name of several Israelites
Original Word: עֲזַרְאֵל
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Azarel
Phonetic Spelling: (az-ar-ale')
Definition: "God has helped", the name of several Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from azar and el
Definition
"God has helped," the name of several Isr.
NASB Translation
Azarel (6).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
עֲזַרְאֵל proper name, masculine (El hath helped); — Οζρειηλ, Εζριηλ, etc.;

1 warrior of David 1 Chronicles 12:7 (van d. H. v.1 Chronicles 12:6).

2 Levite musician 1 Chronicles 25:18 (= עֻזִּיאֵל 1 Chronicles 25:4), B Αζαρια, A Εζριηλ.

3 a Danite 1 Chronicles 27:22, Αζαραηλ, Εζριηλ.

4 one with foreign wife Ezra 10:41.

5 priestly name:

a. Nehemiah 11:13.

b. Nehemiah 12:36.
