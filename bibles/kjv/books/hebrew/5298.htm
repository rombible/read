1 a Levite Exodus 6:21 (P), ᵐ5 Ναφεκ, F Ναφεγ.

2 a son of David 2 Samuel 5:15 (ᵐ5 id., ᵐ5L Ναφεθ), 1 Chronicles 3:7 (ᵐ5 Ναφαθ, A Ναφεγ, ᵐ5L Νεεγ), 1 Chronicles 14:6 (ᵐ5 id., א Ναφατ, A Ναφαγ, ᵐ5L Ναφεκ).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Nepheg

From an unused root probably meaning to spring forth; a sprout; Nepheg, the name of two Israelites -- Nepheg.
