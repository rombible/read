Brown-Driver-Briggs
[זָעַךְ] verb extinguish, only

Niph`al be extinguished יָמַי נִזְעָ֑כוּ Job 17:1 ("" רוּחִי חֻבָּ֑לָה). Elsewhere always דעך (q. v.), and so in cognate languages. Probably error for נדעכו.
