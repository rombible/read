שְׁלַבִּים noun [masculine] plural joinings of bases (ᵑ9 juncturae); — ׳בֵּין הַשׁ 1 Kings 7:28,29, ׳עַלהַֿשְּׁ 1 Kings 7:29; AV RV ledges, ribs (Kit) or frames (Benz), joining or binding the parts together.
