Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Zoba, Zobah

Or Tsowbah {tso-baw'}; or Tsobah {tso-baw'}; from an unused root meaning to station; a station; Zoba or Zobah, a region of Syria -- Zoba, Zobah.
