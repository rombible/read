Strong's Concordance
Abinadab: "my father is noble," three Israelites
Original Word: אֲבִינָדָב
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Abinadab
Phonetic Spelling: (ab-ee-naw-dawb')
Definition: "my father is noble", three Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from ab and nadab
Definition
"my father is noble," three Isr.
NASB Translation
Abinadab (12).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֲבִינָדָב proper name, masculine (my father is noble)

1 a man of Gibeah in whose house the ark tarried 1 Samuel 7:1; 2 Samuel 6:3 (twice in verse); 2 Samuel 6:4; 1 Chronicles 13:7.

2 a son of Jesse 1 Samuel 16:8; 1 Samuel 17:13; 1 Kings 4:11 (? perhaps otherwise unknown; Klo proposes אֲבִינֵר בְּ).

3 a son of Saul 1 Samuel 31:2; 1 Chronicles 8:33; 1 Chronicles 9:39; 1 Chronicles 10:2.
