◄ 8345. shishshi ►
Strong's Concordance
shishshi: sixth
Original Word: שִׁשִּׁי
Part of Speech: Noun
Transliteration: shishshi
Phonetic Spelling: (shish-shee')
Definition: sixth
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from the same as shesh
Definition
sixth
NASB Translation
sixth (27), sixth part (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
שִׁשִּׁי masculine22 שִׁשִּׁית feminine6

adjective ordinal number sixth; — (הַ)יוֺם הַשִּׁשִּׁי Genesis 1:31 5t., etc.; בַּשָּׁנָה הַשִּׁשִּׁית Leviticus 25:21; Ezekiel 8:1, etc.; construct שִׁשִּׁית הַהִין Ezekiel 4:11 6th part of the hin, compare Ezekiel 45:13; Ezekiel 46:14; read ׳שׁ also Ezekiel 45:13 b (see [ שִׁשָּׁה] infra).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
sixth part

From shesh; sixth, ord. Or (feminine) fractional -- sixth (part).

see HEBREW shesh
