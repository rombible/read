Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
עִצָּבוֺן noun [masculine] pain, toil; — ׳ע absolute Genesis 3:17 toil; construct עִצְּבוֺן יָדֵינוּ Genesis 5:29 (both of agriculture); suffix עִצְּבוֺנֵךְ Genesis 3:16 (of travail; all J).
