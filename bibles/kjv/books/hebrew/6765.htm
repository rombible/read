Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
צְלָפְחָד proper name, masculine of Manasseh, Numbers 26:33,23; Numbers 27:1,7; Numbers 36:2,6,10,11; Joshua 17:3; 1 Chronicles 7:15 (twice in verse), Σαλπααδ, etc.
