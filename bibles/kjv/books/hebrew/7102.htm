I. [קְצִיעָה] noun feminine cassia, a powdered bark, like cinnamon (hence Greek κασία, Latin casia, LewyFremdw. 37); — plural (מֹר וַאֲהָלוֺת) פָצִיעוֺת Psalm 45:9 (Che now תּוּצַק are shed, see inEncy. Bib. CASSIA, n.).
