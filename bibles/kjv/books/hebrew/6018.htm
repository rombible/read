Brown-Driver-Briggs
עָמְרִי18 proper name, masculine

1 Omri, king Israel (MI4,5,7 עמרי; in Assyrian –umri COTGloss); — 1 Kings 16:16 11t. 1 Kings 16; 2 Kings 8:26 2Chronicles 22:2; Micah 6:16, ᵐ5 (ζ)αμβρ(ε)ι.

2. a. name in Benjamin 1 Chronicles 7:8.

b. in Judah 1 Chronicles 9:4.

c. in Issachar 1 Chronicles 27:18.
