Brown-Driver-Briggs
תַּרְבּוּת noun feminine increase, brood; — construct in phrase אֲנָשִׁים חַטָּאִים ׳ת Numbers 32:14 a brood (contemptuous) of sinful men (compare מַרְבִּית 1 Samuel 2:33).
