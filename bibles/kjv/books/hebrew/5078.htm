נֶ֑דֶה (so Baer Ginsb; van d. H. נֵ֑דֶה), noun masculine gift (on formation compare Ges§ 84a c Ol§ 144a Köii.1, 65 BaNB § 12a); — לְכָלזֹֿנוֺת יִתְּנוּנֶֿ֑דֶה Ezekiel 16:33 ("" נְדָבִים).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
gifts

From nadah in the sense of freely flinging money; a bounty (for prostitution) -- gifts.
