[כִּנְעָה] noun feminine bundle, pack (compare Arabic sense of √, above); — suffix אִסְפִי מֵאֶרֶץ כִּנְעָתֵ֑ךְ Jeremiah 10:7 pack thy bundle (and take it) out of the land.
