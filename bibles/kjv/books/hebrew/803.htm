[אָשְׁיָה]

noun feminine wall, bulwark (Talmud אָשִׁיתָא (Levy Nö); Mandean אשיתא wall NöM 113; ᵑ7 plural suffix אֲשִׁיתָהָא (DalmWB אָשְׁיָתַהָא) Jeremiah 50:15, אשיתנא Cant 2:9; Arabic column, support is loan-word according to Frä11); — only plural suffix bulwarks of city of Babylon,' etc.
