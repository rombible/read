NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
(Aramaic) from shemash
Definition
a scribe who opposed Isr.
NASB Translation
Shimshai (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
שִׁמְשַׁי proper name, masculine; — always סָֽפְרָא ׳שׁ: Ezra 4:8,9,17,23 (AndrM 86 * conjectures Old Iranian caritative ששמי, conformed to שׁמשׁ, compare Scheft92, who, however, prefers Old Baktrian sim¢zhi = sima¢zhi, proper name).

שֵׁן see שׁנן. below
