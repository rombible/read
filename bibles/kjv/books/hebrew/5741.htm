Brown-Driver-Briggs
עַדְרִיאֵל proper name, masculine son-in-law of Saul (apparently Aramaic name, my help is God = Hebrew עַזְרִיאֵל q. v.; so NeAmos. Jour. Semitic Lang, xiii (1897), 173 HPS compare GrayProp. N. 309; compare Old Aramaic יעדראל ClGann:J As,1883,Fev.-Mar.,139,No.13); — 1 Samuel 18:19 (ᵐ5 A Ιηλ, ᵐ5L Εδριηλ), 2 Samuel 21:8 (ᵐ5 Σερει, A Εσδρι, ᵐ5L Εζρι).
