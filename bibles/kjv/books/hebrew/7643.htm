Definition

NASB Translation
Sebam (1), Sibmah (5).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
שְׂבָם proper name, of a location Numbers 32:3, ִשׂבְמָה Numbers 32:38; Joshua 13:19; Isaiah 16:8,9; Jeremiah 48:32; in Moad (Reuben), near Heshbon (compare also JeromeIsaiah 16:8) Σεβαμα.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Shebam, Shibmah, Sibmah

Or (feminine) Sibmah {sib-maw'}; probably from basam; spice; Sebam or Sibmah, a place in Moab -- Shebam, Shibmah, Sibmah.

see HEBREW basam
