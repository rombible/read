Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth

Or Kinnereth {kin-neh'-reth}; respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as kinnowr; perhaps harp-shaped; Kinneroth or Kinnereth, a place in Palestine -- Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.

see HEBREW kinnowr
