adargazar: a counselor
Original Word: אֲדרְגָּזֵר
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: adargazar
Phonetic Spelling: (ad-ar''-gaw-zare')

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
(Aramaic) of foreign origin
Definition
a counselor
NASB Translation
counselors (2).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[אֲדַרְגָּזַר] noun masculine counsellor (Persian loan-word = andarzaghar NöCOT Daniel 3:2 Tabari 462 AndrM 51 * Str42* Dr Scheftii. 57; MeyEntst. J. 25 questions, and thinks military title); — plural emphatic אֲדַרְגָָּֽןְרַיָּא Daniel 3:2,3.
