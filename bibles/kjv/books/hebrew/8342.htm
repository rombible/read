sason: exultation, rejoicing
Original Word: שָׂשׂוֹן
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine

Phonetic Spelling: (saw-sone')

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from sus
Definition
exultation, rejoicing
NASB Translation
gaiety (1), gladness (3), joy (15), joyously (1), rejoicing (2).
