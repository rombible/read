Definition

Brown-Driver-Briggs
עֲבוֺט noun [masculine] pledge, article pledged as security for debt; — absolute ׳ע Deuteronomy 24:11,13; suffix עֲבֹטוֺ Deuteronomy 24:10 (as accusative of congnate meaning with verb), Deuteronomy 24:12.
