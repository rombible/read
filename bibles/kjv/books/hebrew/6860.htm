Brown-Driver-Briggs
ִצקְלַג and (1 Chronicles 12:1; 1 Chronicles 12:21 ׳צִיק)

proper name, of a location (צִ֒קלָ֑ג 1 Samuel 27:6 a +, ִצקְלָג֖ 2 Samuel 2:1 צִיקְלָ֔ג 1 Chronicles 12:1); — assigned to Judah Joshua 15:31, to Simeon Joshua 19:5; Philistine city given to David 1 Samuel 27:6 (twice in verse); 1 Samuel 30:1 (3 t. in verse); 1 Samuel 30:14,26; 2 Samuel 1:1; 2 Samuel 4:10; 1 Chronicles 4:30; 1 Chronicles 12:1; 1 Chronicles 12:21 (van d. H. v.1 Chronicles 12:20); Σεκελακ, Σικελαγ, etc.; usually identified with Zuheilikah, 19 miles southwest from Bêt Jibrin, 11 miles east-southeast from Gaza (compare BuhlGeogr. 185).

Ziklag

Or Tsiyqlag (1 Chronicles 12:1, 1 Chronicles 12:20) {tsee-kel-ag'}; of uncertain derivation: Tsiklag or Tsikelag, a place in Palestine -- Ziklag.
