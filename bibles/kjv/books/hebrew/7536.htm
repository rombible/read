Definition

NASB Translation
spitting (2), spittle (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
רֹק noun [masculine] spittle; — absolute ׳ר Isaiah 50:6; Job 30:10 (both of contemptuous spitting); suffix רֻקִּי Job 7:19 while I swallow (בָּלַע) my spittle, i.e. for the briefest time.
