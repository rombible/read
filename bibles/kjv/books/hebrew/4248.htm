Definition

NASB Translation
Mahlon (4).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מַחְלוֺן proper name, masculine son of Elimelech and Naomi, first husband of Ruth, Ruth 1:2,5; Ruth 4:9,10, ᵐ5 Μααλων.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Mahlon

From chalah; sick; Machlon, an Israelite -- Mahlon.
