Definition

NASB Translation
Hannah (13).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
חַנָּה proper name, feminine ᵐ5 Ἄννα (compare Nabataean חנה EutNo. 20), mother of Samuel 1 Samuel 1:2 (twice in verse); 1 Samuel 1:5 (twice in verse); 1 Samuel 1:8,9,13,15,19,20,22; 1 Samuel 2:1,21.
