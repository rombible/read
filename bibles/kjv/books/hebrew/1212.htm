Strong's Concordance
Betsalel: "in the shadow (protection) of El," two Israelites
Original Word: בְּצַלְאֵל
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Betsalel
Phonetic Spelling: (bets-al-ale')
Definition: "in the shadow (protection) of El", two Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
probably from tsel and el
Definition
"in the shadow (protection) of El," two Isr.
NASB Translation
Bezalel (9).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
בְּצַלְאֵל proper name, masculine (in the shadow (protection) of El; compare cuneiform ‚il-Bêl, a king of Gaza, COT Joshua 11:22) —

1 a skilled artisan of tribe of Judah Exodus 31:2; Exodus 35:30; Exodus 36:1,2; Exodus 37:1; Exodus 38:22 (all P) 1 Chronicles 2:20; 2Chronicles 1:5.

2 an Israelite Ezra 10:30.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Bezaleel

Probably from tsel and 'el with a prepositional prefix; in (the) shadow (i.e. Protection) of God; Betsalel, the name of two Israelites -- Bezaleel.
