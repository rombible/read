hed: a shout, shouting, cheer

Definition

NASB Translation

Brown-Driver-Briggs
הֵד noun [masculine] id., only Ezekiel 7:7 הֵד הָרִים (joyous) shout on mountains (opposed to מְהוּמָה noise of battle).
