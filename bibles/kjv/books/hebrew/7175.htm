Strong's Concordance
qeresh: bench
Original Word: קֶרֶשׁ
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: qeresh
Phonetic Spelling: (keh'-resh)
Definition: perhaps to tear down
Brown-Driver-Briggs
קֶ֫רֶשׁ51 noun masculineExodus 26:16 board, boards; — absolute ׳ק Exodus 26:18 +, קָ֑רֶשׁ Exodus 26:20 +; suffix קַרְשֵׁח Ezekiel 27:6; plural קְרָשִׁים Ezekiel 27:15 +; construct קַרְשֵׁי Ezekiel 27:26 +; suffix קְרָשָׁיו Ezekiel 35:11 +; —

1 collective boards, of deck Ezekiel 27:6 (of Tyre under figure of ship).

2 board of tabernacle Exodus 26:15,16 (twice in verse) + 45 t. 26-40; Numbers 3:36; Numbers 4:31 (all P).

קֶ֫רֶת see קרה.

קשׂה (√ of following, meaning dubious; compare Arabic basket of palm-leaves; Ethiopic urn, jar; Late Hebrew קַסְוָה = Biblical Hebrew קשׂוה, compare ᵑ7 קַסְוָותָא, or קסתות; Talmud קִסְטְ, קִיסְטָא a measure, Syriac jar, (ט after ק), NöSB Akkadian 1882, 1179 Frä63, 205 Brocks. see ).

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
bench, board

From an unused root meaning to split off; a slab or plank; by implication, a deck of a ship -- bench, board.
