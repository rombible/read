Definition

NASB Translation
design (2), designs (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[צוּרָה] noun feminine form, fashion; — construct צוּרַת Ezekiel 43:11 see foregoing; suffix צוּרַתוֺ Ezekiel 43:11 (ᵐ5 מִצְוֺתָיו, so Co, or תּוֺרֹתָיו, so Berthol Krae; Toy as MT); so also Kt Ezekiel 43:11; Ezekiel 43:11 (Qr plural suffix צוּרֹתָיו) 2nd of these strike out as dittograph all moderns; 1st read as Kt Co Berthol, as Qr Toy; singular suffix צוּרָם (Ges § 91 e Köii.1, 440) Psalm 49:15 Qr (Kt צירם) their form (of the dead).
