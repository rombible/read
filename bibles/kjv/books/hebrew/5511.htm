Brown-Driver-Briggs
סִיחוֺן, סִיחֹן proper name, masculine Sihon (on formation compare LagBN 198); — סִיחוֺן Numbers 21:27 18t., סִיחֹן Numbers 21:21 17t.; — king of Amorites, Numbers 21:21 7t. Numbers 21 (JE), Numbers 32:33 (R) Deuteronomy 1:4 10t. Deuteronomy; Judges 2:10 4t. Judges (D) + Judges 13:21 (twice in verse); Judges 13:27 (P), Judges 11:19,20 (twice in verse); Judges 11:21; Jeremiah 48:45; 1 Kings 4:19; Nehemiah 9:22; Psalm 135:11; Psalm 136:19. ᵐ5 Σηων, ᵐ5L Σιων.
