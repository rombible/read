Brown-Driver-Briggs
[צֵאָה] noun feminine filth, specifically human excrement; — suffix צֵאָתֶ֑ךָ Deuteronomy 23:14; construct בְּגֶלְלֵי צֵאַת הָאָדָם Ezekiel 4:12 (as fuel; compare [גֵּל] p. 165 above).
