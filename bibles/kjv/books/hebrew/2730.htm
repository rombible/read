◄ 2730. chared ►
Strong's Concordance
chared: afraid
Original Word: חָרֵד
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: chared
Phonetic Spelling: (khaw-rade')
Definition: trembling
Brown-Driver-Briggs
חָרֵד verbal adjective of foregoing, trembling; — חָרֵד Judges 7:3 3t.; חֲרֵדִים Isaiah 66:5; Ezra 10:3 — trembling (from fear), absolute Judges 7:3 ("" ירא), 1 Samuel 4:13 (subject לִבּוֺ, followed by עַל, for, in behalf of); in awe and reverence, at word of God, sq, בְּ Ezra 9:4; Isaiah 66:2 (followed by עַל), Isaiah 66:5 (followed by אֶל); compare ׳בְּמִצְוַת אל ׳ח Ezra 10:3.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
