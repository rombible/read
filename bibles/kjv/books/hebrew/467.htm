◄ 467. Eliphelet ►
Strong's Concordance
Eliphelet: "God is deliverance," the name of several Israelites
Original Word: אֱלִיפֶלֶט
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Eliphelet
Phonetic Spelling: (el-ee-feh'-let)
Definition: "God is deliverance", the name of several Israelites
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from el and pallet
Definition
"God is deliverance," the name of several Isr.
NASB Translation
Eliphelet (8), Elpelet (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֱלִיפֶ֫לֶט (פָֿ֑לֶט 2 Samuel 5:16 +) proper name, masculine (God is deliverance, compare מַּלְטִיאֵל)

a. son of David 2 Samuel 5:16; 1 Chronicles 3:6,8; 1 Chronicles 14:7; = אֶלְמָּ֑לֶט 1 Chronicles 14:5.

b. one of David's heroes 2 Samuel 23:34 (compare also אֱלִיפָל 1 Chronicles 11:35).

c. a Benjamite 1 Chronicles 8:39.

d. one of the line of Adonikam Ezra 8:13.

e. of the line of Hashum Ezra 10:33.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Eliphalet, Eliphelet, Elpalet

Or (shortened) Elpelet {el-peh'-let}; from 'el and pallet; God of deliverance; Eliphelet or Elpelet, the name of six Israelites -- Eliphalet, Eliphelet, Elpalet.
