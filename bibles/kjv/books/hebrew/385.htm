Strong's Concordance
Ithamar: "land of palms," a son of Aaron
Original Word: אִיתָמָר
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Ithamar
Phonetic Spelling: (eeth-aw-mawr')
Definition: "land of palms", a son of Aaron
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from i and tamar
Definition
"land of palms," a son of Aaron
NASB Translation
Ithamar (21).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אִיתָמָר proper name, masculine ((is) land of palms ? Thes) youngest (4th) son of Aaron Exodus 6:23; Exodus 28:1; Exodus 38:21; Leviticus 10:6,12,16; Numbers 3:2,4; Numbers 4:28,33; Numbers 7:8; Numbers 26:60 (all P) 1 Chronicles 5:29; 1 Chronicles 24:1,2,3,4 (twice in verse); 1 Chronicles 24:5,6; Ezra 8:2.
