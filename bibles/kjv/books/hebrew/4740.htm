Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
corner, turning

Or maqtsoa {mak-tso-ah}; or (feminine) maqtso-ah {mak-tso-aw'}; from qatsa' in the denominative sense of bending; an angle or recess -- corner, turning.

see HEBREW qatsa'
