2 devastation, ruin, for nation Hosea 7:13, כְּשֹׁד מִשַּׁדַּי יָבוֺא Isaiah 13:6 (simile of ׳יוֺם י) = Joel 1:13; Isaiah 16:4 (Lo Gr CheHpt Marti שֹׁדֵד; "" רֹמֵס), Isaiah 22:4, וְשֶׁבֶר ׳שׁ Isaiah 51:19; Jeremiah 48:3; more Generally Amos 5:9 (twice in verse); for individual Job 5:21 (van d.
