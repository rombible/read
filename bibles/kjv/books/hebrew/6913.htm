Strong's Concordance
qeber: a grave, sepulcher
Original Word: קֶבֶר
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine
Transliteration: qeber

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from qabar
Definition
a grave, sepulcher
NASB Translation
burial (6), burial place (1), grave (28), graves (16), tomb (8), tombs (8).
