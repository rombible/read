Strong's Concordance
Meshullam: the name of a number of Isr.
Original Word: מְשֻׁלָּם
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Meshullam
Phonetic Spelling: (mesh-ool-lawm')
Definition: the name of a number of Isr
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from shalem
Definition
the name of a number of Isr.
NASB Translation
Meshullam (25).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מְשֻׁלָּם proper name, masculine Μεσουλαμ, Μοσολλαμ, etc.:

1 grandfather of Shaphan 2 Kings 22:3.

2 son of Zerubbabel 1 Chronicles 3:19.

3 in Benjamin:

a. 1 Chronicles 8:17, perhaps =

b. 1 Chronicles 9:7.

c. 1 Chronicles 9:8.

d. Nehemiah 11:7.

4 in Gad 1 Chronicles 5:13.

5 wall-builders:

a. Nehemiah 3:4,30.

b. Nehemiah 3:6.

6 a chief Nehemiah 10:21.

7 priests (distinctions in part obscure):

a. 1 Chronicles 9:11; Nehemiah 11:11; Nehemiah 12:13 (perhaps = שַׁלּוּם 11).

b. 1 Chronicles 9:12.

c. Nehemiah 10:8.

d. Nehemiah 12:6.

e. Nehemiah 12:33.

8 Levites:

a.2Chronicles 34:12.

b. Ezra 8:16; Ezra 10:15; Nehemiah 8:14.

c. Nehemiah 12:25 ( = שַׁלּוּם 12a).

9 Ezra 10:29. above
