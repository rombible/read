Strong's Concordance
abedah: a lost thing
Original Word: אֲבֵדָה
Part of Speech: Noun Feminine
Transliteration: abedah
Phonetic Spelling: (ab-ay-daw')
Definition: a lost thing
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from abad
Definition
a lost thing
NASB Translation
lost (1), lost thing (2), what was lost (1).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
אֲבֵדָה noun feminine a lost thing — absolute except Deuteronomy 22:3 construct אֲבֵדַת — Exodus 22:8; with מָצָא Leviticus 5:22,23; with אָבַד + מָצָא Deuteronomy 22:3; — (אבדה Proverbs 27:20 Kt compare אֲבַדּוֺ below)

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
lost

From 'abad; concrete, something lost; abstract, destruction, i.e. Hades -- lost. Compare 'abaddoh.
