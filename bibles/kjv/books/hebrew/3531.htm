כְּבָרָה noun feminine a sieve (a net-like implement), Amos 9:9 כַּאֲשֶׁר יִנּוֺעַ בַּכְּבָרָה as it (corn) is shaken (i.e. sifted) in a sieve. compare WetzstZPV xiv. 1 ff.
