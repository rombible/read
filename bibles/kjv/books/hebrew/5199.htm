Brown-Driver-Briggs
נְטֹפָה proper name, of a location in Judah (dropping, dripping, compare LewySemitic Fremdwörter 40); — Ezra 2:22 = Nehemiah 7:26; ᵐ5. Νετωφα, ᵐ5L Νετωφαγι (as adjective, of a people Nehemiah 12:28).
