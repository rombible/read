Brown-Driver-Briggs
[נָהַק] verb bray, cry (Late Hebrew id.; Arabic bray (of ass); Aramaic נְהַק cry out (of men), נְהִיקָא braying); —

Qal Imperfect יִנְהַק Job 6:5 of wild ass; יִנְהָ֑קוּ Job 30:7 of cries of destitute outcasts.
