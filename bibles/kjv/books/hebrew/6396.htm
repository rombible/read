Pallu: a son of Reuben
Original Word: פַלּוּא
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Pallu
Phonetic Spelling: (pal-loo')

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from the same as pele
Definition
a son of Reuben
NASB Translation
Pallu (5).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
מַּלּוּא proper name, masculine (DHM Epigr. Denkm. 9, 1 compare Lihyanian proper name פלה); — son of Reuben Genesis 46:9; Exodus 6:14; Numbers 26:5,8; 1 Chronicles 5:3, Φαλλου(ς), etc.; see also מֶּלֶת.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance

From palah; distinguished; Pallu, an Israelite -- Pallu, Phallu.
