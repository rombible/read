chedel: world

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
another reading for cheled, q.v.

Brown-Driver-Briggs
[חֶ֫דֶל] noun [masculine] cessation, only יוֺשְׁבֵי חָ֑דֶל Isaiah 38:11 (so Baer) inhabitants of (land of) cessation, i.e. of She'ôl; see Di; but read rather חָ֑לֶד, see חֶלֶד.
