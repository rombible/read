Brown-Driver-Briggs
II. עֶגְלָה proper name, feminine wife of David (on sense heifer compare לֵאָה, רָחֵל; see also GrayProp. N. 92, No.27); — 2 Samuel 3:5 (ᵐ5 Αιγαλ, A Αιγας, ᵐ5L Αγλα) = 1 Chronicles 3:3 (ᵐ5 Αλα, A Αγλα, ᵐ5L Εγλα).
