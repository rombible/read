Brown-Driver-Briggs
גַּמְלִיאֵל proper name, masculine (reward of God) a prince of Manasseh Numbers 1:10; Numbers 2:20; Numbers 7:54,59; Numbers 10:23, compare Mishna גמליאל, Palmyrene VogNo. 124, ᵐ5 Γαμαλιήλ Acts 5:34.
