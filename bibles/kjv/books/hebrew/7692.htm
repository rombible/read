Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Shiggaion, Shigionoth

Or shiggayonah {shig-gaw-yo-naw'}; from shagah; properly, aberration, i.e. (technically) a dithyramb or rambling poem -- Shiggaion, Shigionoth.
