Strong's Concordance
Qehathi: descendant of Kohath
Original Word: קֱהָתִי
Part of Speech: Adjective
Transliteration: Qehathi
Phonetic Spelling: (ko-haw-thee')
Definition: descendant of Kohath
NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from Qehath
Definition
desc. of Kohath
NASB Translation
Kohathite (2), Kohathites (13).

Brown-Driver-Briggs
קְהָתִי adjective, of a people of foregoing, with article ׳הַקּ as substantive collective: ׳(ו־ֺת) הַקּ מִשְׁפַחַתּ Numbers 26:57 8t.; ׳בְּנֵי הַקּ Numbers 4:34; 1 Chronicles 6:18; 2Chronicles 29:12; plural הַקְּהָתּים Numbers 10:21 and ׳בְּנֵי הַקּ2Chronicles 20:19; 34:12. — Van d. H. has ( ם)הַקֳּהָתִי Numbers 3:27; Numbers 10:21 6t.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Kohathites
