osher: riches
Original Word: עשֶׁר
Part of Speech: Noun Masculine

Phonetic Spelling: (o'-sher)

NAS Exhaustive Concordance
Word Origin
from ashar
Definition
riches
NASB Translation
fortune (1), riches (32), wealth (4).
