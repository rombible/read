עֶצְיוֺן גֶּ֫בֶר proper name, of a location usually Γασιων [Γες] Γαβερ, near Elath at head of Gulf of Akaba 1 Kings 9:26 2Chronicles 8:17, גָּ֑בֶר ׳י 1 Kings 22:49 2Chronicles 20:36; עֶצְיֹן גָּ֑בֶר Deuteronomy 2:8; Numbers 33:35,36 (P).
