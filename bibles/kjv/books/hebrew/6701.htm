Strong's Concordance
Tsuwriyshadday: Zurishaddai
Original Word: צוּרִישַׁדַּי
Part of Speech: Proper Name Masculine
Transliteration: Tsuwriyshadday
Phonetic Spelling: (tsoo-ree-shad-dah'-ee)
Definition: "my rock is the Almighty", a Simeonite
Brown-Driver-Briggs
צוּרִישַׁדָּ֑י proper name, masculine Σουρ(ε)ισαδαι[ε] (my rock is Shadday); — a Simeonite Numbers 1:6; Numbers 2:12; Numbers 7:36,41; Numbers 10:19.

Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Zurishaddai

From tsuwr and Shadday; rock of (the) Almighty; Tsurishaddai, an Israelite -- Zurishaddai.
