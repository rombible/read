Glorious and Majestic Are His Works

1Praise ye Jah!

א

I thank Jehovah with the whole heart,

ב

In the secret meeting of the upright, And of the company.

ג

2Great [are] the works of Jehovah,

ד

Sought out by all desiring them.

ה

3Honourable and majestic is His work,

ו

And His righteousness is standing for ever.

ז

4A memorial He hath made of His wonders,

ח

Gracious and merciful [is] Jehovah.

ט

5Prey He hath given to those fearing Him,

י

He remembereth to the age His covenant.

כ

6The power of His works He hath declared to His people,

ל

To give to them the inheritance of nations.

מ

7The works of His hands [are] true and just,

נ

Stedfast [are] all His appointments.

ס

8They are sustained for ever to the age.

ע

They are made in truth and uprightness.

פ

9Redemption He hath sent to His people,

צ

He hath appointed to the age His covenant,

ק

Holy and fearful [is] His name.

ר

10The beginning of wisdom [is] fear of Jehovah,

ש

Good understanding have all doing them,

ת

His praise [is] standing for ever!
