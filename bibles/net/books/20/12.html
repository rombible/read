1 The one who loves discipline loves knowledge,1

but the one who hates reproof is stupid.2

2 A good person obtains favor from the Lord,

but the Lord3 condemns a person with wicked schemes.4

3 No one5 can be established6 through wickedness,

but a righteous root7 cannot be moved.

4 A noble wife8 is the crown9 of her husband,

but the wife10 who acts shamefully is like rottenness in his bones.11

5 The plans12 of the righteous are just;

the counsels of the wicked are deceitful.13

6 The words of the wicked lie in wait14 to shed innocent blood,15

but the words16 of the upright will deliver them.

7 The wicked are overthrown17 and perish,18

but the righteous household19 will stand.

8 A person20 is praised in accordance with21 his wisdom,

but the one who has a twisted mind22 is despised.

9 Better is a person of humble standing23 who nevertheless has a servant,24

than one who pretends to be somebody important25 yet has no food.

10 A righteous person cares for26 the life of his animal,

but even the most compassionate acts27 of the wicked are cruel.

11 The one who works28 his field will have plenty29 of food,

but whoever chases daydreams30 lacks wisdom.31

12 The wicked person desires a stronghold,32

but the righteous root33 endures.34

13 The evil person is ensnared35 by the transgression of his speech,36

but the righteous person escapes out of trouble.37

14 A person will be satisfied with good from the fruit of his words,38

and the work of his hands39 will be rendered to40 him.

15 The way of a fool41 is right42 in his own opinion,43

but the one who listens to advice is wise.44

16 A fool’s annoyance45 is known at once,46

but the prudent47 overlooks48 an insult.

17 The faithful witness49 tells what is right,50

but a false witness51 speaks52 deceit.

18 Speaking recklessly53 is like the thrusts of a sword,

but the words54 of the wise bring55 healing.56

19 The one who tells the truth57 will endure forever,

but the one who lies58 will last only for a moment.59

20 Deceit is in the heart of those who plot evil,60

but those who promote peace61 have joy.

21 The righteous do not encounter62 any harm,63

but the wicked are filled with calamity.64

22 The Lord65 abhors a person who lies,66

but those who deal truthfully67 are his delight.68

23 The shrewd person69 conceals70 knowledge,

but foolish people71 publicize folly.72

24 The diligent73 person74 will rule,

but the slothful75 will become a slave.76

25 Anxiety77 in a person’s heart78 weighs him down,79

but an encouraging80 word brings him joy.81

26 The righteous person is cautious in his friendship,82

but the way of the wicked leads them astray.

27 The lazy person does not roast83 his prey,

but personal possessions84 are precious to the diligent.

28 In the path of righteousness there is life,

but another path leads to death.85
