Admonition to Follow Righteousness and Avoid Wickedness1

1 Listen, children,2 to a father’s instruction,3

and pay attention so that4 you may gain5 discernment.

2 Because I give6 you good instruction,7

do not forsake my teaching.

3 When I was a son to my father,8

a tender only child9 before my mother,

4 he taught me, and he said to me:

“Let your heart lay hold of my words;

keep my commands so that10 you will live.

5 Acquire wisdom, acquire understanding;

do not forget and do not turn aside from the words I speak.11

6 Do not forsake wisdom,12 and she will protect you;

love her, and she will guard you.

7 Wisdom is supreme13 – so14 acquire wisdom,

and whatever you acquire,15 acquire understanding!16

8 Esteem her highly17 and she will exalt you;

she will honor you if you embrace her.

9 She will place a fair18 garland on your head;

she will bestow19 a beautiful crown20 on you.”

10 Listen, my child,21 and accept my words,

so that22 the years of your life will be many.23

11 I will guide you24 in the way of wisdom

and I will lead you in upright paths.25

12 When you walk, your steps26 will not be hampered,27

and when you run,28 you will not stumble.

13 Hold on to instruction,29 do not let it go;

protect it,30 because it is your life.

14 Do not enter the path of the wicked

or walk31 in the way of those who are evil.

15 Avoid it, do not go on it;

turn away from it, and go on.32

16 For they cannot sleep unless they cause harm;33

they are robbed of sleep34 until they make someone stumble.35

17 For they eat bread36 gained from wickedness37

and drink wine obtained from violence.38

18 But the path of the righteous is like the bright morning light,39

growing brighter and brighter40 until full day.41

19 The way of the wicked is like gloomy darkness;42

they do not know what causes them to stumble.43

20 My child, pay attention to my words;

listen attentively44 to my sayings.

21 Do not let them depart45 from your sight,

guard46 them within your heart;47

22 for they are life to those who find them

and healing to one’s entire body.48

23 Guard your heart with all vigilance,49

for from it are the sources50 of life.

24 Remove perverse speech51 from your mouth;52

keep devious talk far from your lips.53

25 Let your eyes look directly54 in front of you

and let your gaze55 look straight before you.

26 Make the path for your feet56 level,57

so that58 all your ways may be established.59

27 Do not turn60 to the right or to the left;

turn yourself61 away from evil.62
