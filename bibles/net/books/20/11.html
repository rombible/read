1 The Lord abhors1 dishonest scales,2

but an accurate weight3 is his delight.

2 When pride4 comes,5 then comes disgrace,6

but with humility7 comes8 wisdom.

3 The integrity of the upright guides them,9

but the crookedness of the unfaithful destroys them.10

4 Wealth does not profit in the day of wrath,11

but righteousness delivers from mortal danger.12

5 The righteousness of the blameless will make straight their way,13

but the wicked person will fall by his own wickedness.14

6 The righteousness of the upright will deliver them,15

but the faithless will be captured16 by their own desires.17

7 When a wicked person dies, his expectation perishes,18

and the hope of his strength19 perishes.20

8 The righteous person is delivered21 out of trouble,

and the wicked turns up in his stead.22

9 With his speech23 the godless person24 destroys25 his neighbor,

but by knowledge26 the righteous will be delivered.

10 When the righteous do well,27 the city rejoices;28

when the wicked perish, there is joy.

11 A city is exalted by the blessing provided from29 the upright,

but it is destroyed by the counsel30 of the wicked.31

12 The one who denounces32 his neighbor lacks wisdom,33

but the one who has discernment34 keeps silent.35

13 The one who goes about slandering others36 reveals37 secrets,

but the one who is trustworthy38 conceals a matter.

14 When there is no guidance39 a nation falls,

but there is success40 in the abundance of counselors.

15 The one who puts up security for a stranger41 will surely have trouble,42

but whoever avoids43 shaking hands44 will be secure.

16 A generous woman45 gains honor,

and ruthless men46 seize wealth.47

17 A kind person48 benefits49 himself,50

but a cruel person brings himself trouble.51

18 The wicked person52 earns53 deceitful wages,54

but the one who sows55 righteousness reaps56 a genuine57 reward.58

19 True59 righteousness leads to60 life,

but the one who pursues evil pursues it61 to his own death.62

20 The Lord abhors63 those who are perverse in heart,64

but those who are blameless in their ways65 are his delight.66

21 Be assured that67 the evil person will certainly be punished,68

but the descendants of the righteous69 will not suffer unjust judgment.70

22 Like a gold ring71 in a pig’s snout72

is73 a beautiful woman who rejects74 discretion.75

23 What the righteous desire76 leads77 only to good,

but what the wicked hope for78 leads79 to wrath.

24 One person is generous80 and yet grows more wealthy,81

but another withholds more than he should82 and comes to poverty.83

25 A generous person84 will be enriched,85

and the one who provides water86 for others87 will himself be satisfied.88

26 People will curse89 the one who withholds grain,90

but they will praise91 the one who sells it.92

27 The one who diligently seeks93 good seeks favor,

but the one who searches94 for evil – it will come to him.95

28 The one who trusts in his riches will fall,

but the righteous96 will flourish like a green leaf.97

29 The one who troubles98 his family99 will inherit nothing,100

and the fool101 will be a servant to the wise person.102

30 The fruit of the righteous is like103 a tree producing life,104

and the one who wins souls105 is wise.106

31 If the righteous are recompensed on earth,107

how much more108 the wicked sinner!109
