Psalm 1381

By David.

1 I will give you thanks with all my heart;

before the heavenly assembly2 I will sing praises to you.

2 I will bow down toward your holy temple,

and give thanks to your name,

because of your loyal love and faithfulness,

for you have exalted your promise above the entire sky.3

3 When4 I cried out for help, you answered me.

You made me bold and energized me.5

4 Let all the kings of the earth give thanks6 to you, O Lord,

when they hear the words you speak.7

5 Let them sing about the Lord’s deeds,8

for the Lord’s splendor is magnificent.9

6 Though the Lord is exalted, he takes note of the lowly,

and recognizes the proud from far away.

7 Even when I must walk in the midst of danger,10 you revive me.

You oppose my angry enemies,11

and your right hand delivers me.

8 The Lord avenges me.12

O Lord, your loyal love endures.

Do not abandon those whom you have made!13
