Psalm 131

For the music director; a psalm of David.

1 How long, Lord, will you continue to ignore me?2

How long will you pay no attention to me?3

2 How long must I worry,4

and suffer in broad daylight?5

How long will my enemy gloat over me?6

3 Look at me!7 Answer me, O Lord my God!

Revive me,8 or else I will die!9

4 Then10 my enemy will say, “I have defeated him!”

Then11 my foes will rejoice because I am upended.

5 But I12 trust in your faithfulness.

May I rejoice because of your deliverance!13

6 I will sing praises14 to the Lord

when he vindicates me.15
