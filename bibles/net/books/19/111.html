Psalm 1111

1 Praise the Lord!

I will give thanks to the Lord with my whole heart,

in the assembly of the godly and the congregation.

2 The Lord’s deeds are great,

eagerly awaited2 by all who desire them.

3 His work is majestic and glorious,3

and his faithfulness endures4 forever.

4 He does5 amazing things that will be remembered;6

the Lord is merciful and compassionate.

5 He gives7 food to his faithful followers;8

he always remembers his covenant.9

6 He announced that he would do mighty deeds for his people,

giving them a land that belonged to other nations.10

7 His acts are characterized by11 faithfulness and justice;

all his precepts are reliable.12

8 They are forever firm,

and should be faithfully and properly carried out.13

9 He delivered his people;14

he ordained that his covenant be observed forever.15

His name is holy and awesome.

10 To obey the Lord is the fundamental principle for wise living;16

all who carry out his precepts acquire good moral insight.17

He will receive praise forever.18
