Psalm 981

A psalm.

1 Sing to the Lord a new song,2

for he performs3 amazing deeds!

His right hand and his mighty arm

accomplish deliverance.4

2 The Lord demonstrates his power to deliver;5

in the sight of the nations he reveals his justice.

3 He remains loyal and faithful to the family of Israel.6

All the ends of the earth see our God deliver us.7

4 Shout out praises to the Lord, all the earth!

Break out in a joyful shout and sing!

5 Sing to the Lord accompanied by a harp,

accompanied by a harp and the sound of music!

6 With trumpets and the blaring of the ram’s horn,

shout out praises before the king, the Lord!

7 Let the sea and everything in it shout,

along with the world and those who live in it!

8 Let the rivers clap their hands!

Let the mountains sing in unison

9 before the Lord!

For he comes to judge the earth!

He judges the world fairly,8

and the nations in a just manner.
