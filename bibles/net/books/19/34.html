Psalm 341

Written by David, when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, causing the king to send him away.2

1 I will praise3 the Lord at all times;

my mouth will continually praise him.4

2 I will boast5 in the Lord;

let the oppressed hear and rejoice!6

3 Magnify the Lord with me!

Let’s praise7 his name together!

4 I sought the Lord’s help8 and he answered me;

he delivered me from all my fears.

5 Those who look to him for help are happy;

their faces are not ashamed.9

6 This oppressed man cried out and the Lord heard;

he saved him10 from all his troubles.

7 The Lord’s angel camps around

the Lord’s11 loyal followers12 and delivers them.13

8 Taste14 and see that the Lord is good!

How blessed15 is the one16who takes shelter in him!17

9 Remain loyal to18 the Lord, you chosen people of his,19

for his loyal followers20 lack nothing!

10 Even young lions sometimes lack food and are hungry,

but those who seek the Lord lack no good thing.

11 Come children! Listen to me!

I will teach you what it means to fear the Lord.21

12 Do you want to really live?22

Would you love to live a long, happy life?23

13 Then make sure you don’t speak evil words24

or use deceptive speech!25

14 Turn away from evil and do what is right!26

Strive for peace and promote it!27

15 The Lord pays attention to the godly

and hears their cry for help.28

16 But the Lord opposes evildoers

and wipes out all memory of them from the earth.29

17 The godly30 cry out and the Lord hears;

he saves them from all their troubles.31

18 The Lord is near the brokenhearted;

he delivers32 those who are discouraged.33

19 The godly34 face many dangers,35

but the Lord saves36 them37 from each one of them.

20 He protects38 all his bones;39

not one of them is broken.40

21 Evil people self-destruct;41

those who hate the godly are punished.42

22 The Lord rescues his servants;43

all who take shelter in him escape punishment.44
