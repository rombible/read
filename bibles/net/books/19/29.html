Psalm 291

A psalm of David.

1 Acknowledge the Lord, you heavenly beings,2

acknowledge the Lord’s majesty and power!3

2 Acknowledge the majesty of the Lord’s reputation!4

Worship the Lord in holy attire!5

3 The Lord’s shout is heard over the water;6

the majestic God thunders,7

the Lord appears over the surging water.8

4 The Lord’s shout is powerful,9

the Lord’s shout is majestic.10

5 The Lord’s shout breaks11 the cedars,

the Lord shatters12 the cedars of Lebanon.13

6 He makes Lebanon skip like a calf

and Sirion14 like a young ox.15

7 The Lord’s shout strikes16 with flaming fire.17

8 The Lord’s shout shakes18 the wilderness,

the Lord shakes the wilderness of Kadesh.19

9 The Lord’s shout bends20 the large trees21

and strips22 the leaves from the forests.23

Everyone in his temple says, “Majestic!”24

10 The Lord sits enthroned over the engulfing waters,25

the Lord sits enthroned26 as the eternal king.

11 The Lord gives27 his people strength;28

the Lord grants his people security.29
