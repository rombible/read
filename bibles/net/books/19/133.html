Psalm 1331

A song of ascents,2 by David.

1 Look! How good and how pleasant it is

when brothers live together!3

2 It is like fine oil poured on the head

which flows down the beard4 –

Aaron’s beard,

and then flows down his garments.5

3 It is like the dew of Hermon,6

which flows down upon the hills of Zion.7

Indeed8 that is where the Lord has decreed

a blessing will be available – eternal life.9
