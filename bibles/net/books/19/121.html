Psalm 1211

A song of ascents.2

1 I look up3 toward the hills.

From where4 does my help come?

2 My help comes from the Lord,5

the Creator6 of heaven and earth!

3 May he not allow your foot to slip!

May your protector7 not sleep!8

4 Look! Israel’s protector9

does not sleep or slumber!

5 The Lord is your protector;

the Lord is the shade at your right hand.

6 The sun will not harm you by day,

or the moon by night.10

7 The Lord will protect you from all harm;

he will protect your life.

8 The Lord will protect you in all you do,11

now and forevermore.
