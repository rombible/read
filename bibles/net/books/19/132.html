Psalm 1321

A song of ascents.2

1 O Lord, for David’s sake remember

all his strenuous effort,3

2 and how he made a vow to the Lord,

and swore an oath to the powerful ruler of Jacob.4

3 He said,5 “I will not enter my own home,6

or get into my bed.7

4 I will not allow my eyes to sleep,

or my eyelids to slumber,

5 until I find a place for the Lord,

a fine dwelling place8 for the powerful ruler of Jacob.”9

6 Look, we heard about it10 in Ephrathah,11

we found it in the territory of Jaar.12

7 Let us go to his dwelling place!

Let us worship13 before his footstool!

8 Ascend, O Lord, to your resting place,

you and the ark of your strength!

9 May your priests be clothed with integrity!14

May your loyal followers shout for joy!

10 For the sake of David, your servant,

do not reject your chosen king!15

11 The Lord made a reliable promise to David;16

he will not go back on his word.17

He said,18 “I will place one of your descendants19 on your throne.

12 If your sons keep my covenant

and the rules I teach them,

their sons will also sit on your throne forever.”

13 Certainly20 the Lord has chosen Zion;

he decided to make it his home.21

14 He said,22 “This will be my resting place forever;

I will live here, for I have chosen it.23

15 I will abundantly supply what she needs;24

I will give her poor all the food they need.25

16 I will protect her priests,26

and her godly people will shout exuberantly.27

17 There I will make David strong;28

I have determined that my chosen king’s dynasty will continue.29

18 I will humiliate his enemies,30

and his crown will shine.
