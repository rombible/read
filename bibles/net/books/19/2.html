Psalm 21

1 Why2 do the nations rebel?3

Why4 are the countries5 devising6 plots that will fail?7

2 The kings of the earth8 form a united front;9

the rulers collaborate10

against the Lord and his anointed king.11

3 They say,12 “Let’s tear off the shackles they’ve put on us!13

Let’s free ourselves from14 their ropes!”

4 The one enthroned15 in heaven laughs in disgust;16

the Lord taunts17 them.

5 Then he angrily speaks to them

and terrifies them in his rage,18 saying,19

6 “I myself20 have installed21 my king

on Zion, my holy hill.”

7 The king says,22 “I will announce the Lord’s decree. He said to me:23

‘You are my son!24 This very day I have become your father!

8 Ask me,

and I will give you the nations as your inheritance,25

the ends of the earth as your personal property.

9 You will break them26 with an iron scepter;27

you will smash them like a potter’s jar!’”28

10 So now, you kings, do what is wise;29

you rulers of the earth, submit to correction!30

11 Serve31 the Lord in fear!

Repent in terror!32

12 Give sincere homage!33

Otherwise he34 will be angry,35

and you will die because of your behavior,36

when his anger quickly ignites.37

How blessed38 are all who take shelter in him!39
