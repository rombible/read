Psalm 151

A psalm of David.

1 Lord, who may be a guest in your home?2

Who may live on your holy hill?3

2 Whoever lives a blameless life,4

does what is right,

and speaks honestly.5

3 He6 does not slander,7

or do harm to others,8

or insult his neighbor.9

4 He despises a reprobate,10

but honors the Lord’s loyal followers.11

He makes firm commitments and does not renege on his promise.12

5 He does not charge interest when he lends his money.13

He does not take bribes to testify against the innocent.14

The one who lives like this15 will never be upended.
