Psalm 1401

For the music director; a psalm of David.

1 O Lord, rescue me from wicked men!2

Protect me from violent men,3

2 who plan ways to harm me.4

All day long they stir up conflict.5

3 Their tongues wound like a serpent;6

a viper’s7 venom is behind8 their lips. (Selah)

4 O Lord, shelter me from the power9 of the wicked!

Protect me from violent men,

who plan to knock me over.10

5 Proud men hide a snare for me;

evil men11 spread a net by the path;

they set traps for me. (Selah)

6 I say to the Lord, “You are my God.”

O Lord, pay attention to my plea for mercy!

7 O sovereign Lord, my strong deliverer,12

you shield13 my head in the day of battle.

8 O Lord, do not let the wicked have their way!14

Do not allow their15 plan to succeed when they attack!16 (Selah)

9 As for the heads of those who surround me –

may the harm done by17 their lips overwhelm them!

10 May he rain down18 fiery coals upon them!

May he throw them into the fire!

From bottomless pits they will not escape.19

11 A slanderer20 will not endure on21 the earth;

calamity will hunt down a violent man and strike him down.22

12 I know23 that the Lord defends the cause of the oppressed

and vindicates the poor.24

13 Certainly the godly will give thanks to your name;

the morally upright will live in your presence.
