Psalm 331

1 You godly ones, shout for joy because of the Lord!

It is appropriate for the morally upright to offer him praise.

2 Give thanks to the Lord with the harp!

Sing to him to the accompaniment of a ten-stringed instrument!

3 Sing to him a new song!2

Play skillfully as you shout out your praises to him!3

4 For4 the Lord’s decrees5 are just,6

and everything he does is fair.7

5 The Lord promotes8 equity and justice;

the Lord’s faithfulness extends throughout the earth.9

6 By the Lord’s decree10 the heavens were made;

by a mere word from his mouth all the stars in the sky were created.11

7 He piles up the water of the sea;12

he puts the oceans13 in storehouses.

8 Let the whole earth fear14 the Lord!

Let all who live in the world stand in awe of him!

9 For he spoke, and it15 came into existence,

he issued the decree,16 and it stood firm.

10 The Lord frustrates17 the decisions of the nations;

he nullifies the plans18 of the peoples.

11 The Lord’s decisions stand forever;

his plans abide throughout the ages.19

12 How blessed20 is the nation whose God is the Lord,

the people whom he has chosen to be his special possession.21

13 The Lord watches22 from heaven;

he sees all people.23

14 From the place where he lives he looks carefully

at all the earth’s inhabitants.

15 He is the one who forms every human heart,24

and takes note of all their actions.

16 No king is delivered by his vast army;

a warrior is not saved by his great might.

17 A horse disappoints those who trust in it for victory;25

despite its great strength, it cannot deliver.

18 Look, the Lord takes notice of his loyal followers,26

those who wait for him to demonstrate his faithfulness27

19 by saving their lives from death28

and sustaining them during times of famine.29

20 We30 wait for the Lord;

he is our deliverer31 and shield.32

21 For our hearts rejoice in him,

for we trust in his holy name.

22 May we experience your faithfulness, O Lord,33

for34 we wait for you.
