1 “Call now!1 Is there anyone who will answer you?2

To which of the holy ones3 will you turn?4

2 For5 wrath kills the foolish person,6

and anger7 slays the silly one.

3 I myself8 have seen the fool9 taking root,

but suddenly I cursed his place of residence.10

4 His children are far11 from safety,

and they are crushed12 at the place where judgment is rendered,13

nor is there anyone to deliver them.14

5 The hungry15 eat up his harvest,16

and take it even from behind the thorns,17

and the thirsty18 swallow up19 their fortune.20

6 For evil does not come up from the dust,21

nor does trouble spring up from the ground,

7 but people22 are born23 to trouble,

as surely as the sparks24 fly25 upward.26

Blessings for the One Who Seeks God27

8 “But28 as for me,29 I would seek30 God,31

and to God32 I would set forth my case.33

9 He does34 great and unsearchable35 things,

marvelous things without36 number;37

10 he gives38 rain on the earth,39

and sends40 water on the fields;41

11 he sets42 the lowly43 on high,

that those who mourn44 are raised45 to safety.

12 He frustrates46 the plans47 of the crafty48

so that49 their hands cannot accomplish

what they had planned!50

13 He catches51 the wise in their own craftiness,52

and the counsel of the cunning53 is brought to a quick end.54

14 They meet with darkness in the daytime,55

and grope about56 in the noontime as if it were night.57

15 So he saves58 from the sword that comes from their mouth,59

even60 the poor from the hand of the powerful.

16 Thus the poor have hope,

and iniquity61 shuts its mouth.62

17 “Therefore,63 blessed64 is the man whom God corrects,65

so do not despise the discipline66 of the Almighty.67

18 For68 he69 wounds,70 but he also bandages;

he strikes, but his hands also heal.

19 He will deliver you71 from six calamities;

yes, in seven72 no evil will touch you.

20 In time of famine73 he will redeem you from death,

and in time of war from the power of the sword.74

21 You will be protected75 from malicious gossip,76

and will not be afraid of the destruction77 when it comes.

22 You will laugh at destruction and famine78

and need not79 be afraid of the beasts of the earth.

23 For you will have a pact with the stones80 of the field,

and the wild animals81 will be at peace82 with you.

24 And83 you will know84 that your home85

will be secure,86

and when you inspect87 your domains,

you will not be missing88 anything.

25 You will also know that your children89 will be numerous,

and your descendants90 like the grass of the earth.

26 You will come to your grave in a full age,91

As stacks of grain are harvested in their season.

27 Look, we have investigated this, so it is true.
