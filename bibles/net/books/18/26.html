Job’s Reply to Bildad1

1 Then Job replied:

2 “How you have helped2 the powerless!3

How you have saved the person who has no strength!4

3 How you have advised the one without wisdom,

and abundantly5 revealed your insight!

4 To whom6 did you utter these words?

And whose spirit has come forth from your mouth?7

A Better Description of God’s Greatness8

5 “The dead9 tremble10 –

those beneath the waters

and all that live in them.11

6 The underworld12 is naked before God;13

the place of destruction lies uncovered.14

7 He spreads out the northern skies15 over empty space;16

he suspends the earth on nothing.17

8 He locks the waters in his clouds,

and the clouds do not burst with the weight of them.

9 He conceals18 the face of the full moon,19

shrouding it with his clouds.

10 He marks out the horizon20 on the surface of the waters

as a boundary between light and darkness.

11 The pillars21 of the heavens tremble

and are amazed at his rebuke.22

12 By his power he stills23 the sea;

by his wisdom he cut Rahab the great sea monster24 to pieces.25

13 By his breath26 the skies became fair;

his hand pierced the fleeing serpent.27

14 Indeed, these are but the outer fringes of his ways!28

How faint is the whisper29 we hear of him!

But who can understand the thunder of his power?”
