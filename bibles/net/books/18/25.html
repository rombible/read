Bildad’s Third Speech1

1 Then Bildad the Shuhite answered:

2 “Dominion2 and awesome might3 belong to4 God;

he establishes peace in his heights.5

3 Can his armies be numbered?6

On whom does his light7 not rise?

4 How then can a human being be righteous before God?

How can one born of a woman be pure?8

5 If even the moon is not bright,

and the stars are not pure as far as he is concerned,9

6 how much less a mortal man, who is but a maggot10 –

a son of man, who is only a worm!”
