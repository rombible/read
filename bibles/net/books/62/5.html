Testimony About the Son

This21 is the conquering power22 that has conquered23 the world: our faith. 5 Now who is the person who has conquered the world except the one who believes that24 Jesus is the Son of God? 6 Jesus Christ is the one who came by water and blood – not by the water only, but by the water and the blood. And the Spirit is the one who testifies, because25 the Spirit is the truth. 7 For26 there are three that testify,27 8 the Spirit and the water and the blood, and these three are in agreement.

9 If we accept the testimony of men, the testimony of God is greater, because28 this29 is the testimony of God that30 he has testified concerning his Son. 10 (The one who believes in the Son of God has the testimony in himself; the one who does not believe God has made him a liar, because he has not believed in the testimony that God has testified concerning his Son.)31 11 And this is the testimony: God32 has given us eternal life,33 and this life is in his Son. 12 The one who has the Son34 has this35 eternal36 life; the one who does not have the Son of God does not have this37 eternal38 life.

Assurance of Eternal Life

13 I have written these things39 to you who believe40 in the name of the Son of God so that41 you may know that you have eternal life.

14 And this is the confidence that we have before him: that42 whenever43 we ask anything according to his will, he hears us. 15 And if we know44 that he hears us in regard to whatever we ask, then we know that we have the requests that we have asked from him. 16 If45 anyone sees his fellow Christian46 committing a sin not resulting in death,47 he should ask, and God48 will grant49 life to the person who commits a sin not resulting in death.50 There is a sin resulting in death.51 I do not say that he should ask about that. 17 All unrighteousness52 is sin, but there is sin not resulting in death.53
