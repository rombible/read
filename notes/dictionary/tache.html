Tache.

The word thus rendered occurs only in the description of the structure of the tabernacle and its fittings, ( Exodus 26:6 Exodus 26:11 Exodus 26:33 ; 35:11 ; 36:13 ; 39:33 ) and appears to indicate the small hooks by which a curtain is suspended to the rings from which it hangs, or connected vertically, as in the case of the veil of the holy of holies, with the loops of another curtain.
