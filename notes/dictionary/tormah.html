TORMAH

tor'-ma (tormah, "fraud"; Codex Vaticanus en kruphe, "in secret," Codex Alexandrinus meta doron, "with gifts"):

This name is given in EVm as an alternative to "privily" , or "craftily" the Revised Version (British and American) (Judges 9:31). There is no knowledge of such a place. The text is corrupt.
