IBHAR

ib'-har (yibhchar, "He (God) chooses"; in Codex Sinaiticus, Codex Vaticanus, Ebear, in Chronicles, Codex Vaticanus, Baar; Codex Alexandrinus, Iebaar):

One of David's sons, born at Jerusalem; son of a wife and not of a concubine (1 Chronicles 3:6; 2 Samuel 5:15); otherwise unknown. His name in all three lists follows Solomon's.' In the Peshitta, "Juchabar."
