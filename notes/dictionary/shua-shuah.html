SHUA; SHUAH

shoo'-a:

(1) (shua` "prosperity"):

A Canaanite whose daughter Judah took to wife (Genesis 38:2,12; 1 Chronicles 2:3).

See BATHSHUA.

(2) (shu`-a'', "prosperity"):

Daughter of Heber, an Asherite (1 Chronicles 7:32).

(3) (shuach, "depression"):

A son of Keturah by Abraham (Genesis 25:2; 1 Chronicles 1:32), and his posterity.

See BILDAD.

(4) A brother of Caleb (1 Chronicles 4:11).

See SHUHAH.
