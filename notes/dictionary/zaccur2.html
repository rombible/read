Zaccur [N] [E] [H]

(mindful ).

  1. Father of Shammua, the Reubenite spy. ( Numbers 13:4 ) (B.C. 1451.)
  2. A Merarite Levite, son of Jaaziah. ( 1 Chronicles 24:27 )
  3. Son of Asaph the singer. ( 1 Chronicles 25:2 1 Chronicles 25:10 ; Nehemiah 12:35 )
  4. The son of Imri who assisted Nehemiah in rebuilding the city wall. ( Nehemiah 3:2 ) (B.C. 446.)
  5. A Levite, or family of Levites, who signed the covenant with Nehemiah. ( Nehemiah 10:18 ) (B.C. 410.)
  6. A Levite whose son or descendant Hanan was one of the treasurers over the treasuries appointed by Nehemiah. ( Nehemiah 13:13 )
