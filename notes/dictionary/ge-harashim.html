GE-HARASHIM

ge-ha-ra'-shim (ge'charashim):

In 1 Chronicles 4:14, the King James Version renders "valley of Charashim." In Nehemiah 11:35, English Versions of the Bible renders "valley of craftsmen"; here it is named with Lod and Ono. Something of the name perhaps survives in Khirbet Hirsa, East of Lydda.
