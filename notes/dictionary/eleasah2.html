Eleasah [N]

(whom God made ).

  1. Son of Helez, one of the descendants of Judah, of the family of Hezron. ( 1 Chronicles 2:39 ) (B.C. after 1046.)
  2. Son of Rapha or Rephaiah; a descendant of Saul through Jonathan and Merib-baal or Mephibosheth. ( 1 Chronicles 8;37 1 Chronicles 37 ; 9:43 ) (B.C. before 588.)
