ZETHAM

ze'-tham (zetham, meaning unknown):

A Gershonite Levite (1 Chronicles 23:8; 26:22). In the second passage Curtis holds that "the sons of Jehieli" is a gloss; he points the Massoretic Text to read "brethren" instead of "brother," and so has "Jehiel (1 Chronicles 26:22) and his brethren, Zetham and Joel, were over the treasures."
