Ammihud [N] [E] [H]

(people of praise ).

  1. An Ephraimite father of Elishama, the chief of the tribe at the time of the Exodus. ( Numbers 1:10 ; 2:18 ; Numbers 7:48 Numbers 7:53 ; 10:22 ; 1 Chronicles 1:1 7:26 ) and, through him, ancestor of Joshua. (B.C. 1491.)
  2. A Simeonite, father of Shemuel. ( Numbers 34:20 )
  3. The father of Pedahel, prince of the tribe of Naphtali. ( Numbers 34:28 )
  4. The father-of Talmai king of Geshur. ( 2 Samuel 13:37 )
  5. A descendant of Pharez, son of Judah. ( 1 Chronicles 9:4 )
