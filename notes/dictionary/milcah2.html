Milcah [N] [H]

(queen or counsel ).

  1. Daughter of Haran and wife of her uncle Nahor, Abrahams brother, to whom she bore eight children. ( Genesis 11:29 ; Genesis 22:20 Genesis 22:23 ; Genesis 24:15 Genesis 24:24 Genesis 24:47 )
  2. The fourth daughter of Zelophehad. ( Numbers 26:33 ; 27:1 ; 36:11 ; Joshua 17:3 )
