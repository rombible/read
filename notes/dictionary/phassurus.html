PHASSURUS

fas-su'-rus, fas'-u-rus (Phassouros; Codex Vaticanus Phassoros King James Version Phassaron, after Aldine):

The name of one of the families which went up from exile with Zerubbabel (1 Esdras 5:25)="Pashhur" of Ezra 2:38; Nehemiah 7:41; according to Ezra and Nehemiah and Revised Version numbering, 1,247; according to King James Version following A, 1,047.
