Uzza, The garden of,

the spot in which Manasseh king of Judah and his son Amon were buried. ( 2 Kings 21:18 2 Kings 21:26 ) It was the garden attached to Manassehs palace. ver. 18. The fact of its mention shows that it was not where the usual sepulchres of the kings were. No clue, however, is afforded to its position.
