Mamre [N] [E] [H]

(strength, fatness ) an ancient Amorite, who with his brothers, Eshcol and Aner, was in alliance with Abram, ( Genesis 14:13 Genesis 14:51 ) and under the shade of whose oak grove the patriarch dwelt in the interval between his residence at Bethel and at Beersheba. ch. ( Genesis 13:18 ; 18:1 ) In the subsequent chapters Mamre is a mere local appellation. ch, ( Genesis 23:17 Genesis 23:19 ; 25:9 ; 49:30 ; 50:13 )
