Judea [E]

  • Also called JUDAH and JUDAEA

  • (The southern division of Palestine)
    • It extended from the Jordan River and the Dead Sea to the Mediterranean Sea, and from Shiloh on the north to the wilderness on the south
      Matthew 4:25 ; Luke 5:17 ; John 4:47 John 4:54
    • The term applies to all of Palestine in
      Luke 1:5
    • It applies to the territory east of the Jordan River in
      Matthew 19:1 ; Mark 10:1 ; Luke 23:5

  • Wilderness of
    • Called Beth-arabah
      Joshua 15:6 Joshua 15:61
    • Assigned to Benjamin
      Joshua 18:22
    • John the Baptist preaches in
      Matthew 3:1 ; Luke 3:3
