ISH-SECHEL

ish'-se-kel ('ish sekhel, "man of discretion"):

Ezra, at one time in need of ministers for the house of God, sent "unto Iddo the chief at the place Casiphia." "And according to the good hand of our God upon us they brought us a man of discretion (m "Ish-sechel"), of the sons of Mahli, the son of Levi, the son of Israel" (Ezra 8:18). This is the only reference to Ish-sechel.
