Shaul [N] [H]

(asked ).

  1. The son of Simeon by a Canaanitish woman, ( Genesis 48:10 ; Exodus 6:15 ; Numbers 26:13 ; 1 Chronicles 4:24 ) and founder of the family of the Shaulites. (B.C. 1712.)
  2. One of the kings of Edom. ( 1 Chronicles 1:48 1 Chronicles 1:49 ) In the Authorized Version of ( Genesis 36:37 ) he is less accurately called SAUL.
