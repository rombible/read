CONCLUDE

kon-klood' (sumbibazo):

Used only in Acts 16:10, where the King James Version has "assuredly gathering," i.e. "inferring." Where the King James Version has "conclude," the Revised Version (British and American) more accurately renders "reckon" (Romans 3:28); "giving judgment" (Acts 21:25); "shut up" (Romans 11:32; Galatians 3:22).
