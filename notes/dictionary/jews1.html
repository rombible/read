Jews

  • A corrupted form, doubtless, of Judah, and applied to the people of the kingdom of Judah and Benjamin
    2 Kings 16:6 ; 25:25 ; 2 Chronicles 32:18

  • (After the dissolution of the kingdom of Israel the name was applied to all Israelites, as well as to those of the two remaining tribes)
