Lehi [N] [E] [H]

(jaw bone ), a place in Judah, probably on the confines of the Philistines country, between it and the cliff Etam; the scene of Samsons well-known exploit with the jaw bone. ( Judges 15:9 Judges 15:14 Judges 15:19 ) It may perhaps be identified with Beit-Likiyeh , a village about two miles below the upper Beth-horon.
