Remmon-metho-ar

a place which formed one of the landmarks of Zebulun. ( Joshua 19:13 ) only. Methoar does not really form a part of the name, but should be translated (as in the margin of the Authorized Version) "Remmon which reaches to Neah." Dr. Robinson and Mr. Van Deuteronomy Velde place Rummaneh on the south border of the plain of Buttauf , three miles north-northeast of Seffurieh .
