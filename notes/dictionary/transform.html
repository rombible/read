TRANSFORM

trans-form' (Romans 12:2; the Revised Version (British and American) 2 Corinthians 3:18 for metamorphoomai, and the King James Version 2 Corinthians 11:13,14,15 for metaschematizo, the Revised Version (British and American) "fashion"):

The commentaries often explain the former word as connoting a change of nature, while the latter refers only to the appearance, but this distinction is probably fanciful.
