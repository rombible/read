TAREA

ta'-re-a, ta-re'-a (ta'area`, a copyist's mistake (1 Chronicles 8:35) for tacharea, "the shrewd one," in 1 Chronicles 9:41; Codex Vaticanus Theree; Codex Alexandrinus Tharee; Lucian, Tharaa; in 1 Chronicles 9:41, Codex Vaticanus Tharach; Codex Alexandrinus Thara; Lucian, Tharaa; see TAHREA):

A descendant of Saul mentioned in a genealogy of Benjamin (1 Chronicles 9:41).
