Ahio [N] [E] [H]

(brotherly ).

  1. Son of Abinadab, who accompanied the ark when it was brought out of his fathers house. ( 2 Samuel 6:3 2 Samuel 6:4 ; 1 Chronicles 13:7 ) (B.C.1043.)
  2. A Benjamite, one of the sons of Beriah. ( 1 Chronicles 8:14 )
  3. A Benjamite, Son of Jehiel. ( 1 Chronicles 8:31 ; 9:37 )
