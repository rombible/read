Shuah [N] [E] [H]

(wealth ).

  1. Son of Abraham by Keturah. ( Genesis 25:2 ; 1 Chronicles 1:32 ).) (B.C. before 1820.)
  2. Properly Shuchah brother of Chelub. ( 1 Chronicles 4:11 )
  3. The father of Judahs wife, ( Genesis 38:2 Genesis 38:12 ) called also Shua in the Authorized Version. (B.C. before 1725.)
