MINIAMIN

min'-ya-min, mi-ni'-a-min (minydmin):

(1) A Levite who assisted Kore, the son of Imnah, in the distribution of the freewill offerings (2 Chronicles 31:15).

(2) A priestly family of the time of the high priest Joiakim (Nehemiah 12:17), probably equals MIJAMIN (2).

(3) A priestly participant in the ceremony of the dedication of the wall (Nehemiah 12:41).
