SALT, CITY OF

(`ir ha-melach; Codex Alexandrinus hai pol(e)is halon):

One of the six cities in the wilderness of Judah mentioned between Nibshan and Engedi (Joshua 15:62). The site is very uncertain. The large and important Tell el-Milch (i.e. "the salt hill"), on the route from Hebron to Akaba, is possible.
