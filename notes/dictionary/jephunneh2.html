Jephunneh [N] [E] [H]

(for whom a way is prepared ).

  1. Father of Caleb the spy, appears to have belonged to an Edomitish tribe called Kenezites, from Kenaz their founder. See ( Numbers 13:6 ) etc.; Numb 32:12 etc.; Josh 14:14 etc.; 1Chr 4:15 (B.C. 1530.)
  2. A descendant of Asher, eldest of the three sons of Jether. ( 1 Chronicles 7:38 ) (B.C. 1017.)
