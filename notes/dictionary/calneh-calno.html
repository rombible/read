CALNEH; CALNO

kal'-ne, kal'-no (kalneh (Amos 6:2), kalno (Isaiah 10:9)):

"Probably the Kulnia (Kullani) associated with Arpad and Hadrach, Syrian cities, in the Assyrian `tribute' list (Western Asiatic Inscriptions, II, 53, number 3); Kullanhu about six miles from Arpad" (HDB, I, 344, and 1-vol HDB, 109).
