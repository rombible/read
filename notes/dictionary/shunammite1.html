Shunammite [E]

  • A person from the city of Shunem
    • Abishag, the girl who took care of David
      1 Kings 1:3
    • Desired by Adonijah as wife
      1 Kings 2:13-25

  • A woman who gave hospitality to Elisha, and whose son he raised to life
    2 Kings 4:8-37
