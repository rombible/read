MOSEROTH

mo-se'-roth, mo'-se-roth, -roth (moceroth, "bonds"):

A desert camp of the Israelites between Hashmonah and Bene-jaakan (Numbers 33:30,31). It is probably the same as Moserah (Deuteronomy 10:6), though in that passage the name follows Bene-jaakan. There Aaron died and was buried.

See WANDERINGS OF ISRAEL.
