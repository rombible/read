SHAHAZUMAH

sha-ha-zoo'-ma, sha-haz'-oo-ma (shachatsumah; Codex Vaticanus Saleim kata thalassan; Codex Alexandrinus Saseimath; the King James Version Shahazimah, sha-haz'i-mah):

A town in the territory of Issachar on the boundary which ran from Tabor to the Jordan (Joshua 19:22). The site, which has not yet been recovered, must be sought, probably, to the Southeast of the mountain.
