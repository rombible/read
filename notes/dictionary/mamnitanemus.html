MAMNITANEMUS

mam-ni-ta-ne'-mus (Mamnitanaimos; Codex Vaticanus Mamtitanaimos; the King James Version, Mamnitanaimus):

1 Esdras 9:34, where it represents the two names Mattaniah and Mattenai in the parallel Ezra 10:37, which probably represent only one person. It must be a corruption of these names. The Aldine gives a still more corrupt form, Mamnimatanaimos.
