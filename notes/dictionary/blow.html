BLOW

blo (nashaph):

Used with reference to the wind (Exodus 15:10; Psalms 78:26; 147:18; pneo, "to breathe," Luke 12:55; John 3:8; Revelation 7:1); taqa`, with reference to trumpet sound (Numbers 10:3-10; 31:6 the King James Version; Judges 7:18,20; 1 Kings 1:34; 1 Chronicles 15:24; Psalms 81:3; Ezekiel 33:3,6; Hosea 5:8; Joel 2:1,15); puach, with reference to the strong expulsion of the breath (Ezekiel 21:31; 22:20,21; Haggai 1:9; Isaiah 40:7; 54:16; Job 20:26); nashaph, with reference to a forcible slap or stroke with hand or an instrument (Psalms 39:10; Isaiah 40:24; Jeremiah 14:17 the King James Version).
