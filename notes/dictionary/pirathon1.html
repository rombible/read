Pirathon [N] [E] [H]

(princely ), "in the land of Ephraim in the mount of the Amalekite," a place in ( Judges 12:15 ) Its site, now called Ferata , is about one mile and a half south of the road from Jaffa, by Hableh , to Nablus . Pirathonites are mentioned in ( Judges 12:13 Judges 12:15 ) and 1Chr 27:14
