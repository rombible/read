Carving. [N]

The arts of carving and engraving were much in request in the construction of both the tabernacle and the temple. ( Exodus 31:5 ; 35:33 ; 1 Kings 6:18 1 Kings 6:35 ; Psalms 74:6 ) as well as in the ornamentation of the priestly dresses. ( Exodus 28:9-36 ; 2 Chronicles 2:7 2 Chronicles 2:14 ; Zechariah 3:9 )
