UKNAZ

uk'-naz (uqenaz, "and Kenaz," probably):

Found in the King James Version margin of 1 Chronicles 4:15 for the King James Version "even Kenaz," the Revised Version (British and American) "and Kenaz," whereas the Septuagint omits "and." It is probable that some name has dropped out after Elah. Curtis suggests reading "and these are the sons of Kenaz," i.e. those mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:13 f.

See KENAZ.
