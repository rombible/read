OUTRAGE; OUTRAGEOUS

out'-raj, out-ra'-jus:

The noun (from the French outre plus age, "that which goes beyond") only in the heading to Psalms 10 the King James Version; the adjective in Proverbs 27:4, the King James Version and the English Revised Version, for sheTeph, "flood." "Anger is overwhelming" (American Standard Revised Version), is much better.
