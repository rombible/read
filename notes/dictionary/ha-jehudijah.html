HA-JEHUDIJAH

ha-je-hu-di'-ja (ha-yehudhiyah):

Named in the genealogical list (1 Chronicles 4:18). Possibly a proper name (Revised Version, margin), but probably "the Jewess" (Revised Version (British and American)). May be so given in order to distinguish from the Egyptian named in this verse. The King James Version translates "Jehudijah."
