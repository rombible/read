Shechaniah [S]

  • A descendant of David
    1 Chronicles 3:21 1 Chronicles 3:22

  • Two men whose descendants returned with Ezra from the captivity in Babylon
    Ezra 8:3 Ezra 8:5

  • A Jew who proposed the putting away (divorce) of Gentile wives to Ezra
    Ezra 10:2

  • Father of Shemaiah
    Nehemiah 3:29
    • Possibly identical with number one

    • Father-in-law of Tobiah
      Nehemiah 6:18

  • A Levite who returned with Zerubbabel from the captivity in Babylon
    Nehemiah 12:3
