ALNATHAN

al'-na-than (Alnathan, "God has given," the Revised Version (British and American) ELNATHAN):

Apocryphal name of a person (1 Esdras 8:44) corresponding to Elnathan of Ezra 8:16. He was one of the learned men summoned by Ezra, as he was beginning his journey to Jerusalem, and sent to Iddo to ask for ministers for the house of Yahweh.
