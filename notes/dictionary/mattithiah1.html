Mattithiah [E]

  • 1, A Levite who had charge of the baked offerings
    1 Chronicles 9:31

  • A Levite musician
    1 Chronicles 15:18 1 Chronicles 15:21 ; 16:5

  • A chief of the fourteenth division (shift) of temple musicians
    1 Chronicles 25:3 1 Chronicles 25:21

  • An Israelite who divorced his Gentile wife after the captivity
    Ezra 10:43

  • A prince who stood beside Ezra when he read the law aloud to the people
    Nehemiah 8:4
