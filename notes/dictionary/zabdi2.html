Zabdi [N] [E] [H]

(my gift ).

  1. Son of Zerah the son of Judah, and ancestor of Achan. ( Joshua 7:1 Joshua 7:17 Joshua 7:18 ) (B.C. before 1480.)
  2. A Benjamite, of the sons of Shimhi. ( 1 Chronicles 8:19 ) (B.C. about 1442.)
  3. Davids officer over the produce of the vineyards for the wine-cellars. ( 1 Chronicles 21:27 ) (B.C. 1043.)
  4. Son of Asaph the minstrel, ( Nehemiah 11:17 ) called ZACCUR in ( Nehemiah 12:35 ) and ZICHRI in ( 1 Chronicles 9:15 ) (B.C. before 446.)
