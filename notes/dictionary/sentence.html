SENTENCE

sen'-tens:

Eight Hebrew and three Greek words are thus translated in the King James Version. Sometimes it points to a mystery (Daniel 5:12; 8:23); then again to the contents of the Law (Deuteronomy 17:11); then again to the idea of judgment (Psalms 17:2) or of a judicial sentence (2 Corinthians 1:9; Luke 23:24), or of judicial advice (Acts 15:19, the American Standard Revised Version "judgment").
