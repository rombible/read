IZHARITES

iz'-har-its (yitshari):

The descendants of Izhar, son of Kohath, and grandson of Levi (Numbers 3:19,27). In David's reign some of these were "over the treasures of the house of Yahweh" (1 Chronicles 26:23), others "were for the outward business over Israel, for officers and judges" (ibid., 26:29).
