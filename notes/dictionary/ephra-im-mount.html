Ephra-im, Mount, [E]

is a district which seems to extend as far south as Ramah and Bethel, ( 1 Samuel 1:1 ; 7:17 ; 2 Chronicles 13:4 2 Chronicles 13:19 ) compared with 2Chr 15:8 places but a few miles north of Jerusalem, and within the limits of Benjamin.
