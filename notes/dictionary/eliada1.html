Eliada [N] [E] [H]

(known by God ).

  1. One of Davids sons; according to the lists, the youngest but one of the family born to him after his establishment in Jerusalem. ( 2 Samuel 5:16 ; 1 Chronicles 3:8 ) (B.C. after 1033.)
  2. A mighty man of war, a Benjamite, who led 200,000 of his tribe to the army of Jehoshaphat. ( 2 Chronicles 17:17 ) (B.C. 945.)
