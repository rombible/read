CONFORM; CONFORMABLE

kon-form', kon-form'-a-b'-l (summorphoo "to become or be like," or "of the same form"):

Indicating an inner change of nature, working into the outward life (Romans 8:29; Philippians 3:10,21); while suschematizo, "fashioned according to" (Romans 12:21 the Revised Version (British and American), the King James Version. "conformed"), refers to that which is external.
