BELMAIM

bel'-ma-im, the King James Version Belmen (Belmaim, Judith 7:3; Bailmain, 4:4):

A place in the neighborhood of Dothan (Judith 7:3), to which warning was sent to prepare for the invasion of Holofernes (Judith 4:4). It probably answers to the modern Bir Bil`ameh (Ibleam), a ruined site about half a mile South of Jenin.
