JECHOLIAH

jek-o-li'-a (yekholyahu; 2 Kings 15:2 the King James Version = yekholyah, Qere in 2 Chronicles 26:3, "Yah is able" or "Yah has been able"):

The mother of King Uzziah (Azariah) of Judah. The Revised Version (British and American) has "Jecoliah" in 2Ki and so the King James Version in 2 Ch.
