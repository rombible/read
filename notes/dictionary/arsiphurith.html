ARSIPHURITH

ar-si-fu'-rith (Arsiphourith; the King James Version Azephurith):

112 of the sons of Arsiphurith returned to Jerusalem with Zerubbabel (1 Esdras 5:16). The name is omitted in Ezra and Nehemiah, but the number corresponds to those mentioned with Jorah (Ezra 2:18) and Hariph (Nehemiah 7:24).
