Midianites [N]

  • Descended form Midian, son of Abraham by Keturah
    Genesis 25:1 Genesis 25:2 ; 1 Chronicles 1:32

  • Dwelt east of Jordan, beside Moab
    Numbers 22:1 Numbers 22:4

  • A SMALL PART OF
    • Dwelt near Horeb
      Exodus 2:15 ; 3:1
    • Retained the knowledge and worship of Jehovah
      Exodus 2:16 ; 18:9-12

  • Governed by kings
    Numbers 31:8 ; Judges 8:5

  • Dwelt in tents
    Habakkuk 3:7

  • Engaged in commerce
    Genesis 37:28 Genesis 37:36

  • Conquered by Hadad
    Genesis 36:25 ; 1 Chronicles 1:46

  • Excited by Moab against Israel
    Numbers 22:4

  • Terrified at approach of Israel
    Habakkuk 3:3-7

  • WITH THE MOABITES
    • Sent for Balaam to curse Israel
      Numbers 22:5-7
    • Seduced Israel to idolatry
      Numbers 25:1-6
    • Punished for seducing Israel
      Numbers 25:16-18 ; 31:1-12

  • Allowed to oppress Israel
    Judges 6:1-6

  • Gideon raised up against
    Judges 6:11-14

  • With Amalek, &c opposed Gideon
    Judges 6:33

  • Miraculously defeated and destroyed by Gideon
    Judges 7:16-22 ; Judges 8:10 Judges 8:11

  • Princes of, slain
    Judges 7:24 Judges 7:25 ; Judges 8:12 Judges 8:21

  • Completeness of their destruction, alluded to
    Psalms 83:9-11 ; Isaiah 9:4 ; 10:26

  • Shall minister to future glory of the church
    Isaiah 60:6
