SOUR

sour:

(1) bocer, "immature," "unripe":

"The fathers have eaten sour grapes" (Jeremiah 31:29; Ezekiel 18:2; compare Isaiah 18:5 the King James Version).

(2) cur, "to turn aside," "degenerate":

"Their drink is turned sour" (the King James Version margin "gone," the Revised Version margin "Their carouse is over").
