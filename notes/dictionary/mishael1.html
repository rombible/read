Mishael [E] [H] [S]

  • A son of Uzziel, helps carry the bodies of Nadab and Abihu out of the camp
    Exodus 6:22 ; Leviticus 10:4

  • A Jew who stood by Ezra when he read the law to the people
    Nehemiah 8:4

  • Also called MESHACH
    • One of three Hebrew young men trained with Daniel at the court of Babylon
      Daniel 1:6 Daniel 1:7 Daniel 1:11-20
    • Assists Daniel in interpreting Nebuchadnezzars dream
      Daniel 2:17-23
    • Thrown into the fiery furnace
      Daniel 3:13-30
