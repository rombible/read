Ellicott's Commentary for English Readers
(17) Which shall divide the land unto you.--Better, Which shall give (or, allot) the land as an inheritance unto you.

Pulpit Commentary
Verse 17. - Eleazar the priest, and Joshua the son of Nun. As the ecclesiastical and military heads respectively of the theocracy (see on Numbers 32:28).
Matthew Henry Commentary
34:16-29 God here appoints men to divide the land to them. So sure must they feel of victory and success while God fought for them, that the persons are named who should be intrusted with the dividing of the land.
